SECTION 1 — POLICY P6 (NEW IN v3)
================================================================

Visual-heavy questions are required to use SCREENSHOT_REQUIRED.
AI does not draw SVGs for question content.

Allowed exceptions (still INLINE_SVG):
  - simple box and arrow (e.g. 3 text boxes + 2 arrows)
  - single decorative icon (no data, no semantics)
  - simple geometric shapes used for layout

All other categories (food chains, food webs, apparatus, line graphs,
plotted points, animal silhouettes, models, force arrows, particle
diagrams, classification keys, planet/data tables) MUST be
SCREENSHOT_REQUIRED.

This policy is enforced at HTML build time and audited at Phase 6.
