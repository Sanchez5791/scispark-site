Chromatography 2: Identifying Substances
色谱法 2：鉴定物质
⏰ 热身 · Warm-up hook
A reference spot is like a fingerprint: same height, strong match.
参考斑点像指纹：高度一样，匹配度高。
  • Run two marker dots side by side on wet paper.
  • Same spots in the same places?
  • They are the same ink.
  • 在湿纸上并排点两支 marker.
  • 斑点位置一样?
  • 那就是同一种墨水.
  • They use chromatography to spot banned substances in athletes' samples at events like the Olympics.
  • 他们用色谱法在运动员样本里找出违禁物, 比如奥运会的兴奋剂检测.

SCREEN 2 · LEARN
Learn — Chromatography 2: Identifying Substances
机制 · 色谱法 2：鉴定物质
  • 要鉴定未知物, 把它跟已知的参照染料并排放.
  • 就像把指纹跟嫌疑人名单核对.
  • 如果未知斑点跟某个参照染料同高, 那种染料就在里面.
  • reference 参照物 你用来对比的已知样本.
🖼️ 配图待出 · Image pendingBest visual: labelled nucleus + shells, clearly marked NOT TO SCALE, nucleus marked 'tiny'. Animate?: No (static). Trap to flag: do not believe the drawn scale; never draw electrons inside the nucleus.
  • 一个斑点 单一物质.
  • 不止一个斑点 混合物.
  • 被带得更高的斑点, 是跟留在下面的不同的染料.
  • 走相同距离的斑点是相同物质.
  • (Y8: 我们看距离用 '远 vs 近', 不算 Rf 数值.)

❌ 常见错误 · Common mistake — don't fall for this!
  • Students think chromatography always fully separates everything, just as they think distillation gives 100% pure water.
  • 学生以为色谱总能把全部成分完全分开, 就像 以为蒸馏出 100% 纯水一样.
  • Separation depends on how each substance interacts with the solvent and the paper.
  • Like distillation, it is not automatically 100% — substances that behave the same can travel together.
  • 分得开不开, 看每种物质跟溶剂和滤纸怎么互动.
  • 跟蒸馏一样, 不是自动 100% — 表现一样的物质会一起走.
🖼️ 配图待出 · Image pendingBest visual: labelled nucleus + shells, clearly marked NOT TO SCALE, nucleus marked 'tiny'. Animate?: No (static). Trap to flag: do not believe the drawn scale; never draw electrons inside the nucleus.
✶ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, chromatography 'identifies' the substance.
  • In real labs it usually needs a second test to be sure.
  • 考试里色谱法能 '鉴定' 物质.
  • 真实实验室通常还要第二个测试才 能确定.
  • WARNING: Use water-based ink and food-safe dyes only.
  • Do not inhale solvent fumes.
  • 警告: 只用水性墨水和食用级染料.
  • 不要吸入溶剂气味.
🧠 口诀金句 · Golden phrase
Same paper, same solvent, same height same substance.
同纸同溶剂同高度＝同物质。
