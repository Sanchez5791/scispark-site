SCREEN 2 · LEARN
Learn — What Is Concentration?
机制 · 什么是浓度?
Concentration how many solute particles in a fixed volume浓度 固定体积里有多少溶质粒子
  • Concentration tells you how crowded the dissolved particles (the solute) are in a fixed amount of liquid (the solvent).
  • More particles in the same volume more concentrated.
  • Fewer particles in the same volume more dilute.
  • 浓度是说: 在固定体积的液体里, 溶解的粒子有多挤。
  • 同样体积里粒子越多 越浓 (concentrated)。
  • 同样体积里粒子越少 越淡 (dilute)。

Two ways to make a solution more concentrated让溶液变浓的两个办法
  • A solution gets more concentrated if you ADD more solute particles (same volume), OR if you REMOVE solvent so the same particles are squeezed into a smaller volume.
  • Stirring or cooling does NOT change how many particles are in the volume.
  • 溶液变浓有两条路: 加更多溶质粒子 (体积不变), 或者 拿走一些溶剂, 让同样的粒子挤进更小的体积。
  • 搅拌或降温不会改变体积里的粒子数。

❌ 常见错误 · Common mistake — don't fall for this!
  • Common mistake — don't fall for this!
  • 常见错误 — 别上当!
  • Students think concentration only means how strong something tastes (or how dark it looks).
  • 学生以为浓度就是味道浓不浓 (或颜色深不深)。
  • Many students think "concentrated" only means "tastes strong" or "looks dark".
  • [marked with X]
  • 很多同学以为 "浓" 只是 "味道重" 或 "颜色深"。
  • [X]
  • But a colourless solution can still be very concentrated — taste and colour are just ONE sign, not the definition.
  • 但是透明无色的溶液也可以很浓 — 味道和颜色只是一种表现, 不是定义。
  • Correct: concentration number of solute particles per unit volume.
  • It applies to ANY dissolved substance, even a clear, tasteless one.
  • [marked with check]
  • 正确: 浓度 单位体积里的溶质粒子数。
  • 任何溶解物都算, 连透明无味的也算。
  • [check]

✶ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, draw solute particles spread evenly in the liquid.
  • In real life there are tiny gaps between all particles, held by forces — particles never fully touch.
  • 考试时画: 溶质粒子在液体里均匀散开。
  • 真实情况: 所有粒子之间都有很小的缝隙, 靠吸引力拉着, 粒子从不真正贴在一起。
🧠 口诀金句 · Golden phrase
Same cup, more solute more concentrated.
同一个杯子，溶质越多，越浓。

总结 · 串联
What you learned你学到了什么
  • Concentration how many solute particles are packed into a fixed volume.
  • More particles per volume concentrated; fewer dilute.
  • Add solute or remove solvent to increase it.
  • Solubility (how much dissolves) usually increases with temperature.
  • Colour and taste are clues, not the rule.
  • 浓度 固定体积里挤了多少溶质粒子。
  • 每体积粒子多 浓; 少 淡。
  • 加溶质或减溶剂会让它变浓。
  • 溶解度 (能溶多少) 通常随 温度升高而增加。
  • 颜色和味道只是线索, 不是规则。
  • How to put a NUMBER on concentration (mol/dm3) is taught in Y9 and above.
  • Here we only compare crowded vs not.
  • 怎么用数字算浓度 (mol/dm3) 是 Y9 以上才教。
