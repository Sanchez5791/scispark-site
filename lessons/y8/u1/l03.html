SCREEN 2 · LEARN
Learn — Inside the Atom
机制 · 原子的内部结构
  • An atom has a nucleus at the centre.
  • The nucleus contains protons and neutrons.
  • Electrons are found in the space around the nucleus.
  • The nucleus is positive.
  • Electrons are negative.
  • 原子的中心有一个原子核 (nucleus)。
  • 核里有质子 (proton) 和 中子 (neutron)。
  • 电子 (electron) 在核外的空间里。
  • 核带正电， 电子带负电。

❌ 常见错误 · Common mistake — don't fall for this!
  • Common mistake — don't fall for this!
  • 常见错误 — 别上当!
  • Many students think an atom is solid like a marble, and that electrons travel on fixed circles like planets.
  • 很多同学以为原子是实心的小球，电子像行星一样按固定圆圈绕核转。
  • An atom is almost all empty space.
  • Electrons do not follow fixed paths.
  • They are spread out in a cloud.
  • 原子几乎全是空的。
  • 电子没有固定路径，它像云一样散在核周围。
  • An atom is mostly empty space.
  • Only the central nucleus is dense.
  • Electrons float in the electron cloud around it — not on fixed rings.
  • 原子大部分是空的。
  • 只有中心的核很密。
  • 电子在外面的电子云里 飘，不是在固定圆圈上。
  • Atoms are solid like marbles and electrons orbit on fixed circles.
  • 原子是实心小球，电子在固定圆轨道上绕核。

✶ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, draw the atom with a small nucleus in the middle.
  • In real life, atoms are almost completely empty — if an atom were a football field, the nucleus would be a grain of rice.
  • 考试时画：原子中间有一个核。
  • 真实情况：原子 99% 是空的。
  • 如果原子是足球场，原子核只是一粒米。
  • In the exam, draw electrons going around the nucleus in circles.
  • In real life, electrons don't follow fixed paths — they form a 'cloud' where we only know the chance of finding them.
  • 考试时画：电子绕原子核转圆圈。
  • 真实情况：电子没固定路径， 它像云一样散在原子周围。
🧠 口诀金句 · Golden phrase
N-C-E: Nucleus centre, Cloud outside, Empty mostly.
N-C-E：核在中间，云在外面，大多是空。

Wrap — connect & summarise
总结 · 串联
  • An atom is a central nucleus (protons + neutrons) with electrons in the cloud around it.
  • The atom is mostly empty space.
  • The nucleus is positive; electrons are negative; opposite charges hold them together.
  • 原子是一个中心核 (质子 + 中子)，电子在外面的电子云里。
  • 原子 大部分是空的。
  • 核带正电，电子带负电，异种电荷把它们拉在一起。
  • The "empty" atoms inside you are what we just mapped — that is the structure of all matter, including you.
  • 你体内那些「空」的原子，就是我们刚画出来的结构——所有物质 (包括你自己) 都是这样构成的。
