Is Your Water Pure?
你的水纯吗?
⏰ 热身 · Warm-up hook
A pure substance is picky: it melts at one fixed point.
纯物质很挑剔：它在固定温度融化。
Is your water pure?你的水纯吗?
  • Look at a glass of clear water.
  • It looks clean.
  • But "clean" and "pure" are not the same.
  • Pure water has nothing else mixed in.
  • The water from a tap usually has tiny amounts of other things dissolved in it.
  • In science, we can test how pure something is.
  • 看一杯清水, 看起来很干净。
  • 但 "干净" 和 "纯" 不一样。
  • 纯水里 没有别的东西。
  • 自来水里通常溶了少量别的东西。
  • 在科学里, 我们可以测一样 东西有多纯。
  • Look at any bottle of water near you.
  • Does the label say "pure" or does it list minerals added?
  • 看看你旁边任何一瓶水。
  • 标签写 "pure", 还是列了加进 去的矿物质?
  • Remember from Y7 Unit 1: matter can be an element (one kind of atom), a compound (atoms joined in a fixed way), or a mixture (more than one substance, not joined).
  • A pure substance is one element or one compound.
  • An impure substance is a mixture.
  • 还记得 Y7 Unit 1: 物质可以是 element (元素, 一种原子)、 compound (化合物, 原子按固定方式结合)、或 mixture (混合物, 不止一种 物质, 没结合)。
  • 纯物质就是一种 element 或一种 compound。
  • 不纯的物质就是 mixture。

SCREEN 2 · LEARN
Learn — Is Your Water Pure?
机制 · 你的水纯吗?
A pure substance has a sharp melting and boiling point纯物质有固定的熔点和沸点
  • A pure substance is made of only one type of particle.
  • Because of this, it melts at one exact temperature and boils at one exact temperature — a sharp, single point.
  • An impure substance (a mixture) melts and boils over a range of temperatures, not a single point.
  • So the melting point and boiling point can be used as a purity test.
  • 纯物质只由一种粒子组成。
  • 正因为这样, 它在一个固定温度熔化, 在 一个固定温度沸腾 — 一个固定的单一点。
  • 不纯的物质 (混合物) 在一段温度 范围里熔化和沸腾, 不是单一点。
  • 所以熔点和沸点可以拿来当纯度测试。

Testing purity fairly公平地测纯度
  • When you measure a melting or boiling point to test purity, you must keep the test fair and reliable: heat slowly and evenly so the reading is accurate, use a precise thermometer, and repeat the measurement.
  • A range you see should come from impurity, not from a sloppy measurement.
  • 当你测熔点或沸点来检查纯度时, 要让测试公平、可靠: 慢慢 均匀加热, 读数才准; 用精确的温度计; 重复测量。
  • 你看到的温度范围应该 来自杂质, 而不是来自马虎的测量。
Common mistake — don't fall for this!常见错误 — 别上当!
  • Many students think (M6) tap water is a pure substance, and (M7) that a melting/boiling range just means a bad measurement.
  • 很多同学以为 (M6) 自来水是纯物质, 还有 (M7) 熔点/沸点的 范围只是测量不准。
  • [IMAGE: pure water box (only one particle type) vs tap water box (water + minerals + chlorine).] Tap water has minerals and chlorine dissolved in it, so it is a mixture, not pure.
  • And a melting/boiling range is caused by impurity, not by a bad reading.
  • [图: 纯水盒子 (只有一种粒子) 对比 自来水盒子 (水 + 矿物质 + 氯)。
  • ] 自来水溶了矿物质和氯, 所以是混合物, 不是纯的。
  • 熔点/沸点的 范围是杂质造成的, 不是读数不准。
  • ✓ Correct: only distilled water is close to pure; tap water is a mixture.
  • A range impurity present, not measurement error.
  • ✓ 正确: 只有蒸馏水接近纯; 自来水是混合物。
  • 范围 有杂质, 不是测量误差。
  • Tap water is pure (M6); melting range means bad measurement, not impurity (M7).
  • 自来水是纯的 (M6); 熔点范围是测量不准, 不是 杂质 (M7)。

✶ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
✶ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, sort matter into element / compound / mixture.
  • In real life, alloys like steel and brass sit in between — they look like compounds but count as mixtures.
  • 考试 时 分: 元素 / 化合物 / 混合物 三类。
  • 真实: alloy (合金) 像 钢 / 黄铜 算 混合物, 但 性质 像 化合物。

纯＝一个点；不纯＝一段范围。

Pure or not?纯还是不纯?
  • A pure substance has only one type of particle and a sharp, fixed melting and boiling point.
  • An impure substance is a mixture and melts/boils over a range.
  • Melting and boiling points are a purity test — measure them fairly and reliably.
  • 纯物质只有一种粒子, 有固定的单一熔点和沸点。
  • 不纯的物质是 混合物, 在一段范围里熔化/沸腾。
  • 熔点和沸点是纯度测试 — 要公平、可靠 地测量。
