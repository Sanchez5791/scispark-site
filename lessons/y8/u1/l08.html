Chromatography 1: Separating Mixtures
色谱法 1：分离混合物
⏰ 热身 · Warm-up hook
Chromatography is the ink police: hidden colours line up and confess.
色谱像墨水警察：把藏起来的颜色拉出来。
  • Take any coloured marker.
  • Put one dot on a paper towel, then drip water on the dot.
  • Watch the colour split.
  • 拿任何一支彩色 marker.
  • 在纸巾上点一个点, 再滴水上去.
  • 看颜色散开.
  • Forensic chemists use this exact method to match ink on a fake document to a suspect's pen.
  • 法医化学家就用这个方法, 把假文件上的墨水跟嫌疑人的笔对上.

SCREEN 2 · LEARN
Learn — Chromatography 1: Separating Mixtures
机制 · 色谱法 1：分离混合物
  • Chromatography splits a mixture of colours into separate spots.
  • 色谱法把混合的颜色分成一个一个分开的斑点.
  • Like one drop of marker ink opening into blue, red and yellow on wet paper.
  • 就像一滴 marker 墨水在湿纸上散成蓝、红、黄.
  • The paper is the stationary phase (it stays still).
  • The solvent is the mobile phase (it moves up).
  • Dyes that stick less to the paper travel further.
  • 纸是 stationary phase 固定相 (不动).
  • 溶剂是 mobile phase 流动相 (往上爬).
  • 跟纸黏得少的染料走得更远.
🖼️ 配图待出 · Image pendingBest visual: labelled nucleus + shells, clearly marked NOT TO SCALE, nucleus marked 'tiny'. Animate?: No (static). Trap to flag: do not believe the drawn scale; never draw electrons inside the nucleus.
  • 不同染料在纸上走的距离不同.
  • 抓纸抓得少的染料被带得更高; 抓得紧的留在下面.
  • 所以一种墨水能出现好几个不同高度的斑点 — 每个斑点是一种 分开的染料.

❌ 常见错误 · Common mistake — don't fall for this!
  • Students think chromatography always separates everything in a mixture.
  • 学生以为色谱总能把混合物里全部成分分开.
  • It only separates substances that interact differently with the solvent and the paper.
  • 它只能分开那些跟溶剂和滤纸互动不同的物质.
🖼️ 配图待出 · Image pendingBest visual: labelled nucleus + shells, clearly marked NOT TO SCALE, nucleus marked 'tiny'. Animate?: No (static). Trap to flag: do not believe the drawn scale; never draw electrons inside the nucleus.
✶ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, say distillation gives pure water.
  • In real life it isn't 100% pure — chemicals with boiling points near water come along too.
  • 考试时写蒸馏出纯水.
  • 真实蒸馏不是 100% 纯, 沸点接近的杂质会 一起出来.
  • WARNING: Use water-based ink and food-safe dyes only.
  • Do not inhale solvent fumes.
  • 警告: 只用水性墨水和食用级染料.
  • 不要吸入溶剂气味.
🧠 口诀金句 · Golden phrase
Paper holds, solvent moves, colours split.
纸会抓，水会带，颜色分开来。

总结 · 串联
  • Chromatography uses moving solvent + still paper to split a mixture.
  • Different dyes travel different distances.
  • More spots not pure.
  • Start line in pencil; spot above the solvent.
  • 色谱法用流动的溶剂 + 不动的纸把混合物分开.
  • 不同染料走的距离 不同.
  • 斑点越多 不纯.
  • 起始线用铅笔; 墨点在溶剂上方.
