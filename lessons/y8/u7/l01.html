Learn · Concept
Speed = Distance ÷ Time
速率 = 距离 ÷ 时间
What speed means 速率的意思

Speed tells you how much distance is covered in a certain time. Faster = more distance in the same time. 速率告诉你在一段时间内走了多少距离。越快 = 同样时间内走得越远。

🧾 Think of speed like a distance-per-time receipt — it tells you how much distance each second gives. 速率像「每秒距离收据」——每一秒给你多少距离。

⚠️ Real motion can speed up and slow down — this lesson uses average speed. 但实际上，真实运动会忽快忽慢；这一课先用平均速率。

The formula 公式
v = d / t

speed = distance / time 速率 = 距离 / 时间

Chant: Speed is distance over time — d on top, t below. 顺口记：速率看距离除时间——d 在上，t 在下。

The slash / means distance is shared across time. 斜线 / 的意思是距离被时间分掉。

★ The three-piece rule 公式三件套

Formula first. Numbers second. Answer with unit last. 先公式，再数字，最后答案加单位。

Step 1 — write the formula:        v = d / t
Step 2 — substitute numbers + units:  v = 100 m / 20 s
Step 3 — write answer + unit:       v = 5 m/s

🍳 The three steps are like cooking: recipe, ingredients, final dish. 三步像煮东西：先写食谱，再放材料，最后端出成品。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 错误观念红牌

"No unit, no mark." Writing just "5" for a speed is WRONG. Speed must carry a unit (m/s or km/h). 「没单位，没分。」速率只写「5」是错的。速率一定要带单位（m/s 或 km/h）。

Units 单位

m/s = metres per second. km/h = kilometres per hour. Pick the unit that matches the distance and time given in the question. m/s = 米每秒。km/h = 千米每时。用题目给的距离和时间相配的单位。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam, write 'ignoring air resistance'. In real life, air resistance always exists. After falling a long time, an object reaches 'terminal velocity' and stops speeding up. That's why skydivers don't keep accelerating forever. 考试时写：「忽略空气阻力」。真实空气阻力一直在。掉久了速度会停（terminal velocity）。跳伞的人不会永远越掉越快。

Q05
Compare two speeds · 比较速率 L3
3 marks

A balloon rises 15 m in 6 s, then a second balloon rises 15 m in 5 s. Calculate the speed of each balloon and state which is faster. 一个气球 6 秒上升 15 米，第二个气球 5 秒上升 15 米。算出两个气球的速率，并说出哪个更快。

Saved ✓ 已保存
💡 Hint 提示
Same distance, different time. Smaller time = faster. balloon 1 = 15 / 6; balloon 2 = 15 / 5. Then: 15 / 6 = 2.5 m/s; 15 / 5 = 3 m/s. Compare. 距离一样，时间不同。时间越小 = 越快。气球 1 = 15 / 6；气球 2 = 15 / 5。15 / 6 = 2.5 m/s；15 / 5 = 3 m/s。比较。
✓ Marking Scheme
balloon 1 = 2.5 m/s; balloon 2 = 3 m/s; balloon 2 is faster. [1 each correct speed; 1 correct comparison]

✗ Reject: no units; balloon 1 faster; 2.5 / 3 without unit.
