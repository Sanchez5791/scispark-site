The right tool, the right unit, and the right safety check

对的工具、对的单位、对的安全检查
🌍 Real-world · 真实情境
• A lab technician sets up experiments for a whole school.
• Before anyone starts, they pick the correct apparatus, label every chemical with the right hazard symbol, and make sure each measurement uses the correct unit.
• One wrong symbol or wrong tool can cause a burn, a spill, or a ruined result.
• This is the everyday backbone of safe science.
• 实验室技术员为整间学校准备实验。
• 在任何人开始之前,他们选对仪器、给每种化学品贴对危险符号、 并确保每个测量用对单位。
• 一个错的符号或错的工具,可能造成烧伤、泼洒,或毁掉结果。
• 这是安全科学每天的支柱。

Apparatus, Hazard Symbols and Units

学习重点
🔎
Common apparatus and what they are for
常见仪器和它们的用途
• Test tube: holds small amounts of substance for reactions.
• 试管:盛少量物质做反应。
• Beaker: holds and mixes larger volumes of liquid (rough volume).
• 烧杯:盛放和混合较大体积的液体(粗略体积)。
• Measuring cylinder: measures volume of liquid more precisely.
• 量筒:更精确地测量液体体积。
• Conical flask: holds liquid; easy to swirl without spilling.
• 锥形瓶:盛液体;容易摇晃而不泼出。
• Funnel: directs liquid or supports filter paper for filtering.
• 漏斗:引导液体,或托住滤纸做过滤。
• Evaporating dish: holds liquid to be evaporated.
• 蒸发皿:盛放要蒸发的液体。
• Bunsen burner: provides heat.
• 本生灯:提供热。
• Tripod + gauze + clay triangle: support equipment over the flame.
• 三脚架 + 石棉网 + 陶土三角:在火焰上方支撑器材。
• Dropper / pipette: adds liquid drop by drop.
• 滴管 / 移液管:一滴一滴加液体。
• Syringe: measures and delivers a set volume.
• 注射器:测量并送出一个设定体积。
• Spatula: scoops small amounts of solid.
• 药匙:舀取少量固体。
• Crucible tongs: hold hot containers safely.
• 坩埚钳:安全地夹住热容器。
🔎 Plain · 白话
Apparatus names matter because each tool has a job.
仪器名字重要,因为每个工具都有自己的工作。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
Lab apparatus is a kitchen drawer: spatula, tongs, funnel — choose the tool that matches the task.
实验仪器像厨房抽屉:铲子、夹子、漏斗,任务不同工具不同。

⚖️
Choosing the right apparatus for the job
为任务选对仪器
• To measure a precise volume of liquid: use a measuring cylinder (not a beaker).
• 要测量精确的液体体积:用量筒(不用烧杯)。
• To heat a substance: use a Bunsen burner with a tripod and gauze.
• 要加热物质:用本生灯,配三脚架和石棉网。
• To add a few drops: use a dropper or pipette.
• 要加几滴:用滴管或移液管。
• To hold a hot crucible: use crucible tongs.
• 要夹热坩埚:用坩埚钳。
🧠 Mnemonic · 口诀
Rough volume? Beaker. Better volume? Measuring cylinder. Exact transfer? Pipette or burette.
记法:粗略烧杯,较准量筒,精确移液管/滴定管。
🧪
Hazard warning symbols
危险警告符号
• Oxidising: can provide oxygen so other things burn more fiercely.
• 氧化性:能提供氧气,让其他东西烧得更猛烈。
• Explosive: can explode.
• 爆炸性:可能爆炸。
• Flammable: catches fire easily.
• 易燃:容易着火。
• Corrosive: attacks and destroys skin, eyes, or materials.
• 腐蚀性:侵蚀并破坏皮肤、眼睛或材料。
• Toxic: can cause death or serious harm if swallowed/breathed in.
• 有毒:吞下/吸入可能致死或严重伤害。
• Harmful / irritant: can cause less serious harm or irritation.
• 有害 / 刺激性:可能造成较轻伤害或刺激。
• Dangerous for the environment: harms wildlife or the environment.
• 对环境有害:伤害野生生物或环境。
• (Electric shock warning where electricity is used.)
• (用电的地方有触电警告。
• )
🔎 Plain · 白话
• Hazard symbols are not decorations.
• They tell you what can hurt you and what habit prevents it.
• 危险符号不是贴纸。
• 它告诉你哪里会伤人,该用什么习惯防。

🧩
Units of measurement
测量单位
• Temperature: degrees Celsius (C).
• 温度:摄氏度(C)。
• Time: seconds (s), minutes (min), hours (h).
• 时间:秒(s)、分钟(min)、小时(h)。
• Volume: cubic centimetres (cm3) and cubic decimetres (dm3).
• 体积:立方厘米(cm3)和立方分米(dm3)。
• Mass: grams (g) and kilograms (kg).
• 质量:克(g)和千克(kg)。
• Length: millimetres (mm), centimetres (cm), metres (m).
• 长度:毫米(mm)、厘米(cm)、米(m)。
🔎 Plain · 白话
• A number without a unit is half a message.
• 25 could mean 25 cm3, 25 g, or 25 degrees.
• 没单位的数字只说了一半。
• 25 可能是 25 cm3、25 g、也可能是 25 度。
😄 Humour · 幽默
• "Add 25" is not a method.
• It is a mystery novel.
• 「加 25」不是步骤,是悬疑小说。

A beaker is not for measuring exact volume
烧杯不是用来测量精确体积的
🔎 Plain · 白话
• A beaker shows rough volume.
• It is built for holding and mixing, not exact measuring.
• 烧杯只能粗看体积。
• 它主要是装和混,不是精确量。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
• Beaker marks are like guessing time by the sun.
• Useful roughly, bad for exact timing.
• 烧杯刻度像看太阳猜时间。
• 大概可以,精准不行。
🚩 COMMON MISTAKE · 常见错误
✘ Students often think · 学生常以为
Students use the marks on a beaker to measure an exact volume of liquid.
学生用烧杯上的刻度去测量精确的液体体积。
✔ Correct · 正确
• Beaker markings are only rough guides.
• For a precise volume, use a measuring cylinder (or a pipette/burette for very precise work).
• Choosing the wrong apparatus makes the measurement inaccurate.
• 烧杯的刻度只是粗略参考。
• 要精确的体积, 用量筒(非常精确的工作用移液管/滴定管)。
• 选错仪器会让测量不准确。
🎯 What the examiner wants · 考官要什么
State = short answer · Describe = say what & how · Explain = give the reason (fact + because) · Define = use the exact term.
State=短答 · Describe=说什么、怎样 · Explain=讲原因(事实+because) · Define=用准确术语。

Key points to keep
记住这几点
• Apparatus: each tool has a job (measuring cylinder for precise volume, Bunsen burner for heat, tongs for hot items, etc.).
• 仪器:每个工具有它的工作(量筒测精确体积、本生灯加热、钳子夹热物等)。
• Hazard symbols: oxidising, explosive, flammable, corrosive, toxic, harmful, environmental, electric shock.
• 危险符号:氧化性、爆炸性、易燃、腐蚀性、有毒、有害、对环境有害、触电。
• Units: temperature C; time s/min/h; volume cm3/dm3; mass g/kg; length mm/cm/m.
• 单位:温度 C;时间 s/min/h;体积 cm3/dm3;质量 g/kg;长度 mm/cm/m。
