Data only speaks when you organise it well

数据只有整理好了才会说话
🌍 Real-world · 真实情境
• A climate scientist collects thousands of temperature readings.
• The numbers mean nothing as a pile — they only reveal a trend once they are put in a table and drawn as a graph.
• Choosing the right chart, spotting an odd reading, and judging the data's quality is how raw numbers become a discovery that can guide real decisions.
• 气候科学家收集成千上万个温度读数。
• 这些数字堆在一起没有意义
• 只有放进表格、画成图, 才会显出趋势。
• 选对图表、发现怪读数、判断数据质量, 就是把原始数字变成能指导真实决策的发现的方法。
📘
What this lesson is about
这一课讲什么
• This lesson: putting data in tables, drawing line/bar/pie charts, finding anomalies, evaluating data, and improving experiments.
• 这一课:把数据放进表格、画折线图/柱状图/饼图、找异常点、 评估数据、改进实验。
⏰ Warm-up · 热身回顾
• Before you start: think of one experiment you've seen.
• What was being changed, and what was measured?
• 开始前:想一个你看过的实验。
• 它在改变什么?又在测量什么?
🔎 Plain · 白话
• Data is quiet until you organise it.
• Tables and graphs make the pattern visible.
• 数据没整理前不会说话。
• 表格和图会把规律显出来。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
• Raw data is like loose Lego bricks.
• A table sorts them; a graph builds the shape.
• 原始数据像散开的乐高。
• 表格负责分类,图负责搭出形状。

学习重点
🔎
How to build a results table
怎么做一张结果表
• Put the variable you CHANGE (independent) in the first column, with its units.
• 把你"改变"的变量(自变量)放第一列,附上单位。
• Put the variable you MEASURE (dependent) in the next column, with its units.
• 把你"测量"的变量(因变量)放下一列,附上单位。
• Use one row for each test you do.
• 每做一个测试用一行。
• Write the control variables and their values under the table.
• 把控制变量和它们的数值写在表格下方。
🧠 Mnemonic · 口诀
IV first, DV next, units in headings, repeats in rows.
记法:自变量先,因变量后,单位进标题,重复放行里。

⚖️
Choosing the right chart
选对图表
• Line graph: both variables are numbers that change continuously (e.g. temperature over time).
• 折线图:两个变量都是连续变化的数字(例如温度随时间)。
• Bar chart: the independent variable is in categories (e.g. colour, material, type).
• 柱状图:自变量是分类的(例如颜色、材料、种类)。
• Pie chart: shows parts of a whole (proportions adding to 100%).
• 饼图:显示整体的各部分(各部分加起来是 100%)。
🔎 Plain · 白话
Use the chart that matches the data type: continuous numbers, categories, or parts of a whole.
图要配数据类型:连续数字、分类,还是整体比例。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
• Line graph is a road.
• Bar chart is separate buildings.
• Pie chart is a sliced pizza.
• 折线像路,柱状像一栋栋楼,饼图像切好的披萨。

🧪
Steps to draw a line graph or bar chart
画折线图或柱状图的步骤
• Independent variable on the x-axis; dependent variable on the y-axis.
• 自变量放 x 轴;因变量放 y 轴。
• Label both axes with the quantity AND its unit.
• 给两条轴都标上量"和"单位。
• Choose a sensible, even scale that uses most of the grid.
• 选一个合理、均匀、用满大部分网格的刻度。
• Plot points accurately; draw a line of best fit (or neat bars).
• 准确地标点;画最佳拟合线(或整齐的柱子)。
🔎 Plain · 白话
• A graph fails fast if the axes or scale are wrong.
• Fix the frame before drawing points.
• 坐标轴或刻度错,整张图马上坏。
• 先搭框架,再标点。

🧩
Anomalies, and evaluating your data
异常点,以及评估你的数据
• An anomalous result (anomaly) is a point that does not fit the trend.
• 异常结果(异常点)是不符合趋势的点。
• Circle anomalies and ignore them when drawing the line of best fit.
• 把异常点圈出来,画最佳拟合线时忽略它们。
• A good evaluation has three stages:
• 1. Evaluate the quality of data (anomalies, spread, range, errors).
• 2. Evaluate the method (was the measuring method good?).
• 3. Suggest improvements (so you can be more confident in conclusions).
• 好的评估有三个阶段:
• 1. 评估数据质量(异常点、分散、范围、有没有误差)。
• 2. 评估方法(测量方法好不好?)。
• 3. 提出改进(让你对结论更有信心)。
🔎 Plain · 白话
• An anomaly is not automatically rubbish.
• Circle it, think why it happened, and decide how to handle it.
• 异常点不一定是垃圾。
• 先圈出,想原因,再决定怎么处理。

Do not "dot-to-dot" and do not use a bar chart for continuous data
不要"连点成线",也不要用柱状图画连续数据
😄 Humour · 幽默
Dot-to-dot is for little-kid colouring books, not for best-fit science graphs.
连点成线适合幼儿涂色书,不适合科学最佳拟合图。
🧠 Mnemonic · 口诀
Best fit, not connect-it.
记法:最佳拟合,不要硬连。
🚩 COMMON MISTAKE · 常见错误
✘ Students often think · 学生常以为
Students join every point with short straight lines ("dot-to-dot"), and use a bar chart even when both variables are continuous numbers.
学生把每个点用短直线连起来("连点成线"), 而且即使两个变量都是连续数字也用柱状图。
✔ Correct · 正确
• For continuous numerical data, draw a single line (or smooth curve) of best fit that ignores anomalies — not a dot-to-dot zig-zag.
• Use a bar chart only when the independent variable is in categories.
• 对于连续数值数据,画一条忽略异常点的最佳拟合线 (或平滑曲线)
• 不是连点成线的锯齿。
• 只有当自变量是分类时才用柱状图。
⚖️ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
📝 In exams · 考试里
Exams reward a single straight/smooth line of best fit and penalise dot-to-dot.
考试奖励单一的直线/平滑最佳拟合线,惩罚连点成线。
🔬 Real science · 真实科学
Real data is rarely a perfect straight line; scientists fit a model line and treat scatter and anomalies with statistics, but the school skill is the same: best fit, not dot-to-dot.
真实数据很少是完美直线;科学家拟合一条模型线, 用统计处理散布和异常点,但学校阶段的本事一样:最佳拟合,不是连点成线。
🎯 What the examiner wants · 考官要什么
State = short answer · Describe = say what & how · Explain = give the reason (fact + because) · Define = use the exact term.
State=短答 · Describe=说什么、怎样 · Explain=讲原因(事实+because) · Define=用准确术语。

Key points to keep
记住这几点
• Tables: independent (with units) first, dependent next, one row per test.
• 表格:自变量(带单位)在前,因变量在后,每个测试一行。
• Charts: line for continuous numbers, bar for categories, pie for parts of a whole.
• 图表:连续数字用折线,分类用柱状,整体各部分用饼图。
• Graphs: label axes with units, even scale, plot accurately, best-fit line.
• 画图:坐标轴标单位、均匀刻度、准确标点、最佳拟合线。
• Anomalies: circle and ignore for the line.
• 异常点:圈出来,画线时忽略。
• Evaluate: data quality, method, then suggest improvements.
• 评估:数据质量、方法,然后提出改进。
