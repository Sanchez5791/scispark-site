Good science follows the same steps, every time

好的科学,每一次都照同样的步骤走
🌍 Real-world · 真实情境
• A medical researcher testing a new medicine cannot just try it and hope.
• They follow a fixed set of steps — observe, ask, predict, test, analyse, conclude, then share the results so others can check them.
• Skipping a step can put lives at risk.
• The same process is used in every science job, from drug testing to building bridges.
• 测试新药的医学研究员,不能只是试一试、碰运气。
• 他们照一套固定步骤走
• 观察、提问、预测、测试、分析、得出结论, 然后把结果分享出去让别人检查。
• 漏掉一步可能危及生命。
• 从测药到建桥,每一份科学工作都用同一套流程。
📘
What this lesson is about
这一课讲什么
• The scientific method is a step-by-step way to answer questions.
• 科学方法是一套一步一步回答问题的方式。
• This lesson walks through every step, in order.
• 这一课按顺序走过每一个步骤。
• It also covers what happens after an experiment: peer review and publishing.
• 也讲实验之后会发生什么:同行评审和发表。
⏰ Warm-up · 热身回顾
• Before you start: think of one experiment you've seen.
• What was being changed, and what was measured?
• 开始前:想一个你看过的实验。
• 它在改变什么?又在测量什么?
🔎 Plain · 白话
• Good science is not random trying.
• It is a repeatable route.
• 好科学不是乱试。
• 它是一条别人也能走的路线。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
The method is like a recipe: miss a step, and the cake may lie to you.
科学方法像食谱:漏一步,蛋糕可能骗你。

The Scientific Method (the whole process)

学习重点
🔎
The steps of the scientific method
科学方法的步骤
• 1. Make an observation.
• 2. Ask a question.
• 3. Do research.
• 4. Form a hypothesis (a possible explanation).
• 5. Make a prediction (what you expect if the hypothesis is true).
• 6. Perform experiments.
• 7. Organise and analyse the data.
• 8. Draw a conclusion.
• 9. Communicate the results.
• 1. 做观察。
• 2. 提出问题。
• 3. 做研究。
• 4. 形成假设(一个可能的解释)。
• 5. 做预测(如果假设成立,你预计会发生什么)。
• 6. 进行实验。
• 7. 整理和分析数据。
• 8. 得出结论。
• 9. 发表结果。
🧠 Mnemonic · 口诀
O-Q-H-P-E-C-C: Observe, Question, Hypothesis, Predict, Experiment, Conclude, Communicate.
记法:看→问→猜→预→做→结→讲。

⚖️
What if the results do not support the hypothesis?
如果结果不支持假设,怎么办?
• After drawing a conclusion, scientists ask: do the results support the hypothesis?
• 得出结论后,科学家会问:结果支持假设吗?
• YES -> the hypothesis is supported (it is never "proven" forever).
• 是 -> 假设得到支持(但永远不算"永远证明")。
• NO -> go back, change the hypothesis, and test again.
• 否 -> 回头,修改假设,再测一次。
• Science is a loop, not a straight line.
• 科学是一个循环,不是一条直线。
🔎 Plain · 白话
• Wrong prediction does not mean failed science.
• It means the method gave you an honest answer.
• 预测错不等于实验失败。
• 它代表实验给了你真实答案。
😄 Humour · 幽默
• Science is allowed to say, "Oops, back to the drawing board." That is the job.
• 科学可以说「哎呀,重来画板」。
• 这本来就是工作。
🧪
Peer review — other scientists check the work
同行评审 —— 其他科学家检查这份工作
• Research carried out -> results written as a paper -> checked by other scientists -> published in a journal.
• 研究做出来 -> 结果写成论文 -> 由其他科学家检查 -> 在期刊上发表。
• Peer review means other experts read the work and check it for mistakes before it is published.
• 同行评审的意思是:其他专家在发表前阅读这份工作,检查有没有错误。
🔎 Plain · 白话
Other experts check whether the work has mistakes, weak evidence, or unfair claims.
其他专家检查有没有错、证据弱不弱、结论有没有吹太大。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
Peer review is like another chef tasting the soup before it leaves the kitchen.
同行评审像另一位厨师在出菜前先尝汤。

🧩
Why is it important that scientists publish their results?
为什么科学家发表结果很重要?
• Others can check the work -> results become more accurate and trustworthy.
• 别人可以检查这份工作 -> 结果变得更准确、更可信。
• Knowledge can grow -> others build on the findings -> new discoveries.
• 知识可以增长 -> 别人在这些发现上继续建设 -> 新发现。
• Science becomes reliable -> peer review increases trust.
• 科学变得可靠 -> 同行评审增加信任。
• Shared with the world -> helps improve medicine, technology, daily life.
• 与全世界分享 -> 帮助改进医学、科技、日常生活。
🔎 Plain · 白话
Publishing lets other scientists repeat, check, and build on the work.
发表让别人能重复、检查、继续往上盖。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
• Science is like a shared tower.
• If nobody publishes the bricks, nobody can build higher.
• 科学像大家一起盖塔。
• 不把砖拿出来,别人就盖不上去。

A conclusion does not "prove" a hypothesis forever
结论不会"永远证明"一个假设
🔎 Plain · 白话
A conclusion is the best answer from this evidence, not a magic final truth stamp.
结论只是根据这份证据的最好答案,不是永久真理盖章。
🧠 Mnemonic · 口诀
Results support, not prove forever.
记法:结果能支持,不能永远证明。
🚩 COMMON MISTAKE · 常见错误
✘ Students often think · 学生常以为
Students think one experiment that supports a hypothesis "proves it true" and the work is finished.
学生以为一个支持假设的实验就"证明它是对的", 工作就结束了。
✔ Correct · 正确
• A supported hypothesis is only supported so far.
• Other scientists must be able to repeat and check it.
• New evidence can change the conclusion later.
• That is why publishing and peer review matter.
• 一个被支持的假设,只是到目前为止被支持。
• 其他科学家必须能够重复并检查它。
• 新的证据以后可能改变结论。
• 这就是为什么发表和同行评审很重要。
🎯 What the examiner wants · 考官要什么
State = short answer · Describe = say what & how · Explain = give the reason (fact + because) · Define = use the exact term.
State=短答 · Describe=说什么、怎样 · Explain=讲原因(事实+because) · Define=用准确术语。

Key points to keep

• 发表让别人能够检查、继续建设、并信任这门科学。
