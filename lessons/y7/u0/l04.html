Four words that decide if you can trust a measurement

四个词,决定你能不能相信一个测量
🌍 Real-world · 真实情境
• An engineer checking the steel for a bridge takes many measurements.
• They must be accurate (close to the true value), precise (close to each other), and reliable (repeatable, with enough data and no odd results).
• If even one is wrong, the bridge could be unsafe.
• These four words are how scientists judge whether data can be trusted.
• 检查桥梁钢材的工程师会做很多次测量。
• 这些测量必须准确(接近真值)、精密(彼此接近)、 可靠(可重复、数据够、没有怪结果)。
• 只要有一样出错,桥就可能不安全。
• 这四个词,就是科学家判断数据能不能相信的方法。
📘
What this lesson is about
这一课讲什么
• Accuracy, precision, reliability, validity — four different ideas.
• 准确度、精密度、可靠度、有效度
• 四个不同的概念。
• They are easy to mix up, so this lesson defines each clearly.
• 它们很容易混淆,所以这一课把每一个都讲清楚。
⏰ Warm-up · 热身回顾
• Before you start: think of one experiment you've seen.
• What was being changed, and what was measured?
• 开始前:想一个你看过的实验。
• 它在改变什么?又在测量什么?
🔎 Plain · 白话
These four words decide whether a number is trustworthy or just wearing a lab coat.
这四个词决定一个数字可信,还是只是穿了实验袍装可信。
🧩 Analogy · 比喻
A dartboard makes it easy: centre = accurate; tight group = precise.
飞镖靶最好懂:中靶心=准确;聚在一起=精密。

Accuracy, Precision, Reliability and Validity

学习重点
🔎
Reliable and precise
可靠和精密
• Reliable: no anomalies, enough data, and measurements are close to each other (or close to an average).
• 可靠:没有异常、数据足够、测量值彼此接近(或接近平均值)。
• Precise: measurements are close to each other, with more decimal places.
• 精密:测量值彼此接近,而且有更多小数位。
• They overlap (both like measurements close together) but are not the same.
• 它们有重叠(都喜欢测量值彼此接近)但不是同一个。
🔎 Plain · 白话
• Reliable results repeat well.
• Precise readings sit close together.
• 可靠是重复后差不多。
• 精密是读数彼此很靠近。
🧠 Mnemonic · 口诀
Reliable repeats. Precise packs together.
记法:可靠会重复,精密会挤在一起。

⚖️
Valid and accurate
有效和准确
• Valid: the measurement is relevant (it measures what it should) AND close to the true value.
• 有效:测量是相关的(测到了它该测的)而且接近真值。
• Accurate: measurements are close to the true value.
• 准确:测量值接近真值。
• They overlap at "close to the true value"; validity also needs the data to be relevant to the question.
• 它们在"接近真值"上重叠;有效度还要求数据与问题相关。
🔎 Plain · 白话
• Valid means the test really tests the question.
• Accurate means close to the true value.
• 有效是实验真的在测那个问题。
• 准确是靠近真值。

🧩
Quick test for each word
每个词的快速判断
• Accurate?
• Compare to the TRUE value.
• 准确吗?跟"真值"比。
• Precise?
• Compare the repeats to EACH OTHER.
• 精密吗?把重复测量"彼此"比。
• Reliable?
• Are there enough repeats with no anomalies?
• 可靠吗?重复够多、没有异常吗?
• Valid?
• Does the test measure the RIGHT thing fairly?
• 有效吗?这个测试公平地测到了"对的"东西吗?
🧠 Mnemonic · 口诀
Accurate asks: close to true? Precise asks: close to each other? Reliable asks: repeat works? Valid asks: right test?
口诀:准问真值,精问彼此,靠问重复,效问测对没。
Precise does not mean accurate
精密不等于准确
🔎 Plain · 白话
Precise readings can still be wrong if the instrument is biased.
如果仪器有偏差,精密读数也可能全都错。
😄 Humour · 幽默
• A broken clock can be very precise if it is wrong the same way every time.
• Still not useful.
• 坏钟如果每次都错得一样,也很「精密」。
• 但还是没用。
🚩 COMMON MISTAKE · 常见错误
✘ Students often think · 学生常以为
Students think that if measurements are very close to each other (precise), they must also be correct (accurate).
学生以为如果测量值彼此非常接近(精密), 它们一定也是对的(准确)。
✔ Correct · 正确
• Measurements can be precise (tightly grouped) but all wrong in the same way — far from the true value.
• Precise = close to each other; accurate = close to the true value.
• They are different.
• 测量值可以很精密(紧紧聚在一起)却以同样的方式全错
• 都离真值很远。
• 精密=彼此接近;准确=接近真值。
• 两者不同。
⚖️ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
📝 In exams · 考试里
Exams often want the textbook split: accurate = near true value, precise = repeats agree.
考试通常要课本式区分:准确=接近真值,精密=重复一致。
🔬 Real science · 真实科学
• In real labs a faulty instrument (e.g. a wrongly zeroed balance) gives precise but inaccurate readings — consistently wrong.
• 在真实实验室,坏掉的仪器(例如零点没调好的天平) 会给出精密但不准确的读数
• 一致地错。
🎯 What the examiner wants · 考官要什么
State = short answer · Describe = say what & how · Explain = give the reason (fact + because) · Define = use the exact term.
State=短答 · Describe=说什么、怎样 · Explain=讲原因(事实+because) · Define=用准确术语。

Key points to keep

• 有效:公平地测到"对的"东西。
• Precise does NOT mean accurate.
• 精密"不"等于准确。
