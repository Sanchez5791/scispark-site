Reactants and Products

反应物与生成物
Real-world spark
•Bread does not look like flour, water or yeast.
•The starting things changed into a new thing.
•In chemistry, the starting things are called REACTANTS.
•The new things made are called PRODUCTS.
•面包看起来不像面粉、水或酵母。
•起始的东西变成了一个新东西。
•在化学里,起始的东西叫"反应物"(reactants)。
•做出来的新东西叫"产物"(products)。

Reactants and Products
•REACTANTS are the substances you start with, before the reaction.
•PRODUCTS are the new substances made, after the reaction.
•We show this as: reactants -> products
•反应物(reactants)= 你一开始就有的物质,反应前。
•产物(products)= 反应后做出来的新物质。
•写成:反应物 -> 产物
Reading a reaction in words
•A reaction can be written using only the names of the substances.
•Example: magnesium + oxygen -> magnesium oxide The things before the arrow are reactants.
•The thing after the arrow is the product.
•(We only use names here.
•We do not use symbols or formulae.)
•反应可以只用物质的名字写出来。
•例子:镁 + 氧气 -> 氧化镁 箭头前的东西是反应物。
•箭头后的东西是产物。
•(这里只用名字,不用符号或化学式。
•)
•When two solutions react, a new solid can form.
•This solid is a PRECIPITATE.
•The two solutions are the reactants.
•The solid is a product.
•当两种溶液反应时,会生成一个新的固体。
•这个固体叫"沉淀" (precipitate)。
•两种溶液是反应物。
•固体是产物。
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘the product is always bigger or heavier than the reactants.
产物总是比反应物更大或更重。
✔a product is simply the new substance made. It can be a solid, a liquid or a gas, and it is not always bigger or heavier.
产物只是做出来的新物质。它可以是固体、液体或气体, 也不一定更大或更重。

WRAP · 连接与总结

Reactants and Products

反应物与生成物
📌 SUMMARY · 总结
◆Reactants are what you start with.
◆Products are the new substances made.
◆We write it as reactants -> products.
◆The names change because a new substance is made — that is a chemical reaction.
◆This links back to the signs we saw: gas, precipitate, colour change and temperature change all show a reaction is making products.
◆反应物是你一开始就有的。
◆产物是做出来的新物质。
◆写成:反应物 -> 产物。
◆名字变了,因为生成了新物质——这就是 化学反应。
◆这接回我们看过的几个 sign:气体、沉淀、颜色变化和 温度变化,都说明反应正在生成产物。
