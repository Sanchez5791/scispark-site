LEARN · 核心

Thermal Decomposition

热分解
WHAT THERMAL DECOMPOSITION IS
•Thermal decomposition is when one substance is broken down by heat into two or more new substances.
•Only heat is needed.
•No other chemical is added.
•热分解是用热把一种物质分解成两种或更多新物质。
•只需要热。
•不加别的化学物质。
WORKED EXAMPLE — COPPER CARBONATE
•Green copper carbonate is heated.
•It turns into black copper oxide and gives off carbon dioxide gas.
•copper carbonate -> copper oxide + carbon dioxide Two signs of a chemical reaction are seen:
•- colour change (green -> black)
•- a gas is given off The carbon dioxide turns limewater milky (cloudy) — this is the test.
•加热绿色的碳酸铜。
•它变成黑色的氧化铜，并放出二氧化碳气体。
•碳酸铜 → 氧化铜 + 二氧化碳 看到两个化学反应的信号：
•- 颜色变化（绿 → 黑）
•- 放出气体 二氧化碳使石灰水变浑浊——这是检验方法。
HEATING APPARATUS
•The solid is heated in a test tube over a Bunsen burner.
•A delivery tube carries the gas into limewater to test it.
•固体放在试管里，用本生灯加热。
•导管把气体送进石灰水来检验。
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘When the solid is heated it just melts or disappears.
固体加热只是熔化了或消失了。
✔It does not just melt or disappear. The heat changes it into new substances (a different solid + a gas). That is why the colour changes and a gas is given off.
它不是熔化或消失。热把它变成新物质（不同的固体 + 一种气体）。 所以颜色才会变、才会放出气体。
VARIABLES BOX (TWS —
•In an investigation, we heat the solid and measure its mass over time.
•- Independent variable (the one we change): heating time
•- Dependent variable (the one we measure): the mass of the solid As gas escapes, the mass of the solid goes down.
•在探究里，我们加热固体，并随时间测它的质量。
•- 自变量（我们改的）：加热时间
•- 因变量（我们测的）：固体的质量 气体跑掉时，固体的质量会下降。
🧠 口诀金句 · MNEMONIC
Thermal = heat; decomposition = break down.
thermal=热；decomposition=拆开。

A solid is heated and decomposes. As it is heated, gas escapes. The graph shows the mass of the solid against heating time. Describe how the mass of the solid changes as it is heated.
the mass goes down as the solid is heated (1); then it stops changing / stays the same / levels off when the reaction is finished (1).

Q8[2 marks]
A green solid is heated. It turns into a black solid and gives off a gas that turns limewater milky. (a) Name the type of reaction that is happening.
(a) thermal decomposition (1). (b) any two: a colour change happens (green to black); a gas is given off; new substances are formed. (1 mark each, max 2)
(a) 热分解 (1). (b) 任意两个: a 颜色变化 happens (绿色 to black); a 气体 is given off; new substances are formed. (每项1分, max 2)
Q9[1 mark]
Combustion and thermal decomposition are both chemical reactions. Compare them by stating one way they are different.
Combustion needs oxygen and gives out heat; thermal decomposition does not need oxygen and takes in heat to break one substance apart (any one clear difference).
燃烧 needs 氧气 and gives out 热; 热分解 does not need 氧气 and takes in 热 to break one substance apart (任意一个 clear difference).
Q10[2 marks]
🖼️ IMAGE PENDING · 配图待出 (L08_HOOK_IMG01, L08_LEARN_IMG01)
A student heats a metal carbonate in a sealed tube and the total mass does not change, but when heated in an open tube the mass goes down. Explain why the mass goes down only in the open tube.
The reaction gives off carbon dioxide gas (1); in the open tube this gas escapes so the weighed mass falls, but in the sealed tube the gas is kept so the mass stays the same (1).
The reaction gives off 二氧化碳 气体 (1); in the open tube this 气体 escapes so the weighed 质量 falls, but in the sealed tube the 气体 is kept so the 质量 stays the same (1).

📌 SUMMARY · 总结
◆- Thermal decomposition = one substance broken down by heat into new substances.
◆- Only heat is needed;
◆nothing else is added.
◆- Example: green copper carbonate -> black copper oxide + carbon dioxide.
◆- Signs of the reaction: colour change + a gas given off.
◆- Carbon dioxide turns limewater milky.
◆- In an investigation: heating time is the independent variable, the mass of the solid is the dependent variable.
◆Next lesson: substances that are acids and alkalis — Introducing Acids and Alkalis.
◆- 热分解 = 用热把一种物质分解成新物质。
◆- 只需要热；
◆不加别的。
◆- 例子：绿色碳酸铜 → 黑色氧化铜 + 二氧化碳。
◆- 反应信号：颜色变化 + 放出气体。
◆- 二氧化碳使石灰水变浑浊。
◆- 探究里：加热时间是自变量，固体的质量是因变量。
