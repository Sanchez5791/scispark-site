Real-world spark
•A candle burns and seems to disappear.
•Did its mass really vanish?
•If we trap everything in a sealed jar and weigh it before and after, the total mass stays the same.
•In a reaction, mass is not lost — this is CONSERVATION OF MASS.
•一根蜡烛烧着,好像消失了。
•它的质量真的不见了吗?
•如果我们把所有东西都封在一个密封罐里,反应前后各称一次, 总质量是一样的。
•在反应里,质量不会减少——这就是"质量守恒" (conservation of mass)。

LEARN · 核心

Conservation of Mass

质量守恒
What conservation of mass means
•In a chemical reaction, the total mass of the reactants equals the total mass of the products.
•Nothing is created and nothing is destroyed.
•The particles are not lost — they are only rearranged into new substances.
•在化学反应里,反应物的总质量等于产物的总质量。
•没有东西被创造,也没有东西被毁灭。
•粒子没有消失——只是重新组合成新物质。
Sealed vs open container
•To test conservation of mass fairly, we compare a SEALED container and an OPEN container.
•We change only ONE thing: whether the container is sealed or open.
•Everything else stays the same: same chemicals, same amounts, same balance, same room.
•The one thing we change is the INDEPENDENT VARIABLE.
•The mass reading we measure is the DEPENDENT VARIABLE.
•The things we keep the same are the CONTROL VARIABLES.
•为了公平地测试质量守恒,我们比较一个"密封"容器和一个"开口" 容器。
•我们只改一个东西:容器是密封还是开口。
•其他全部保持一样:一样的化学品、一样的量、一样的天平、一样 的房间。
•我们改的那一个 = 自变量(independent variable)。
•我们测的质量读数 = 因变量(dependent variable)。
•我们保持不变的 = 控制变量(control variables)。
•In an OPEN container, a gas product can escape into the air.
•The balance then reads a LOWER mass after the reaction.
•在"开口"容器里,气体产物会跑到空气中。
•天平在反应后就会显示一个"更低"的质量。
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘the open container lost mass, so mass is destroyed.
开口容器变轻了,所以质量被毁灭了。
✔the mass is not destroyed. A gas escaped and the balance cannot weigh it. If we sealed the container, the gas would stay and the mass would not change.
质量没有被毁灭。是气体跑掉了,天平称不到它。 如果把容器密封,气体留在里面,质量就不会变。

WRAP · 连接与总结

Conservation of Mass

质量守恒
📌 SUMMARY · 总结
◆Mass is conserved in a reaction: the total mass of the reactants equals the total mass of the products.
◆Nothing disappears — the particles are only rearranged into new substances.
◆In a sealed container the balance reading does not change.
◆In an open container a gas can escape, so the reading looks smaller, but the mass was not lost.
◆This links back to L04: reactants become products, and the mass is carried across.
◆反应里质量守恒:反应物的总质量等于产物的总质量。
◆没有东西 消失——粒子只是重新组合成新物质。
◆在密封容器里,天平读数不会 变。
◆在开口容器里,气体会跑掉,所以读数看起来变小,但质量 没有减少。
◆这接回 L04:反应物变成产物,质量被带了过去。
