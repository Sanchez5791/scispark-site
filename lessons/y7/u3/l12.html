LEARN · 核心

Neutralisation

中和反应
What Neutralisation Is
•Neutralisation is a chemical reaction between an acid and an alkali.
•The products are a salt and water.
•Word equation: acid + alkali -> salt + water.
•中和是酸和碱之间的化学反应。
•产物是盐和水。
•字方程式: acid + alkali -> salt + water。
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✔Use an indicator. As the acid is neutralised, the indicator colour changes. Universal Indicator moves from red/orange (acid) towards green (neutral) and on to blue (alkali) if excess alkali is added.
用指示剂。酸被中和时,指示剂颜色会变。 广用指示剂从红/橙(酸)往绿(中性)走,若加多了碱会到蓝(碱)。
The pH Scale
•The pH scale tells us how acidic or alkaline a solution is.
•pH 标度告诉我们溶液有多酸或多碱。
🧱 EXAM vs REALITY · 考试 vs 真实
In the exam · 考试里
•In the exam, write pH from 1 to 14, with 7 = neutral.
考试时写: pH 是 1 到 14, 7 = 中性。
In real life · 真实里
•In real life, very strong acids can have pH below 0, and very strong bases above 14.
•The 1-14 scale is a simplified range for everyday substances.
真实里浓强酸可到 0 以下, 浓强碱可到 14 以上。
1-14 是简化范围。
🧠 口诀金句 · MNEMONIC
Acid + alkali -> salt + water.
酸 + 碱 → 盐 + 水。
