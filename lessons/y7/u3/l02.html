4 Signs of a Chemical Reaction

化学反应的 4 个迹象
•A chemical reaction often leaves visible signs.
•Four common signs are: (1) a gas is given off (bubbles);
•(2) a precipitate forms (a solid appears when two liquids mix);
•(3) a colour change;
•(4) a temperature change (it gets hotter or colder).
•化学反应常常留下看得见的信号。
•四个常见信号是:(1) 放出气体(冒泡); (2) 生成沉淀(两种液体混合时出现固体);(3) 颜色变化;(4) 温度变化 (变热或变冷)。
•A precipitate is a solid that forms when two solutions react.
•It does not dissolve in the liquid, so it appears as a cloudy solid.
•Carbon dioxide gas is tested with limewater, which turns milky.
•沉淀是两种溶液反应时生成的固体。
•它不溶于液体,所以会以混浊固体出现。
•二氧化 碳气体用石灰水测,石灰水会变混浊。
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘Any bubbles mean a chemical reaction.
只要有气泡就是化学反应。
✔Bubbles from boiling water are just water turning to steam — no new substance, so it is physical. Bubbles of a NEW gas given off during a reaction are a chemical sign. Ask whether the gas is a new substance.
水沸腾的气泡只是水变成水蒸气——没有新物质,是物理变化。反应中放出 一种新气体的气泡才是化学信号。要问:这气体是不是新物质。
🧱 EXAM vs REALITY · 考试 vs 真实
Note · 注
•"In the exam, draw a one-way arrow (→) for reactions.
•In real life, most reactions go both ways (⇌) — they reach a balance, not a complete stop."
"考试时画单向箭头 (→)。
真实多数反应是双向的 (⇌),反应不会真的'完成', 只是到平衡点。
"

Copper carbonate is a green solid. When copper carbonate is heated a black solid called copper oxide is made. Carbon dioxide is also made. (a) Write down the names of the reactant and the products in this reaction. reactant ______ products ______
💡 The reactant is what you start with; the products are what is made. For (b), think about the four signs of a reaction.
反应物是起始物;产物是做出来的东西。(b) 想四个反应信号。
(a) Reactant: copper carbonate. Products: copper oxide and carbon dioxide. (b) Any two: colour change; green turns black; gas is made; carbon dioxide is made; heat or energy change; mass changes because gas escapes. (c) Limewater.
(a) 反应物: 碳酸铜. 产物: 氧化铜 and 二氧化碳. (b) 任意两个: 颜色变化; 绿色 turns black; 气体 is made; 二氧化碳 is made; 热 or energy change; 质量 changes because 气体 escapes. (c) 石灰水.

Q8L3[2 marks]
A student warms a liquid and sees bubbles rising. The student says this proves a chemical reaction is happening. Explain why the student may be wrong, and state what extra evidence would show a chemical reaction.
The bubbles may just be the liquid boiling, which is a physical change with no new substance. Extra evidence of a reaction would be a colour change, a precipitate forming, a temperature change, or a new gas being given off.
The 气泡 may just be the liquid boiling, which is a 物理变化 with no 新物质. Extra evidence of a reaction would be a 颜色变化, a 沉淀 forming, a 温度变化, or a new 气体 being given off.
Q9L2[2 marks]
A glow stick gets warmer when it is bent and shaken, and it changes colour as it lights up. Explain why these observations suggest a chemical reaction is happening.
It shows a temperature change and a colour change (1); these are two of the signs that a new substance is being made / a chemical reaction is happening (1).
It shows a 温度变化 and a 颜色变化 (1); these are two of the signs that a 新物质 is being made / a 化学反应 is happening (1).
Q10L3[2 marks]
A student says: "I saw bubbles when I opened a fizzy drink, so a chemical reaction must have happened." Explain why bubbles alone do not prove a chemical reaction.
The gas was already dissolved in the drink and is only escaping (1); no new substance is made, so this is a physical change, not a chemical reaction (1).
The 气体 was already dissolved in the drink and is only escaping (1); no 新物质 is made, so this is a 物理变化, not a 化学反应 (1).
