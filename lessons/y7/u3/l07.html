LEARN · 核心

Combustion

燃烧
WHAT COMBUSTION IS
•Combustion is burning.
•It is a chemical reaction.
•A fuel reacts with oxygen and releases energy as heat and light.
•Word equation (complete combustion): fuel + oxygen -> carbon dioxide + water
•燃烧就是烧。
•它是一个化学反应。
•燃料和氧气反应，放出热和光。
•文字方程（完全燃烧）： 燃料 + 氧气 → 二氧化碳 + 水
THE FIRE TRIANGLE
•A fire needs three things at the same time: 1.
•fuel 2.
•oxygen 3.
•heat Take any one away and the fire stops.
•火要同时有三样东西： 1. 燃料 2. 氧气 3. 热 拿走任何一样，火就停。
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘Fire is a substance / a thing.
火是一种物质 / 一样东西。
✔Fire is not a substance. It is what we see and feel while a chemical reaction (combustion) is happening — the light and heat given out as fuel reacts with oxygen.
火不是物质。它是燃烧这个化学反应正在发生时，我们看到和感到的 光和热——燃料和氧气反应放出来的。
🧱 EXAM vs REALITY · 考试 vs 真实
Note · 注
•In the exam, write Fuel + O2 -> CO2 + H2O.
•In real life, burning is often incomplete — it also makes CO (carbon monoxide, dangerous) and black smoke.
•That's why kitchens need good ventilation.
考试时写：燃料 + O2 → CO2 + 水。
真实燃烧经常不完全，还会出 CO（有毒）+ 黑烟。
所以厨房要通风。
SAFETY WARNING BAR
•Fire is dangerous.
•Only burn things under adult / teacher supervision.
•Never leave a flame unattended.
•Know where to remove fuel, oxygen, or heat to stop a fire.
•火很危险。
•只在大人 / 老师看着时才点火。
•绝不让火无人看管。
•要知道怎么拿走燃料、氧气或热来灭火。
🧠 口诀金句 · MNEMONIC
Fire needs F-O-H: Fuel, Oxygen, Heat.
火要三样：燃料、氧气、热。

Q7[2 marks]
🖼️ IMAGE PENDING · 配图待出 (L07_Q04_IMG01)
A Bunsen burner can give a clean blue flame or a yellow smoky flame. (a) The yellow smoky flame is incomplete combustion. Name the dangerous gas that incomplete combustion can make.
(a) carbon monoxide (CO). (1) (b) blue flame = complete combustion / burns fully (1); yellow flame = incomplete combustion / does not burn fully, makes soot/smoke (1).
(a) 一氧化碳 (CO). (1) (b) 蓝色 flame = 完全燃烧 / burns fully (1); 黄色 flame = 不完全燃烧 / does not burn fully, makes soot/smoke (1).

🖼️ IMAGE PENDING · 配图待出 (L07_Q04_IMG01)

(1) (c) any one: to act quickly if the fire spreads; to stop unsafe actions; to help if someone is hurt. (1)

Q9[2 marks]
A camper pours water on a campfire and the fire goes out. Explain how adding water puts out the fire, using the fire triangle.
The water removes heat / cools the fuel below its burning temperature (1); one side of the fire triangle (heat) is removed, so the fire cannot continue (1).
The 水 removes 热 / cools the 燃料 below its burning 温度 (1); one side of the fire triangle (热) is removed, so the fire cannot continue (1).
Q10[2 marks]
🖼️ IMAGE PENDING · 配图待出 (L07_HOOK_IMG01, L07_LEARN_IMG01, L07_Q03_IMG01, L07_Q04_IMG01)
A faulty heater burns gas where there is too little oxygen, making a poisonous gas called carbon monoxide. Explain why too little oxygen leads to carbon monoxide instead of carbon dioxide.
With too little oxygen the fuel burns incompletely (1); there is not enough oxygen to make carbon dioxide, so carbon monoxide is made instead (1).
With too little 氧气 the 燃料 burns incompletely (1); there is not enough 氧气 to make 二氧化碳, so 一氧化碳 is made instead (1).

📌 SUMMARY · 总结
◆- Combustion = burning = a chemical reaction.
◆- A fire needs fuel + oxygen + heat (the fire triangle).
◆- Complete combustion: fuel + oxygen -> carbon dioxide + water.
◆- Incomplete combustion also makes carbon monoxide and smoke.
◆- Take away fuel, oxygen, or heat to stop a fire.
◆Next lesson: heating a substance to break it down — Thermal Decomposition.
◆- 燃烧 = 烧 = 一个化学反应。
◆- 火需要燃料 + 氧气 + 热（火三角）。
◆- 完全燃烧：燃料 + 氧气 → 二氧化碳 + 水。
◆- 不完全燃烧还会出一氧化碳和烟。
◆- 拿走燃料、氧气或热，就能灭火。
◆下一课：加热一种物质把它分解——热分解。
