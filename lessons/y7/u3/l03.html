Real-world spark
•A materials engineer designs a new metal.
•To do it, they have to know what happens to the tiny particles when substances react.
•In a reaction, no particle is created or destroyed — the same particles just join up in new ways, like the same building blocks rebuilt into a different model.
•This lesson looks at reactions at the particle level.
•材料工程师设计一种新金属。
•要做到这点,得知道物质反应时那些微小粒子发生了什么。
•反应中,没有粒子被创造或消灭——同一批粒子只是用新方式重新组合,就像同一堆积木 拼成不同模型。
•这节课从粒子层面看反应。

LEARN · 核心

Particles Rearrange in Reactions

反应中粒子重新排列
•In a chemical reaction, the particles of the reactants are rearranged to make the products.
•No particles are made and none are lost — they are just joined together in a new way.
•This is why a reaction makes a new substance.
•化学反应里,反应物的粒子重新排列,做出产物。
•没有粒子被造出来,也没有粒子消失 ——只是用新方式连在一起。
•这就是反应会做出新物质的原因。
•An element is made of one kind of particle.
•A compound is made of two or more kinds of particle joined together.
•In a gas the particles are far apart and random;
•in a solid they are close together in a fixed, regular arrangement.
•元素由一种粒子组成。
•化合物由两种或多种粒子连在一起组成。
•气体里粒子相距远、 排列乱;固体里粒子靠得近、排列固定整齐。
•GAP_POINT_LINE (LOW #1) :
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘In a reaction, old particles disappear and brand new particles appear.
反应中,旧粒子消失,全新的粒子出现。
✔The same particles stay; they are only rearranged into new groupings. Nothing is created or destroyed — that is why the atoms can be counted before and after and the totals match.
同一批粒子还在;只是重新组合成新的组合。没有被创造或消灭——所以反应 前后原子能数得出来、总数对得上。
🧠 口诀金句 · MNEMONIC
Re-action = re-arrange.
反应=重排，不是魔法。

📌 SUMMARY · 总结
◆Summary.
◆A chemical reaction rearranges particles to make a new substance.
◆No particles are made or lost, so the number of atoms is the same before and after.
◆Particle models help us picture this, but every model leaves something out.
◆总结。
◆化学反应把粒子重新排列,做出新物质。
◆没有粒子被造出来或消失,所以反应前 后原子数目一样。
◆粒子模型帮我们想象这件事,但每个模型都漏掉一些东西。
