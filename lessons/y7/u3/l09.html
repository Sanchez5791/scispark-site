Introducing Acids and Alkalis

认识酸和碱
ACIDS, ALKALIS, NEUTRAL
•Substances can be acids or alkalis.
•- Acids: e.g.
•lemon juice, vinegar.
•- Alkalis: e.g.
•soap, baking soda solution.
•- Neutral: neither acid nor alkali, e.g.
•pure water.
•物质可以是酸或碱。
•- 酸：例如柠檬汁、醋。
•- 碱：例如肥皂、小苏打溶液。
•- 中性：既不是酸也不是碱，例如纯水。
THE pH SCALE (0-14)
•The pH scale goes from 0 to 14.
•- pH less than 7 = acid
•- pH equal to 7 = neutral
•- pH more than 7 = alkali The lower the pH, the stronger the acid.
•The higher the pH, the stronger the alkali.
•(Read the scale only — no calculation.)
•pH 尺从 0 到 14。
•- pH 小于 7 = 酸
•- pH 等于 7 = 中性
•- pH 大于 7 = 碱 pH 越低，酸性越强。
•pH 越高，碱性越强。
•（只读尺，不计算。
•）
INDICATORS — UNIVERSAL INDICATOR + LITMUS
•An indicator changes colour to show acid or alkali.
•- Universal indicator: a range of colours from pH 0 to 14 (red = strong acid, green = neutral, purple = strong alkali).
•- Litmus paper: red in acid, blue in alkali.
•指示剂用变色来显示酸或碱。
•- 万能指示剂：从 pH 0 到 14 的一系列颜色 （红 = 强酸，绿 = 中性，紫 = 强碱）。
•- 石蕊纸：在酸中变红，在碱中变蓝。
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘Alkalis are safe because only acids are dangerous.
碱是安全的，只有酸才危险。
✔Alkalis can be just as harmful as acids. Both strong acids and strong alkalis can damage skin and eyes.
碱可以和酸一样有害。强酸和强碱都能伤害皮肤和眼睛。
🧱 EXAM vs REALITY · 考试 vs 真实
Note · 注
•In the exam, write pH from 0 to 14.
•In real life, very strong acids can be below 0 and very strong alkalis above 14 — the 0-14 scale is a simplified range for everyday substances.
考试时写 pH 是 0 到 14；
真实里浓强酸可低于 0、浓强碱可高于 14， 0–14 只是日常物质的简化范围。
SAFETY BAR
•Even dilute acids and alkalis can irritate skin and eyes.
•Always wear safety goggles and a lab coat when using them.
•就算是稀的酸和碱也会刺激皮肤和眼睛。
•使用时一定要戴护目镜、穿实验服。
🧠 口诀金句 · MNEMONIC
Low pH = acid; high pH = alkali.
pH低是酸；pH高是碱。
