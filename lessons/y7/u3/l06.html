Word Equations

文字方程式
What a word equation is
•A WORD EQUATION uses the NAMES of the substances.
•reactant + reactant -> product The "+" means "and".
•The arrow "->" means "turns into" / "makes".
•We only use names.
•We do NOT use symbols or formulae.
•"文字方程式"用物质的"名字"来写。
•反应物 + 反应物 -> 产物 "+"代表"和"。
•箭头"->"代表"变成" / "生成"。
•我们只用名字。
•绝不用符号或化学式。
A worked example
•Magnesium burns in oxygen and makes magnesium oxide.
•As a word equation: magnesium + oxygen -> magnesium oxide Reactants: magnesium and oxygen (left of the arrow).
•Product: magnesium oxide (right of the arrow).
•镁在氧气中燃烧,生成氧化镁。
•写成文字方程式: 镁 + 氧气 -> 氧化镁 反应物:镁 和 氧气(箭头左边)。
•产物:氧化镁(箭头右边)。
•The arrow "->" is NOT an equals sign.
•It shows the direction of the change: reactants on the left turn into products on the right.
⛔ RED CARD · 常错红牌
✘the arrow means equals, so I can swap the two sides.
箭头就是等号,所以两边可以对调。
✔the arrow shows direction. Reactants always go on the LEFT and products always go on the RIGHT. You cannot swap them.
箭头表示方向。反应物永远在"左边",产物永远在"右边"。 不能对调。
🧱 EXAM vs REALITY · 考试 vs 真实
Note · 注
•In the exam, draw a one-way arrow (->) for reactions.
•In real life, most reactions go both ways — they reach a balance, not a complete stop.
考试时画单向箭头 (->)。
真实多数反应是双向的, 反应不会真的"完成",只是到平衡点。

📌 SUMMARY · 总结
◆A word equation is chemistry's recipe, written with names: reactants -> products.
◆The "+" means "and";
◆the arrow means "turns into".
◆Reactants always go on the left and products on the right.
◆In the exam we draw a one-way arrow, but in real life most reactions go both ways and reach a balance.
◆This links back to L04 (reactants and products) and L05 (mass is conserved — the same matter is just rearranged).
◆文字方程式是化学的食谱,用名字写:反应物 -> 产物。
◆"+"代表"和";箭头代表"变成"。
◆反应物永远在左边,产物在右边。
◆考试时画单向箭头,但真实多数反应是双向的、会到平衡点。
◆这接回 L04(反应物与产物)和 L05(质量守恒——同样的物质 只是重新组合)。
