Hook · Real-world spark
Turning seawater into drinking water
把海水变成能喝的水
DouDou · Turning seawater into drinking water
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
Separate, don't change
分开,不改变

Seawater is salt dissolved in water. To drink it, you must SEPARATE the salt from the water. 海水是盐溶在水里的混合物。要喝,就得把盐和水分开。

💡 Aside · 补充

Separating does not make a new substance — it only pulls the old substances apart. (It is science doing housework: "you go here, you go there, no fighting in the cup".) 分离不会制造新物质,只是把原本混在一起的东西拉开。 (像科学在做家务:「你去这边,你去那边,别全挤在杯子里。」)

Learn · Mechanism
Four physical methods
四种物理方法
Physical means
物理方法

Separation methods are PHYSICAL — they do not change what the substances are; they only move them apart. (Chemical change is NOT separation.) 分离方法是「物理方法」——不改变物质本身,只是把它们分开。(化学变化不是分离。)

Filtration 过滤 — separates an insoluble solid from a liquid. 把不溶的固体从液体里分出来。
Evaporation 蒸发 — gets a dissolved solid back from a solution. 把溶解了的固体从溶液里拿回来。
Distillation 蒸馏 — gets a pure liquid back from a solution. 把纯液体从溶液里取回来。
Using a magnet 用磁铁 — separates a magnetic solid from a non-magnetic one. 把有磁性的固体从没磁性的分出来。
🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✗ Wrong: "Filtration removes dissolved salt." 「过滤能滤掉溶解的盐。」

✓ Right: Dissolved salt passes straight through the filter paper with the water. Filtration only catches solids that did NOT dissolve. 溶解的盐会跟水一起穿过滤纸。过滤只挡住「没溶解」的固体。

🟡 Note · 注意

Distillation involves boiling — wear eye protection and keep hands away from the hot apparatus. 蒸馏要烧开液体——戴护目镜,手别碰烫的器材。

💡 Aside · 补充

F-E-D-M: Filter big bits; Evaporate solid back; Distill liquid back; Magnet pulls iron. Not dissolved → filter. Dissolved & want solid → evaporate. Want the liquid → distil. (Choosing a method is like choosing a tool: a net catches fish not salt; a magnet pulls iron not sand.) FEDM 记法:过滤挡大粒;蒸发拿固体;蒸馏拿液体;磁铁拉铁。 没溶→过滤。溶了但要固体→蒸发。要液体→蒸馏。 (选方法像选工具:网捞鱼不捞盐;磁铁吸铁不吸沙。)

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam: Distillation gives pure water from salt water. 考试写蒸馏可从盐水得到纯水。

Real science: Real distillation is not always 100% pure; close-boiling impurities can come over too. 真实蒸馏不一定百分百纯,沸点接近的杂质可能一起出来。
