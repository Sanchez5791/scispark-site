HOOK · Vacuum
真空
HOOK
•
In 1971, astronaut David Scott stood on the Moon.
1971 年，太空人 David Scott 站在月球上。
•
He held a hammer in one hand and a feather in the other, then let go of both at the same time.
一手拿锤子，一手拿羽毛，同时松手。
•
On Earth the feather drifts down slowly and the hammer lands first.
在地球上羽毛会慢慢飘、锤子先落地。
•
On the Moon, both hit the ground at the same time.
在月球上，两个同时落地。
•
Why?
为什么？
•
The Moon is almost a vacuum — there are no air particles to slow the feather down.
因为月球几乎是真空 —— 没有空气粒子拖住羽毛。
LEARN · Vacuum
真空
LEARN
•
A vacuum is a space that contains no particles.
真空 = 一个空间里完全没有粒子。
•
There is no air and no matter of any kind inside it.
里面没有空气，也没有任何物质。
•
L1 — teach only "what it is". Do NOT teach how a vacuum ismade and do NOT teach pressure formulas.
•
The Moon is almost a vacuum, so there are no air particles to slow the feather down.
月球几乎是真空，所以没有空气粒子拖住羽毛。
•
With no air to hold it back, the feather falls just like the hammer.
没有空气拖着，羽毛就和锤子一样落下。
•
L1 — teach only "no particles to slow it down". Do NOT teachgravity formulas or terminal velocity.
✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
•
In the exam write "a vacuum has no particles".
考试时写「真空 = 没有粒子」。
•
In real life even outer space has about 1 particle per cm3, so a perfect vacuum cannot really be made.
真实连外太空每 cm3 也有约 1 个粒子，完美真空做不出来。
•
Gas law PV=nRT -> Year 9.
LEARN_BOUNDARY (★ required):气体定律 PV=nRT -> Year 9。
•
Not taught in this lesson.
这课不教。

📝 SUMMARY · 总结
•
A vacuum is a space with no particles.
真空是没有粒子的空间。
•
With no air particles, there is no air resistance and no sound transmission.
没有空气粒子，就没有空气阻力，也不能传声。
•
A vacuum can reduce heat transfer, so it helps keep a drink warm.
真空能减少热传递，所以能帮饮料保温。
