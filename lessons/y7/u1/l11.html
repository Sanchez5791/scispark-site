Same substance, new state
同一物质,新状态

A substance can change state — solid, liquid, gas — without changing what it is made of. 一种物质能改变状态——固、液、气——但它是什么东西没有变。

Learn · Mechanism
Six changes, one rule
六种变化,一条规律
Heating (gain energy → move apart): melting (solid→liquid), evaporation/boiling (liquid→gas), sublimation (solid→gas). 加热(得能量→分开):熔化(固→液)、蒸发/沸腾(液→气)、升华(固→气)。
Cooling (lose energy → come together): freezing (liquid→solid), condensation (gas→liquid), deposition (gas→solid). 冷却(失能量→靠拢):凝固(液→固)、凝结(气→液)、凝华(气→固)。
Same particles
粒子没变

Changing state changes the particles' energy, motion and arrangement — not the particles themselves. 状态变化改变的是粒子的能量、运动和排列——不是粒子本身。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✗ Wrong: "When a substance melts or boils, its particles get bigger or change into something new." 「物质熔化或沸腾时,它的粒子会变大或变成新的东西。」

✓ Right: The particles stay exactly the same size and the same substance. Only their energy, spacing and movement change. 粒子大小完全不变,还是同一种物质。变的只是它们的能量、间距和运动。

🟡 Note · 注意

SAFETY — hot ice and boiling-water experiments must be supervised by a teacher; do not do them alone. NOTE — the exact energy to melt or boil can be calculated with a formula, studied in a later year (not here). 安全——热冰和沸水实验必须有老师看着;不要自己一个人做。 提醒——熔化或沸腾需要的确切能量可用公式算出,以后年级才学(这里不学)。

💡 Aside · 补充

Changing state is like rearranging the same furniture in a room — same furniture, new layout. A change of state changes spacing, motion and arrangement; it does not create a new substance. 状态变化像同一套家具重新摆位——东西没换,排法换了。它改变间距、运动和排列,不会变出新物质。

What you learned today 今天学到了什么

Heating gives energy → particles move apart (melt, evaporate, boil, sublime). 加热给能量→粒子分开(熔化、蒸发、沸腾、升华)。
Cooling takes energy → particles come together (condense, freeze, deposit). 冷却拿走能量→粒子靠拢(凝结、凝固、凝华)。
The particles themselves never change; only energy, spacing and movement do. 粒子本身从不改变;变的只是能量、间距和运动。
