HOOK · Atoms, Elements (Symbols)
原子、元素（符号）
HOOK
•
There are only about 90 natural building blocks of all matter, called atoms.
全宇宙所有物质,只用大约 90 种基本积木,叫原子。
•
Just like 26 letters can spell every word in the English language, around 90 kinds of atom build every substance in the universe.
就像 26 个字母能拼出所有英文单词,大约 90 种原子拼出宇宙里每一种物质。
•
Look at any coin, any cup of water, the air you breathe — all built from the same ~90 kinds of atom.
看看任何一枚硬币、任何一杯水、你呼吸的空气 —全部由同样这约 90 种原子拼成。
•
Antoine Lavoisier (France, 1789) helped create the modern idea of a chemical element.
CAREER ANCHOR (Around the World scientist):Antoine Lavoisier(法国,1789)帮忙建立了现代「元素」的概念。

原子、元素（符号）
LEARN
An atom is the smallest particle of an element
•
An atom is the smallest particle of an element.
原子是一种元素最小的粒子。
•
If you keep cutting a piece of pure copper smaller and smaller, the smallest piece that is still copper is one copper atom.
把一块纯铜一直切、一直切,切到最小、还是铜的那一粒,就是一个铜原子。

Every element has one chemical symbol
•
Each element has its own chemical symbol of one or two letters, e.g.
每种元素都有自己的化学符号,1 到 2 个字母,例如 H 是氢、He 是氦、Na 是钠、O 是氧。
•
H for hydrogen, He for helium, Na for sodium, O for oxygen.
Symbol case rule
•
First letter is a capital, second letter (if any) is small.
第一个字母大写,第二个字母(如果有)小写。
•
Co means cobalt; CO means carbon monoxide.
Co 是钴;CO 是一氧化碳。
•
They are not the same.
两个不一样。
🔴 RED CARD · 误区红牌
✘
Writing the symbol case any way you like, e.g. AL, AR, NA, CL.
✔
Aluminium = Al; argon = Ar; sodium = Na; chlorine = Cl. WHY IT wrong case can change the meaning (Co vs CO) and loses the mark. ZH 错: 大小写乱写,例如 AL、AR、NA、CL。 ZH 对: 铝 Al;氩 Ar;钠 Na;氯 Cl。 ZH 为何重要: 大小写写错会改变意思(Co 和 CO),而且不给分。
铝 Al;氩 Ar;钠 Na;氯 Cl。 ZH 为何重要: 大小写写错会改变意思(Co 和 CO),而且不给分。
Chemical formula = symbols + numbers
•
A chemical formula uses symbols and small numbers to show how many of each atom are in one unit.
化学式用符号加小数字,表示一个单位里有几个什么原子。
•
In H2O there are 2 hydrogen atoms and 1 oxygen atom.
H2O 里有 2 个氢原子、1 个氧原子。
•
This lesson only counts atoms.
这课只「数原子」。

"考试时画:原子中间有一个核。
•
In real life, atoms are almost completely empty — if an atom were a football field, the nucleus would be a grain of rice."
真实情况:原子 99% 是空的。
•
如果原子是足球场,原子核只是一粒米。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
•
"In the exam: write the symbol with the first letter capital and the second small — get it wrong and you lose the mark (Co = cobalt, CO = carbon monoxide).
"考试时:符号第一个字母大写,第二个小写,写错不给分(Co 是钴,CO 是一氧化碳)。

In real life: this rule is an agreement between scientists, not a law of nature.
真实情况:这套写法是科学家的'约定',不是自然法则。
•
Nature never said cobalt must be written 'Co' — people chose it so the whole world wouldn't mix things up."
大自然没规定钴要写 Co,是人为了全世界不搞混才定的。
