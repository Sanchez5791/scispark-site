Hook · Real-world spark
Atoms are the alphabet of matter
原子是物质的字母表
DouDou · Atoms are the alphabet of matter
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
Atoms = alphabet
原子 = 字母表

About 90 natural atom types build all natural matter — like letters make words. 大约 90 种天然原子拼出所有天然物质——像字母拼单词。

💡 Aside · 补充

Symbol rule: Big then small — Na, not NA. An element is one type of atom; a symbol is its short name. (Atoms are tiny building blocks, but they do not come with a LEGO booklet.) 符号规则:第一个大、第二个小 — Na,不是 NA。element 是一种原子;symbol 是它的短名字。 (原子像超小积木,但不附 LEGO 说明书。)

🟢 Try it · 动手做

Any coin, cup or air around you is built from atoms. 身边的硬币、杯子、空气,都由原子组成。

Learn · Mechanism
Atom, element, symbol, formula
原子、元素、符号、化学式
1
An atom is the smallest particle of an element. 原子是一种元素最小的粒子。
2
Every element has one chemical symbol. 每种元素有一个化学符号。
3
Symbol case matters. 符号大小写很重要。
4
A chemical formula uses symbols and numbers to count atoms. 化学式用符号和数字数原子。
Symbol 符号 — first letter capital, second (if any) small: H, He, Na, O. 符号 — 第一个字母大写,第二个(如果有)小写:H、He、Na、O。
Formula 化学式 — letters = which element; numbers = how many. H2O = 2 H + 1 O. 化学式 — 字母说「哪种元素」,数字说「几个」。H2O = 2 个 H + 1 个 O。
💡 Aside · 补充

A formula is like a shopping receipt: H2O says "buy 2 H and 1 O". (CO and Co are not twins: one is carbon monoxide, one is cobalt.) One capital, one small, numbers count atoms. 化学式像购物单:H2O 写着「2 个 H,1 个 O」。 (CO 和 Co 不是双胞胎:一个是一氧化碳,一个是钴。)一大一小,数字数原子。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam: In the exam, draw the atom with a small nucleus in the middle. 考试画:原子中间有一个小原子核。

Real science: Real atoms are mostly empty space; the nucleus is tiny compared with the atom. 真实原子大部分是空的;原子核比整个原子小很多。 框B(REF #30)

In the exam: In the exam, symbol case is fixed: Co and CO are different. 考试时,符号大小写固定:Co 和 CO 不一样。

Real science: Symbol case is a scientist agreement, not a law of nature. 符号大小写是科学家的约定,不是自然法则。
