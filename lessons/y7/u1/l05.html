Every element has its place
每种元素都有位置

About 90 elements is a lot. Scientists order them in one big chart — the Periodic Table. 大约 90 种元素很难全记。科学家把它们排进一张大表——周期表。

💡 Aside · 补充

Dmitri Mendeleev (Russia) arranged the first widely used Periodic Table; chemists still use it to pick the right element. Today you only learn how to READ the table, not memorise every box. (It is like the class seating plan — nobody wants 90 students at the door.) Dmitri Mendeleev(俄国)排出第一张被广泛使用的周期表;化学家至今用它挑合适的元素。 今天只学「怎么看懂这张表」,不是背完整张表。 (它像班级座位表——90 个学生全站门口可不行。)

Learn · Mechanism
How to read the table
怎么看周期表
1
The Periodic Table lists all known elements in order. 周期表把所有已知元素按顺序排好。
2
Each box shows one element's chemical symbol. 每个格子放一种元素的化学符号。
3
Metals are on the left/middle; non-metals on the right. 金属在左/中,非金属在右。
4
Groups (columns) and periods (rows) give each element a place. group(列)和 period(行)给每种元素一个位置。
Metals 金属 — left/middle; conduct heat & electricity; malleable and ductile. 金属 — 左/中;导电、导热;可锤打、可拉成丝。
Non-metals 非金属 — on the right of the table. 非金属 — 在表的右边。
🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✗ Wrong: "Sodium does not conduct heat" / treating a metal as having non-metal properties. 「钠不导热」/ 把金属当成有非金属性质。

✓ Right: Metals (e.g. sodium) conduct heat AND electricity, and are ductile and malleable. 金属(例如钠)既导电又导热,可拉成丝、可锤打。

Picking the wrong property loses the mark. 选错性质就不给分。

Column = Group, Row = Period
列 = group,行 = period

A column is a group; a row is a period. Sodium is in Group 1. (This lesson only reads positions & symbols.) 一列叫 group,一行叫 period。钠在 Group 1。(这课只看位置和符号,不计算。)

💡 Aside · 补充

Think of it as an apartment block: group = column address, period = floor/row. (But position is set by atomic number & properties, not by people.) 像一栋公寓:group 是列地址,period 是行地址。(但位置由 atomic number 和性质决定,不是人。)

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam: Split elements into metals and non-metals. 考试先分金属和非金属。

Real science: Some elements are metalloids, like silicon; they act a bit like both. 真实中间还有类金属,例如硅,性质有点像两边。
