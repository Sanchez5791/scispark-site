答案都一样:粒子之间互相拉的力有多强。

LEARN · Particle Attraction (3 States)
粒子吸引力（三态）
LEARN
Solids
•
In a solid, the force of attraction between particles is the strongest.
固体里,粒子之间的吸引力最强。
•
This strong attraction locks each particle into a fixed position.
这股强吸引力把每个粒子锁在固定位置。
•
Particles can only vibrate on the spot; they cannot move past each other.
粒子只能原地振动,不能彼此越过。
•
Result: a solid has a fixed shape and a fixed volume.
结果:固体有固定形状、固定体积。
Liquids
•
In a liquid, the force of attraction between particles is medium.
液体里,粒子之间的吸引力中等。
•
The attraction is strong enough to hold particles close together, but weak enough to let them slide past one another.
这股力够强,能把粒子拉在一起;又够弱,能让粒子互相滑动。
•
Result: a liquid has no fixed shape (it takes the shape of its container) but it has a fixed volume.
结果:液体没有固定形状(随容器变),但有固定体积。
Gases
•
In a gas, the force of attraction between particles is the weakest — almost none.
气体里,粒子之间的吸引力最弱——几乎没有。
•
Particles move freely in all directions and are far apart.
粒子向各个方向自由运动,彼此离得很远。
•
Result: a gas has no fixed shape and no fixed volume; it spreads out and fills its whole container.
结果:气体没有固定形状、也没有固定体积;会散开填满整个容器。
🔴 RED CARD · 误区红牌
✘
Gas particles have no force between them at all, so they can never be turned into a liquid.
气体粒子之间完全没有力,所以永远不能变成液体。
✔
Gas particles still have a very weak attraction. If they are cooled enough, this weak attraction can pull them back together into a liquid.
气体粒子之间还有一点点很弱的吸引力。冷却到足够低时, 这点弱吸引力能把粒子重新拉回成液体。
✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
•
In the exam, draw solid particles touching in a regular pattern.
考试时画:粒子紧挨着,排列规则。
•
In real life, there are tiny gaps between them held by forces — that's why solids can be squeezed a tiny bit.
真实情况:粒子之间其实有很小的缝隙,靠吸引力拉在一起。

Q04Explain4 marks
A liquid takes the shape of its container but keeps the same volume. Use the idea of attraction between particles to explain why a liquid has no fixed shape but a fixed volume. [2]
Expected answer · 标准答案
Liquid particles are attracted enough to stay close, so the volume is fixed. The attraction is not strong enough to hold particles in fixed positions, so particles can slide and the liquid has no fixed shape.
液体粒子之间的吸引力足够让粒子保持靠近，所以体积固定。这个吸引力又不够强到把粒子固定在位置上，所以粒子能滑动，液体没有固定形状。
Accept · 可收：
  • attraction keeps liquid particles close
  • particles stay close together
  • fixed volume because particles stay close
  • attraction is weaker than in a solid
  • particles are not fixed in position
  • particles can slide past each other
  • no fixed shape because particles can move around
Q05Explain6 marks
A gas is cooled until it turns into a liquid. Use the idea of attraction between particles to explain why the gas can change into a liquid when it is cooled. [3]
Expected answer · 标准答案
Cooling removes energy so gas particles slow down. They can no longer overcome the attraction easily. The attraction pulls particles closer together, forming a liquid.
冷却会拿走能量，所以气体粒子变慢。它们不再容易克服吸引力。吸引力把粒子拉近，形成液体。
Accept · 可收：
  • cooling removes energy
  • particles lose energy
  • particles slow down
  • particles move more slowly
  • particles cannot overcome attraction as easily
  • attraction pulls particles closer
  • particles come closer together
  • gas condenses into liquid

Q10Explain6 marks
A solid is heated strongly until it melts into a liquid. Use the idea of attraction between particles to explain why heating can turn a solid into a liquid. [3]
Expected answer · 标准答案
Heating gives particles more energy so they move faster. They partly overcome the strong attraction holding them in fixed positions. They leave fixed positions and slide past each other, so the solid melts into a liquid.
加热给粒子更多能量，所以粒子动得更快。粒子部分克服把它们固定住的强吸引力。它们离开固定位置并能互相滑过，所以固体融化成液体。
Accept · 可收：
  • heating gives particles more energy
  • particles gain energy
  • particles move faster
  • particles partly overcome strong attraction
  • particles break out of fixed positions
  • particles can slide past each other
  • solid melts into a liquid
