Strength of attraction
吸引力的强弱

Why is a rock hard, water flows, gas spreads everywhere? It comes down to how strongly the particles pull on each other. 石头为什么硬?水为什么流?气体为什么到处跑?关键是粒子之间互相拉的力有多强。

Learn · Mechanism
Strong, medium, weak
强、中、弱
Solid 固体 — strongest attraction; particles locked in place, only vibrate → fixed shape & volume. 吸引力最强;粒子被锁在固定位置,只振动 → 固定形状、固定体积。
Liquid 液体 — medium attraction; holds particles close but lets them slide → no fixed shape, fixed volume. 吸引力中等;把粒子拉在一起,又让它们滑动 → 没固定形状,有固定体积。
Gas 气体 — weakest, almost none; particles free, far apart → no fixed shape or volume; fills container. 吸引力最弱、几乎没有;粒子自由、离得远 → 没固定形状也没固定体积;填满容器。
🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✗ Wrong: "Gas particles have no force between them at all, so they can never be turned into a liquid." 「气体粒子之间完全没有力,所以永远不能变成液体。」

✓ Right: Gas particles still have a very weak attraction. If cooled enough, this weak attraction can pull them back into a liquid. 气体粒子之间还有一点点很弱的吸引力。冷却到足够低时,这点弱吸引力能把它们重新拉回成液体。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam: In the exam, draw solid particles touching in a regular pattern. 考试画:粒子紧挨着,排列规则。

Real science: In real life there are tiny gaps between them held by forces — that's why solids can be squeezed a tiny bit. 真实粒子之间其实有很小的缝隙,靠吸引力拉住——所以固体能被压缩一点点。

REF: SCISPARK_EXAM_VS_REAL_SCIENCE_REFERENCE #1

💡 Aside · 补充

Strong = solid, Medium = liquid, Weak = gas (S-M-W → S-L-G). Attraction means how strongly particles pull on each other: more pull = harder to move apart. (Like small magnets between particles — but it is not real magnetism, just a model.) 强=固,中=液,弱=气(强中弱→固液气)。吸引力就是粒子互相拉得多紧:拉得越强,越难分开。 (像粒子之间的小磁铁——但不是真磁铁,只是帮助理解的模型。)
