LEARN · What is Matter?
什么是物质
LEARN
•
Matter is anything that has TWO things at the same time:
Matter(物质)是同时有这两样东西的东西:
•
It has MASS (the amount of stuff inside it).
有 MASS(里面东西的量)。
•
It takes up SPACE (it has volume).
占 SPACE / 空间(有 volume / 体积)。
•
Both are needed.
两样都要。
•
If something is missing one of these, it is NOT matter.
少一样,就不是 matter。
•
A SUBSTANCE is what something is made of (e.g.
SUBSTANCE(物质材料)是某样东西由什么做成(例:水、铁)。
•
water, iron).
OBJECT(物体)是用 substance 做出来的东西(例:杯子、钉子)。
•
An OBJECT is a thing made from substance (e.g.
错的想法:「看得见就是 matter。
•
a cup, a nail).
」对的:看得见不是判断标准。
•
Wrong idea: "If you can see it, it is matter." Correct: Seeing it is not the test.
光看得见,但不是 matter(光没有 mass,也不占空间)。
•
Light can be seen but is NOT matter (light has no mass and takes up no space).
判断标准永远是:有 mass + 占空间。
•
The test is always: mass AND space.
•
We measure mass in kilograms (kg).
我们用 kilogram(kg / 千克)量 mass。
•
Mass tells you how much stuff is inside something.
Mass 告诉你某样东西里面有多少东西。
•
Side note — mass vs weight
旁注 — mass vs weight
•
In the exam: write "mass = the amount of matter, in kg".
考试时:写 "mass = matter 的量,单位 kg"。
•
In real life: mass and weight are different.
真实情况:mass 和 weight 不一样。
•
Mass is how much stuff is inside (never changes); weight is how hard gravity pulls (changes — on the Moon you weigh less, but your mass is the same).
mass 是里面东西多少(不变);weight 是地球拉它的力(会变 —— 月球上比较轻,但 mass 一样)。

📝 SUMMARY · 总结
•
Matter = has mass AND takes up space (both needed).
Matter = 有 mass + 占空间(两样都要)。
•
Substance = what it is made of; object = a thing made from it.
Substance = 由什么做成;object = 用它做出来的东西。
•
Seeing something is not the test.
看得见不是判断标准。
•
Light is not matter.
光不是 matter。
•
Mass = amount of stuff (does not change with shape or place).
Mass = 东西的量(换形状、换地方都不变)。
•
You think water is one lump — really it is made of particles.
