Hook · Real-world spark
How small can matter get?
物质可以小到多小?
DouDou · How small can matter get?
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
Too small to see ≠ not real
小到看不见 ≠ 不存在

A drop of water looks smooth, but it is made of huge numbers of tiny particles. 一滴水看起来很滑顺,其实由非常多 tiny particles(超小粒子)组成。

💡 Aside · 补充

Eye → Microscope → Mind: big things, small things, particles. (A particle is so small even a microscope says "too tiny for me".) 记法:眼睛 → 显微镜 → 脑想象:大东西、小东西、粒子。 (particle 小到显微镜都说「这个太小了」。)

🟢 Try it · 动手做

Look at one drop of water. It looks smooth, but it is not one piece. 看一滴水。它看起来很滑顺,但不是一整块。

Learn · Mechanism
The size ladder
大小楼梯
1
Big things — planet, person, grain of salt. 大东西——行星、人、盐粒。
2
Smaller things — cells, tiny structures. 更小——细胞、小结构。
3
Tiny science pieces — particles, atoms, molecules. 超小科学小块——particle、atom、molecule。
4
For the tiniest ones, we use models and imagination. 最小那些,用 model 和想象。
💡 Aside · 补充

Size is like zooming a map: country → city → street → one tiny house. (Real particles are far smaller than houses; the map only shows scale.) 大小像地图放大:国家 → 城市 → 街道 → 一间小屋。 (真实粒子比房子小得多,地图只是帮你看比例。)

Molecule 分子 — made from atoms joined together. Molecule 分子 — 由 atoms 连起来组成。
Particle 粒子 — the tiny piece we use in the model. Particle 粒子 — 模型里说的超小一粒。
