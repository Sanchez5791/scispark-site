LEARN · Unit Review and Integration
单元复习与整合
LEARN
•
Everything in this unit branches out from one core idea: the particle model.
这单元的一切都从一个核心概念分支出来：粒子模型。
•
Matter is made of particles.
物质由粒子组成。
•
Atoms and elements name the particles.
原子和元素给粒子命名。
•
Elements, compounds and mixtures describe how particles are combined.
元素/化合物/混合物描述粒子怎么组合。
•
The three states (solid, liquid, gas) describe how particles are arranged, move and attract.
三态（固/液/气）描述粒子怎么排列、运动、吸引。
•
State changes are particles rearranging.
状态变化就是粒子重新排列。
•
A vacuum is what is left when there are no particles.
真空就是没有粒子时剩下的东西。
🔴 RED CARD · 误区红牌
✘
The space between particles is filled with air.
粒子之间填满了空气。
✔
The space between particles is empty — a vacuum. Air is itself made of particles, so it cannot sit in the gaps between other particles.
粒子之间是空的——是真空。空气本身也是粒子做的，不能塞在 别的粒子之间的空隙里。 This particle model is the base of the whole SciSpark system. Every later unit — materials, chemical changes, forces, sound, cycles — uses it. 粒子模型是整个 SciSpark 系统的基础。后面每个 unit ——材料、化学变化、力、声音、循环——都会用到它。

📝 SUMMARY · 总结
•
Matter is made of tiny particles.
物质由很小的粒子组成。
•
Elements, compounds and mixtures describe what particles are made of and how they are combined.
元素、化合物和混合物说明粒子由什么组成，以及怎样组合。
•
Solids, liquids and gases differ in particle arrangement, motion and attraction.
固体、液体、气体的粒子排列、运动和吸引力不同。
•
A vacuum is a space with no particles.
真空是没有粒子的空间。
