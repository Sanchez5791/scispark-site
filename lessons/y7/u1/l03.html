Hook · Real-world spark
Why use a particle model?
为什么要用粒子模型?
DouDou · Why use a particle model?
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
See the unseen
看见「看不见」的

Particles are too small to see; scientists use models to picture them. 粒子太小,不能直接看见;科学家用 model 想象看不见的东西。

💡 Aside · 补充

Model = See the Unseen. A model is a useful simple picture, not a perfect copy. (A toy car cannot drive you to school.) 记法:Model = 帮你看见「看不见」的东西。model 是有用的简单图,不是完美复制品。 (玩具车不能载你去学校。)

🟡 Note · 注意

If hot water is used in a class demo, use heat-proof gloves. 如果课堂示范用热水,要用防热手套。

Learn · Mechanism
Strengths and limits
优点和限制
1
Particles are too small to see. 粒子太小,看不见。
2
A model helps us picture them. model 帮我们想象它们。
3
A model has strengths. model 有优点。
4
A model has limitations. model 有限制。
5
Evidence decides whether a model is useful. evidence(证据)决定 model 有没有用。
Strength 优点 — it makes invisible particles easier to picture. 优点 — 把看不见的粒子变得容易想象。
Limit 限制 — it cannot show every real detail. 限制 — 不能显示所有真实细节。
Evidence decides
证据说了算

Evidence means facts from observation or experiments — it decides if a model is useful. evidence 就是观察或实验来的事实——它决定 model 有没有用。

💡 Aside · 补充

A model is like a map: it helps you find the road, but it is not the real city. model 像地图:帮你找路,但它不是真正的城市。
