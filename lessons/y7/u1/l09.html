DouDou · A smell crossing the room
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
Particles are moving
粒子在动

When food cooks on one side, the smell travels across on its own — the gas particles are moving, fast and in all directions. 一边在煮东西,味道自己跑到另一头——带味道的气体粒子在动,又快又乱。

💡 Aside · 补充

Motion means "how particles move", not "where they are arranged". Solid = shake; Liquid = slide; Gas = go everywhere. (Gas particles are like students after the bell — everyone goes everywhere at once.) 运动是说粒子怎么动,不是说粒子排在哪。固体 shake、液体 slide、气体 everywhere(摇、滑、飞)。 (气体粒子像下课铃响后的学生——一秒四散。)

Learn · Mechanism
How particles move
粒子怎么动
Arrangement vs Motion
排列 vs 运动

L08 was where particles SIT (arrangement). This lesson is whether and how they MOVE. L08 说粒子「站在哪」(排列)。这课说「动不动、怎么动」。

Solid 固体 — particles only vibrate on the spot; they do not leave their place. 粒子只在原地振动,不离开位置。
Liquid 液体 — particles slide past each other but stay close together. 粒子能滑动、换位置,但仍挨在一起。
Gas 气体 — particles move fast and randomly in all directions, far apart. 粒子快速、随机、四面八方地飞,相距远。
Heat = faster
加热 = 更快

When a substance is heated, its particles gain energy and move faster. 加热时,粒子获得能量、动得更快。

Solid 固体 — cannot flow; particles are fixed and only vibrate. 不能流;粒子被固定,只振动。
Liquid 液体 — flows and takes the container's shape; particles change places. 能流、取容器形状;粒子换位置。
Gas 气体 — flows and fills the container; particles move everywhere. 能流、填满容器;粒子到处动。
🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✗ Wrong: "Solid particles do not move at all." 「固体粒子完全不动。」

✓ Right: Solid particles still move — they vibrate in place. They just do not travel from their fixed position. 固体粒子还是动的——在原地振动,只是不离开固定位置。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam: Draw solid particles touching in a regular pattern. 考试画法:固体粒子紧挨着,排列规则。

Real science: Real particles have tiny gaps held by attraction forces; heating means particles move faster. 真实粒子之间有极小缝隙,由吸引力拉住;加热表示粒子动得更快。
