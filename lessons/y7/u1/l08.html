Hook · Real-world spark
Same water, three forms
同一个东西,三种样子
DouDou · Same water, three forms
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
Same particles — what changed?
粒子一样——变的是什么?

Ice, liquid water and steam are all water, yet they behave differently. What changed is how the particles are arranged. 冰、水、水蒸气都是水,却表现不同。变的是粒子怎么「排」。

💡 Aside · 补充

State changes are not about particles becoming bigger — they are about spacing and arrangement. (Same water, three personalities: ice is strict, liquid is relaxed, steam wants space.) 三态变化不是粒子变大,真正变的是粒子间距和排列。 (同样是水,三种性格:冰守规矩、水随和、水蒸气天天要空间。)

Learn · Mechanism
Three arrangements
三种排列
Solid 固体 — particles close, regular, fixed pattern. 粒子紧挨,排列规则、固定。
Liquid 液体 — particles close but irregular, not fixed. 粒子也挨近,但排列不规则、不固定。
Gas 气体 — particles far apart, spread out, no pattern. 粒子相距远、散开、没有排列。
Solid 固体 — fixed shape (locked in place); hard to compress (tiny gaps). 固体 — 形状固定(粒子被锁住);难压缩(间隙几乎没有)。
Gas 气体 — fills its container; easy to compress (large gaps). 气体 — 填满容器;易压缩(间隙大)。
🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✗ Wrong: "Gas particles are bigger or lighter than solid particles." 「气体粒子比固体粒子大/轻。」

✓ Right: The particles are the same; only the SPACING and arrangement differ between states. 粒子是同一种,变的只是「间距和排列」。

🟡 Note · 注意

Matter is usually taught as three states, but there is a fourth — plasma (lightning, the sun, neon lights). (Mention only — not assessed at Y7.) 常说三态,其实还有第四态 plasma(闪电/太阳/霓虹灯)。(只提一句,Y7 不考。)

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam: Draw solid particles touching in a regular pattern. 考试画固体粒子紧挨、排列规则。

Real science: Real particles have tiny gaps held by attraction forces; the touching picture is a simplification. 真实粒子间有很小缝隙,靠吸引力拉住;紧贴图是简化。 框B(REF #2)

In the exam: Learn three states: solid, liquid and gas. 考试先学三态:固体、液体、气体。

Real science: Real matter also includes plasma, such as lightning, the sun and neon signs. 真实还有 plasma,例如闪电、太阳、霓虹灯。

💡 Aside · 补充

Solid = Set; Liquid = Loose; Gas = Gone everywhere. Compress means "push particles closer": gas compresses (big gaps), solid hardly does (tiny gaps). (Solid = students in fixed seats; liquid = moving in a small room; gas = spread across the playground. But particles move from energy & forces, not choice.) 固挨齐,液挨乱,气分散。压缩=「把粒子推更近」:气体能压(空隙大),固体难压(空隙小)。 (固体像坐固定座位;液体像在小房间走动;气体像散到操场。但粒子靠能量和力,不是自己选。)

What you learned today 今天学到了什么

Solid = regular, close-packed, fixed. 固体=规则、紧密、固定。
Liquid = close but irregular. 液体=挨近但不规则。
Gas = far apart, spread out. Arrangement explains shape & compressibility. 气体=松散分开。排列解释形状和能否压缩。
💡 Aside · 补充

Solid: close + regular. Liquid: close + irregular. Gas: far apart + random. 固体:近+整齐。液体:近+乱。气体:远+乱。
