Hook · Real-world spark
Why does the line go flat?
为什么线会变平?
DouDou · Why does the line go flat?
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
Temperature vs time
温度对时间

Measure a cooling drink every minute and plot it — you draw a cooling curve. Why does the line go flat at certain points even while you keep heating or cooling? 每分钟量一次冷却的饮料并画出来,就是冷却曲线。为什么一直加热/冷却,线却在某些点变平?

Learn · Mechanism
Reading the curve
看曲线
Sloping section 斜段 — the temperature is changing. 斜的一段——温度在变化。
Flat section / plateau 平段(平台) — temperature stays the same even though heating/cooling continues. 平的一段(平台)——就算还在加热或冷却,温度也保持不变。
Plateau = changing state
平台 = 正在变状态

During a plateau, energy is used to overcome forces between particles, not to raise temperature. Melting point = solid→liquid plateau; boiling point = liquid→gas plateau. 平台段时,能量用来克服粒子之间的力,不是升温。熔点=固→液的平台;沸点=液→气的平台。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✗ Wrong: "If the line is flat, no energy is being added and nothing is happening." 「如果线是平的,就表示没有加能量、什么都没发生。」

✓ Right: Energy is still being added during a plateau. It is being used to change the state, not to raise the temperature. 平台段时仍然在加能量。这些能量用来改变物质的状态,而不是用来升高温度。

🟡 Note · 注意

SAFETY — heating and boiling-water experiments must be supervised; wear eye protection, keep hands away from hot apparatus. NOTE — the exact energy during a plateau can be calculated with a formula, studied in a later year. Here we only read and describe the curve. 安全——加热和沸水实验必须有老师看着;戴护目镜,手远离烫的仪器。 提醒——平台段的确切能量可用公式算出,以后年级才学。这一课只读图和描述曲线。

💡 Aside · 补充

Slope = temperature changes. Flat = state changes. A flat section does not mean nothing happens — the substance is changing state while temperature stays the same. (A heating curve is like a progress bar: slopes = temperature progress; flat parts = energy spent changing state.) 斜线=温度变;平线=状态变。平的线不是什么都没发生——物质在变状态,温度暂时不变。 (加热曲线像进度条:斜段是温度在变;平段是能量拿去改变状态。)
