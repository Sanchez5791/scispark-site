Learn · Concept · 概念
7 Energy Stores
💬 Plain talk · 大白话

这屏只抓一个重点，先会认，再会写。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

energy store 像抽屉: 同一个房间可以有很多抽屉, 同一个物体也可以同时有很多能量储存。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

7 stores 顺口念: 动、重、化、热、弹、电、磁。

Energy is stored in 7 ways. The 7 stores are: kinetic, gravitational (potential), chemical, thermal, elastic (potential), electric, magnetic. 能量有 7 种储存方式。7 种储存是: 动能、重力(势能)、化学、热、弹性(势能)、电、磁。

"Students think 'energy types' and 'energy stores' are different lists, or that an object can only ever hold one store." "学生以为'能量种类'和'能量储存'是两份 不同的清单, 或以为一个物体只能有一种储存。"

A renewable energy engineer turns one store into a more useful one: sunlight (light) into electric; moving water (kinetic) into electric; wind (kinetic) into electric. 可再生能源工程师把一种储存变成更有用的: 阳光(光)变成电; 流水(动能)变成电; 风(动能)变成电。

What you learned today 今天学到了什么

There are 7 energy stores: kinetic, gravitational, chemical, thermal, elastic, electric, magnetic. One object can hold more than one store. Useful energy is what a device gives out. 有 7 种能量储存: 动能、重力、化学、热、弹性、电、磁。 一个物体可以有不止一种储存。有用能量是装置放出来的那种。

Renewable energy engineers identify energy stores and design machines that convert them — solar (light→electric), wind and hydro (kinetic→electric). 可再生能源工程师识别能量储存, 设计机器把它们转换 — 太阳能(光→电)、风能和水力(动能→电)。
