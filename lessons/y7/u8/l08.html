🌞 Warm-up · 热身

今天不是新魔王, 是打总复习 boss。前面每课都是你捡到的装备。

💬 Plain talk · 大白话

先认能量, 再说 from-to, 再解释声音怎么靠粒子振动传。

You have finished 8 lessons. Look at one ordinary morning: the sun warms your room, your phone battery powers an alarm, the alarm makes a sound, you hear it. Every step is energy. 你已经上完 8 节课。看一个普通的早上: 太阳晒暖房间,手机电池让闹钟响,闹钟发出声音,你听见它。 每一步都是能量。

This unit was one chain: Energy -> 7 Energy Stores -> Particle Vibration -> Sound -> Echo -> Dissipation. 这个单元是一条链: 能量 -> 7 种能量储存 -> 粒子振动 -> 声音 -> 回声 -> 耗散。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Unit Review and Integration
💬 Plain talk · 大白话

这屏只抓一个重点，先会认，再会写。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

复习像把 8 张拼图拼成一张图: 能量线和声音线最后接在一起。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

整单元一条链: 能量变 → 会散开；物体振 → 声传开；碰墙 → 回声；没粒子 → 没声音。

Energy is in every event. It is held in 7 stores: kinetic, gravitational potential, chemical, thermal, elastic, electric, magnetic. 每个事件里都有能量。它存放在 7 种储存里: 动能、重力位能、化学能、热能、 弹性能、电能、磁能。

Events change energy from one store to another. Example: a ball at the top has gravitational potential; as it falls, this changes to kinetic. 事件让能量从一种储存变成另一种。 例子:球在顶端有重力位能; 它落下时,变成动能。

RED CARD: sound is NOT particles flying from the source to your ear. Particles vibrate in place and pass the vibration to the next particle. The vibration travels; the particles do NOT travel. 红牌:声音不是粒子从声源飞到你的耳朵。 粒子在原地振动,把振动传给隔壁的粒子。 传过去的是振动;粒子本身不移动。

Sound needs particles (a medium). In a vacuum there are no particles, so there is no sound. Sound can reflect off a hard surface and come back as an echo. 声音需要粒子(介质)。真空里没有粒子, 所以没有声音。声音碰到硬表面会反射回来,变成回声。

Energy is never destroyed. It spreads out to the surroundings (dissipation). Once spread out, it is too thin to use again. This is irreversible. 能量永远不会被消灭。它分散到周围(耗散)。 一旦分散开,就太稀薄,不能再用。这是不可逆的。

Next years go deeper. You will meet formulas: KE = 1/2 m v^2, GPE = m g h, and the wave rule v = f lambda. We only show them today — you will learn them later. 之后的年级会更深入。你会遇到公式: KE = 1/2 m v^2、GPE = m g h,还有波的规律 v = f lambda。 今天只是给你看一眼 —— 以后才学。
