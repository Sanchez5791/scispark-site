🌞 Warm-up · 热身

太空电影里爆炸“轰”一声很爽, 但科学老师会在旁边冷冷说: 真空里没人帮你传声。

💬 Plain talk · 大白话

compression = 粒子挤近。rarefaction = 粒子分开。amplitude 越大, 声音越响。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

声音像一排人传波浪动作: 动作传过去, 人还站在原位。真空没人排队, 所以传不了。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

有粒子才有声: no particles, no pass, no sound。

When you hum against a cup, the cup vibrates. That vibration is passed to the particles around it. That is how sound travels. 对着杯子哼歌,杯子会振动。振动传给旁边的粒子。声音就是这样传的。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Sound = Particle Vibration Traveling
💬 Plain talk · 大白话

这屏只抓一个重点，先会认，再会写。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

声音像一排人传波浪动作: 动作传过去, 人还站在原位。真空没人排队, 所以传不了。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

有粒子才有声: no particles, no pass, no sound。

Sound is a type of energy. Sound is made when particles vibrate. The vibration is passed from one particle to the next. 声音是一种能量。粒子振动时就产生声音。振动一个粒子传给下一个粒子。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✘ Wrong 错:

✔ Right 对: Particles do NOT fly across from the source to your ear. Each particle vibrates IN PLACE. Only the vibration (the energy) travels — the particles stay where they are. 粒子不会从声源飞到耳朵。每个粒子在原地振动。传过去的是 振动(能量),不是粒子本身。

Where particles are close together = a compression. Where they are far apart = a rarefaction. Sound travels through solids, liquids and gases. It cannot travel through a vacuum, because a vacuum has no particles. 粒子靠近 = 压缩(compression)。粒子分开 = 稀疏(rarefaction)。 声音能在固体、液体、气体中传。真空里没有粒子,所以声音不能传。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam, say sound travels through solids, liquids, and gases. In real life, sound moves 4x faster in water and 15x faster in steel than in air. That is why putting your ear on a railway track lets you hear a train coming before you hear it through air. 考试时写:声能在固/液/气中传。真实速度差很多:水中快4倍, 钢中快15倍。所以耳朵贴铁轨能早听到火车。

A sound can be drawn as an amplitude-time graph. The taller the wave (bigger amplitude), the louder the sound. 声音可以画成 amplitude-time 图。波越高(amplitude 越大),声音越响。
