🌞 Warm-up · 热身

热咖啡变冷不是热能逃跑辞职, 是它分散到杯子、桌子和空气里了。

💬 Plain talk · 大白话

dissipated = 散到周围。wasted = 不是消失, 是我们不能方便地再用。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

耗散像一滴墨水进水杯: 墨水还在, 但散太开, 很难再收回来。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

能量不消失, 只散开: not gone, just spread。

Leave a hot drink on a table. After a while it cools down. Touch the cup and the table — they feel warmer. Where did the heat go? 把一杯热饮放在桌上。过一会儿它凉了。摸摸杯子和桌子——它们变暖了。 热跑到哪里去了？

Today's idea: the energy did not vanish. It spread out into the surroundings. This spreading is called dissipation. 今天的重点：能量没有消失。它散到周围去了。这种散开叫耗散 (dissipation)。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Dissipation
💬 Plain talk · 大白话

这屏只抓一个重点，先会认，再会写。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

耗散像一滴墨水进水杯: 墨水还在, 但散太开, 很难再收回来。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

能量不消失, 只散开: not gone, just spread。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

The law of conservation of energy: energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transferred or stored. 能量守恒定律：能量不能被创造也不能被消灭，只能被转移或储存。

✘ Wrong 错: "The energy disappears when the drink cools." "饮料变凉时能量消失了。"

✔ Right 对: "Energy is conserved — it cannot be created or destroyed. It is transferred to the surroundings (air, cup, table). This spreading out is dissipation." "能量守恒——不能被创造也不能被消灭。它转移到了周围 （空气、杯子、桌子）。这种散开就是耗散。"

When a device works, some energy does the useful job and some spreads to the surroundings (often as thermal energy). The spread energy is dissipated — too spread out to be useful again. 设备工作时，一部分能量做有用的事，一部分散到周围（常常以热能形式）。 散开的能量被耗散了——太分散，无法再被有效利用。

Example: a hot drink. Thermal energy moves to the air, the cup and the table. The total energy is still the same — it just spread out. 例子：一杯热饮。热能转移到空气、杯子和桌子。总能量还是一样的—— 只是散开了。

What you learned today 今天学到了什么

Dissipation = energy spreading out to the surroundings, usually as thermal energy. Energy is conserved; it is never destroyed. 耗散 = 能量散到周围，通常以热能形式。能量守恒；它从不被消灭。

Because some energy is always dissipated, no machine is 100% efficient. Reducing wasted energy is a global priority. 因为总有一部分能量被耗散，没有机器能 100% 高效。减少浪费的能量 是全球的重点。

"Who does this for a living?" "谁靠这个吃饭？"

Building energy auditors measure how much thermal energy a home dissipates, then advise how to waste less. 建筑能源审计师测量房屋耗散了多少热能，再建议如何减少浪费。
