Particle Vibration
DouDou · Particle Vibration
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon
🌞 Warm-up · 热身

粒子没有买机票飞去你耳朵。它只是原地抖, 把“抖”传给隔壁。

💬 Plain talk · 大白话

vibrate = 来回抖。in place = 原地。最重要: 粒子原地抖, 不旅行。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

粒子像排队传口令的人: 每个人只在原地动一下, 消息传过去, 人没有跑过去。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

声音三字诀: 振、传、不飞。

Tap the table with your finger. You feel it buzz. The table surface is vibrating — moving a tiny bit back and forth. It stays where it is; it does not fly off to your hand. 用手指敲桌子。你感到它在抖。桌面在振动 — 来回动一点点。 它留在原地; 它不会飞到你手上。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Particle Vibration
💬 Plain talk · 大白话

这屏只抓一个重点，先会认，再会写。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

粒子像排队传口令的人: 每个人只在原地动一下, 消息传过去, 人没有跑过去。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

声音三字诀: 振、传、不飞。

Particles vibrate — they move a small distance back and forth around one spot. The double-headed arrow ↔ shows this: out and back, out and back, staying in the same place. 粒子在振动 — 它们绕着一个点来回动一小段。双头箭头 ↔ 就是 这个意思: 出去再回来, 出去再回来, 停在同一个地方。

"Students think particles travel from the source across to the listener (the particle flies over)." "学生以为粒子从声源飞过去到听者那里。"

This is how sound works. A vibrating object makes the particles next to it vibrate. They pass it on, particle to particle, in place. That passed-on vibration is sound. 声音就是这样来的。一个振动的物体让旁边的粒子振动。它们一个 传一个, 都在原地。这传下去的振动就是声音。

Particles vibrate in place — back and forth around one spot (double-headed arrow ↔). They pass the vibration to the next particle. The vibration travels; the particle does NOT. This is how sound moves, and we build on it next lesson. 粒子在原地振动 — 绕着一个点来回(双头箭头 ↔)。它们把振动传给 隔壁粒子。振动在传; 粒子不动。声音就是这样移动的, 下一课接着学。
