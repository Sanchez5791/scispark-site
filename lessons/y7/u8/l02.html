🌞 Warm-up · 热身

只写 “energy changes” 就像跟 Grab 司机说“去那边”——听了, 但完全不知道去哪里。

💬 Plain talk · 大白话

每题先问: 原本能量在哪? 后来变成什么? 中间怎么转过去?

Energy changes from one form to another 能量从一种形式变成另一种

When you toast a slice of bread, energy is transferred from the chemical store (electricity → element) to thermal energy that browns the bread. Energy did not appear or vanish — it changed form. 你烤一片面包时,能量从化学储存(电 → 发热元件)转换成热能, 把面包烤黄。能量没有凭空出现,也没有消失 —— 它改变了形式。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Energy Changes (From X to Y)
能量转换 (从 X 到 Y)
💬 Plain talk · 大白话

这屏只抓一个重点，先会认，再会写。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

能量像包裹搬家: from 是原地址, to 是新地址, by 是运输方式。少写一个, 快递员找不到路。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

答能量变化只背一条: from 老家 → to 新家, by 走哪条路。

Say it the right way: "from X to Y" 用对句式:"从 X 到 Y"

The accepted way to describe an energy change is: "energy is transferred FROM [store X] TO [store Y] BY [pathway]". Always keep "from" and "to". A STORE is where energy sits; a PATHWAY is how it moves. 描述能量转换的标准说法是: "能量从[储存 X]转移到[储存 Y],通过[路径]"。 一定要保留"从"和"到"。储存 = 能量待的地方;路径 = 能量怎么走。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌
Common mistake — don't fall for this! 常见错误 — 别上当!

"Students treat energy stores and energy transfers (pathways) as the same thing." "学生把能量储存和能量转移(路径)当成一样。"

✘ Wrong 错: "energy is transferred from chemical to electrical" (electrical is a pathway, not a store).

✔ Right 对: "energy is transferred from the chemical store to the kinetic store BY an electrical pathway". "能量从化学储存转移到动能储存,通过电的路径"。

Around the World: James Joule 环球科学家:詹姆斯·焦耳

James Joule (UK, 1843) showed that one form of energy can be changed into another by the same fixed amount — the idea behind "from X to Y". 詹姆斯·焦耳(英国,1843)证明一种能量可以按固定的量变成 另一种 —— 这就是"从 X 到 Y"背后的想法。
