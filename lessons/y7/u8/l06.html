🌞 Warm-up · 热身

你在隧道喊名字, 不是墙在学你讲话。是你的声音被反射回来了。

💬 Plain talk · 大白话

硬、平、大的表面更容易有回声。软的表面会吸收更多声音。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

回声像球撞墙弹回, 但弹回的是声音的振动路径, 不是粒子本人。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

Echo = hit wall, come back。中文就记: 碰硬面, 反回来。

Shout your name once in an empty room or a tunnel. A moment later you hear your own voice again. That returning sound is an echo. 在空房间或隧道里喊一声你的名字。过一会儿你又听到自己的声音。 那个返回来的声音就是回声 (echo)。

Today's question: what makes the sound come back to you? 今天的问题：是什么让声音又回到你这里？

Learn · Concept · 概念
Echo
💬 Plain talk · 大白话

这屏只抓一个重点，先会认，再会写。

🔗 Analogy · 类比

回声像球撞墙弹回, 但弹回的是声音的振动路径, 不是粒子本人。

🧠 Memory jingle · 口诀

Echo = hit wall, come back。中文就记: 碰硬面, 反回来。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

An echo is a reflected sound. The vibration travels and reflects; the particles stay in place. 回声是被反射的声音。振动会传播和反射；粒子留在原地。

✘ Wrong 错: "Sound particles fly from the wall back to your ear." "声音的粒子从墙飞回你的耳朵。"

✔ Right 对: "Particles vibrate in place. The vibration is passed on and reflected; the particles themselves do not travel." "粒子在原地振动。被传递和反射的是振动，粒子本身不移动。"

A sound wave hits a hard surface (wall, cliff, tunnel) and is reflected. The reflected sound is called an echo. 声波碰到硬表面（墙、悬崖、隧道）后被反射。被反射的声音叫回声。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

The vibration path is reflected; the energy spreads out over the longer distance, so the echo sounds quieter. 振动的传播路径被反射；能量在更长的距离上散开，所以回声听起来更小声。

✘ Wrong 错: "An echo is sound particles bouncing off the wall." "回声是声音粒子从墙上弹回来。"

✔ Right 对: "An echo is the reflection of the vibration. The sound seems quieter because of the longer distance and dissipation, not because particles bounced." "回声是振动的反射。听起来变小是因为距离更长 + 能量散开， 不是因为粒子弹回来。"

Animals like bats send out sound and listen for the reflected echo to find objects. This is echolocation. 像蝙蝠这样的动物发出声音，再听被反射回来的回声来找到物体。 这叫回声定位 (echolocation)。

What you learned today 今天学到了什么

Echo = a sound wave reflected from a hard surface. The vibration travels and reflects; the particles vibrate in place. 回声 = 声波被硬表面反射。传播和反射的是振动；粒子在原地振动。

Later you will use echoes to calculate distance with a formula. 以后你会用回声配公式来计算距离。
