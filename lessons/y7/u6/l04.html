Food Webs: Chains Interconnected

食物网：多条食物链相连
One Chain is Never the Whole Story一条链子讲不完整个故事
🔗
If one road closes, traffic changes everywhere. If one species disappears, the web shifts too.
一条路封了，附近车流都会变。一个物种少了，食物网也会跟着变。
😄
A food chain is a straight road. A food web is the traffic map.
食物链像一条直路。食物网像整张交通图。
🧠
Web = Many chains sharing organisms.
记：网 = 很多条链，共用一些生物。

Learn · Define

Food Webs: Chains Interconnected

食物网：多条食物链相连
From Chain to Web从链到网
  • A food web = several food chains joined where they share an organism.
  • Every arrow still points from the food to the feeder, i.e.
  • in the direction energy flows.
  • 食物网 = 几条食物链在共享同一个生物的地方连接起来。
  • 每个箭头 仍然从“食物”指向“吃它的生物”，也就是能量流动的方向。
💬
The arrow does not mean “this animal runs toward dinner”. It means “energy goes this way”.
箭头不是“动物跑去吃饭”。箭头是“能量往这边传”。
🔗
Food web arrows are like money transfers: from the payer to the receiver. Energy transfers from food to feeder.
食物网箭头像转账：从付出的一方到收到的一方。能量从食物传给吃它的生物。

Which Way Does the Arrow Point?箭头到底指哪边？
  • In a food web the arrow points FROM the organism that is eaten TO the organism that eats it, because energy moves that way.
  • 食物网里，箭头从“被吃的生物”指向“吃它的生物”，因为能量是这样 传递的。
😄
The arrow is not a predator GPS. It is an energy receipt.
箭头不是捕食者导航。它是能量收据。
🧠
Food -> Feeder. Energy leaves the food.
食物 -> 吃它者。能量从食物出去。

⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
📝 考试 Exam
In the exam, a food chain may be drawn as one straight line.
考试时，食物链可以画成一条直线。
🌍 真实 Reality
In real life, many connected chains form a food web.
真实自然里，很多条连接的链组成食物网。

  • (b) less competition for grass

  • (b) mice have more food, so their number may rise
Q10 L3 Explain 3 marks
Explain why a food web gives a better picture of an ecosystem than a single food chain. [3]
✅ 答案 Answer
A food web shows many chains linked together; most organisms have more than one food source or predator; it shows how removing one species can affect many others, which a single chain cannot show.
食物网显示很多条食物链连接在一起；多数生物有不止一个食物来源或捕食者；它能显示移走一个物种会影响很多其他生物，而单条食物链看不出来。
可接受 Accept:
  • food web shows many food chains joined together
  • food web shows many connected feeding relationships
  • organisms may have more than one food source
  • organisms may have more than one predator
  • removing one species can affect several others
  • food web is more realistic than one straight chain
  • one chain is too simple / shows only one route
