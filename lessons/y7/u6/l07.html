Unit Review and Integration

单元复习与整合
Seven Lessons, One Story七节课，一个故事
  • Your kitchen and garden are a mini ecosystem.
  • A plant grows.
  • An insect eats it.
  • A bird eats the insect.
  • Everything dies one day, and a decomposer breaks it down.
  • Today we tie all seven lessons into one whole picture.
  • 你家的厨房和花园就是一个小型生态系统。
  • 植物长出来。
  • 昆虫吃它。
  • 鸟吃昆虫。
  • 一切总有一天会死，然后分解者把它分解。
  • 今天我们把七节课串成一整幅图。
🔗
The unit is one movie, not seven random TikToks: decomposers, chains, roles, webs, disappearance, complete web.
这个单元是一部电影，不是七个随机短片：分解者、食物链、角色、食物网、物种消失、完整网。
😄
Ecosystems are not single-player games. Everyone affects someone.
生态系统不是单机游戏。每个角色都会影响别人。

MISCONCEPTION RED CARD错误概念 · 红牌
  • RED CARD: "Arrows in a food chain point from the eater to the food." WRONG.
  • Arrows point from prey to predator, showing which way the energy flows.
  • 红牌: "食物链的箭头从吃的指向食物。
  • "错。
  • 箭头从被吃的指向吃的，表示能量流的方向。
Wrong: fox → rabbit (reversed)
错: 狐 → 兔 (反了)
Right: rabbit → fox (energy flows to the eater)
对: 兔 → 狐 (能量流向吃的一方)
📌 Reversed arrows are a common exam loss point.

Test Yourself

Q03 L2 State 2 marks
In a food chain "grass → grasshopper → frog", which way does the energy flow, and what does the arrow mean?
在食物链"草 → 蚱蜢 → 青蛙"里，能量往哪个方向流? 箭头是什么意思?
✅ 答案 Answer
Energy flows in the direction of the arrows, from grass to grasshopper to frog. The arrow means "is eaten by" / energy passes to the eater.
能量沿箭头方向流，从草到蚱蜢到青蛙。箭头表示"被吃"/能量传给吃的一方。
可接受 Accept:
  • energy flows grass to grasshopper to frog
  • arrow means is eaten by
  • energy passes to eater
Q04 L2 Name 2 marks
A food web has grass, rabbits, foxes and owls. If all the rabbits disappear, name ONE animal that would be affected and say why.
一张食物网有草、兔、狐、猫头鹰。如果所有兔子消失了，说出一种会受影响的动物，并说明原因。
✅ 答案 Answer
The fox (or owl) is affected because it loses a food source; it must eat other prey or its numbers fall.
狐(或猫头鹰)受影响，因为失去一个食物来源;它必须吃别的猎物，否则数量下降。
可接受 Accept:
  • fox/owl named
  • loses rabbit food source
  • less food so numbers may fall
Q05 L3 Describe 3 marks
Describe the full ecosystem story in your own words, using these three roles: producer, consumer, decomposer. Say what each one does and how the dead matter is recycled.
用你自己的话描述完整的生态系统故事，用上这三类角色: 生产者、消费者、分解者。说明每一类做什么，以及死物怎么被回收。
✅ 答案 Answer
Producers make food; consumers eat producers or other consumers; decomposers break down dead matter and waste, returning simple substances so producers can use them again.
生产者造食物;消费者吃生产者或其他消费者;分解者分解死物和废物，把简单物质送回去，让生产者再次使用。
可接受 Accept:
  • producer makes food
  • consumer eats organisms
  • decomposer breaks down dead matter/waste
Q06 L1 State 2 marks
State the three roles organisms can have in an ecosystem.
写出生物在生态系统里可以有的三种角色。
✅ 答案 Answer
Producer, consumer and decomposer.
生产者、消费者和分解者。
可接受 Accept:
  • producer
  • consumer
  • decomposer
Q07 L2 State 2 marks
A food chain is grass -> rabbit -> fox. State what the arrows show.
一条食物链是 草 -> 兔 -> 狐。写出箭头表示什么。
✅ 答案 Answer
The flow of energy, from the organism eaten to the organism that eats it.
能量的流动, 从被吃的生物指向吃它的生物。
可接受 Accept:
  • flow of energy
  • from eaten organism to eater
  • prey to predator
Q08 L2 Identify 2 marks
A web shows an arrow pointing fox -> rabbit. Identify what is wrong and give the correct direction.
一个食物网的箭头指向 狐 -> 兔。识别哪里错了, 并给出正确方向。
✅ 答案 Answer
The arrow is reversed; energy flows from prey to predator, so it should be rabbit -> fox.
箭头反了; 能量从猎物流向捕食者, 应为 兔 -> 狐。
可接受 Accept:
  • arrow is reversed
  • correct direction rabbit -> fox
  • energy prey to predator
Q09 L3 Explain 3 marks
Explain why a complete food web must include a decomposer.
解释为什么完整的食物网必须包含分解者。
✅ 答案 Answer
Because decomposers break down dead organisms and waste and recycle nutrients to the soil; without them dead matter builds up and nutrients do not return to producers.
因为分解者分解死掉的生物和废物, 把养分回收到土壤; 没有它们, 死物堆积, 养分无法回到生产者。
可接受 Accept:
  • break down dead organisms/waste
  • recycle nutrients to soil
  • nutrients return to producers
Q10 L2 State 2 marks
State one way energy is lost from a food web between one level and the next.
写出能量在一个层级到下一个层级之间, 从食物网流失的一种方式。
✅ 答案 Answer
Through respiration / movement / heat / waste (faeces and urine).
通过呼吸 / 运动 / 热量 / 废物(粪便和尿液)。
可接受 Accept:
  • respiration
  • movement
  • heat
