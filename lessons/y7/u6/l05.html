Hook · Observe

What If a Species Disappears?

如果一个物种消失了会怎样？
  • What if all the bees disappeared from the world?
  • Around 75% of crops depend on insect pollination.
  • That is not just honey gone — many fruits, vegetables, nuts and oils would be lost too.
  • 如果全世界的蜜蜂都消失了会怎样?大约 75% 的农作物靠昆虫授粉。
  • 那不只是没有蜂蜜 — 很多水果、蔬菜、坚果和油也会消失。
😄
If bees quit, breakfast also quits: fewer fruits, less jam, sad toast.
蜜蜂一罢工，早餐也跟着罢工：水果少了，果酱少了，吐司都忧郁。

Learn · Define

What If a Species Disappears?

如果一个物种消失了会怎样？
  • A food web is many food chains joined together.
  • Most animals eat more than one kind of food, and are eaten by more than one kind of animal.
  • 食物网是很多食物链连在一起。
  • 大多数动物吃不只一种食物,也被不只一种动物吃。
🧠
WEB = Who Eats Both ways of links.
记法: “网”就是“很多条链织在一起”。
🖼️

  • If one species is removed, the species that ate it lose a food source, and the species it ate may increase.
  • The effect spreads through the web.
  • This is the ripple effect.
  • 如果一个物种被拿走,吃它的生物会失去食物,被它吃的生物可能会变多。
  • 这个影响会在网里扩散开。
  • 这就是连锁效应(ripple effect)。
🔗
It is like dropping one stone in water: the ring spreads out, not just one dot moves.
像石头丢进水里：不是只有一个点动，是一圈一圈扩出去。

🚫 红牌 · Red Card — A Common Mistake
✘ 错 WRONG
✔ 对 RIGHT
🔗
Food webs do not gossip, but news travels fast inside them.
食物网不会八卦，但“消息”传得很快。
  • Some species matter more than their numbers suggest.
  • If they go, the whole web changes a lot.
  • These are called keystone species.
  • (We only name the idea here.)
  • 有些物种的重要性超过它的数量。
  • 如果它们消失,整个网会大变。
  • 这些叫关键物种(keystone species)。
  • (这里只认识这个概念。
  • )
🔗
A keystone species is like the middle block in Jenga: small block, huge trouble if removed.
关键物种像叠叠乐中间那块：看着小，抽掉可能整座倒。

⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
📝 考试 Exam
In exam tasks, a food chain is often one straight line.
考题常把食物链画成一条直线。
🌍 真实 Reality
Real nature is a food web with many linked routes.
真实自然是很多路线连成的食物网。

  • fewer otters eat sea urchins

  • kelp decreases / less kelp remains
