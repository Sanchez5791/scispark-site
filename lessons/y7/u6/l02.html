What Is a Food Chain? Arrow = Energy Flow

什么是食物链？箭头 = 能量流
  • Living things are linked by what they eat.
  • We draw this link as a FOOD CHAIN.
  • The arrow does NOT point to "who eats" in plain words — it shows the direction ENERGY FLOWS: from the eaten (prey) to the eater (predator).
  • 生物之间靠"谁吃谁"连起来。
  • 我们把这种连接画成"食物链"。
  • 箭头不是随便指 —— 它表示能量流动的方向： 从被吃的（猎物）指向吃的（捕食者）。
💬
The arrow is not a mouth arrow. It is an energy arrow.
箭头不是“嘴巴指向食物”，而是“能量往哪里走”。
😄
If the arrow goes the wrong way, the hawk looks like it is donating energy to dinner. Not today.
箭头画反，hawk 看起来像在把能量捐给晚餐。今天不准它乱捐。

Learn · Define

What Is a Food Chain? Arrow = Energy Flow

什么是食物链？箭头 = 能量流
  • A food chain shows the feeding order of organisms.
  • It starts with a PRODUCER (a green plant).
  • Each arrow points from the organism being eaten to the organism that eats it.
  • The arrow means: this is the direction energy flows.
  • 食物链显示生物的进食顺序。
  • 它从"生产者"（绿色植物）开始。
  • 每个箭头从被吃的生物指向吃它的生物。
  • 箭头的意思是： 这是能量流动的方向。
🧠
Read the arrow as "energy goes to".
把箭头读成：“能量流向”。
🧠
Eaten → Eater. Energy follows the arrow.
被吃 → 吃者；能量跟箭头走。

🚫 红牌 · Red Card — A Common Mistake
✘ 错 WRONG
The arrow points from the predator to its food (i.e. hawk -> toad), as if "this eats that".
箭头从捕食者指向它的食物（例如 hawk -> toad）， 好像在说"这个吃那个"。
✔ 对 RIGHT
The arrow points from prey to predator (toad -> hawk), showing the direction energy flows.
箭头从猎物指向捕食者（toad -> hawk）， 表示能量流动的方向。
🔗
Think of energy like a parcel. The prey is the sender; the predator is the receiver.
把能量想成包裹。猎物是寄件人，捕食者是收件人。

⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
📝 考试 Exam
In the exam, draw a food chain as one straight line, e.g. grass -> rabbit -> fox.
考试时画一条食物链，例如 grass -> rabbit -> fox。
🌍 真实 Reality
In real life it is a food web with many connections, so one organism can affect many others.
真实自然是食物网，有很多连接；少一个生物会影响很多其他生物。
💬
Exam chain = one clear route. Real nature = many routes tangled together.
考试食物链 = 一条清楚路线。真实自然 = 很多路线缠在一起。

[1] (c) Cheetahs are carnivores. What does this mean? [1] (d) Give an example of a predator and its prey from this food chain. [1] (e) What effect would a severe drought have on the population of antelope? [1]
羚羊吃非洲平原上的草。猎豹捕杀并吃羚羊。 (a) 用这些信息写一条食物链。[1] (b) 食物链里哪个生物是生产者？[1] (c) 猎豹是肉食动物。这是什么意思？[1] (d) 从这条食物链里举一个捕食者和它的猎物的例子。[1] (e) 严重的干旱会对羚羊的数量有什么影响？[1]
(a) grasses -> antelope -> cheetah (b) grasses (c) eats meat / eats animals (d) predator = cheetah; prey = antelope (e) antelope population decreases
(a) grasses -> antelope -> cheetah (b) grasses (c) 吃肉 / 吃动物 (d) 捕食者 = cheetah; 猎物 = antelope (e) antelope 数量减少
  • A food chain shows the feeding order of organisms.
  • It starts with a producer (a plant).
  • Every arrow runs from prey to predator and shows the direction energy flows.
  • Drawing the arrow the wrong way is the most common mistake.
  • 食物链显示生物的进食顺序。
  • 它从生产者（植物）开始。
  • 每个箭头从猎物指向捕食者，表示能量流动的方向。
  • 把箭头画反是最常见的错误。
