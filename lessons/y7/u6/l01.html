Decomposers: The Unsung Heroes

分解者：无名英雄
Where Do the Dead Leaves Go?落叶都去哪了?
  • Look at any garden or forest floor.
  • Leaves fall every year.
  • Trees die.
  • Animals die.
  • Yet nothing piles up forever.
  • Something invisible is clearing it away.
  • 看看任何花园或森林的地面。
  • 每年都有落叶。
  • 树会死。
  • 动物会死。
  • 但东西不会永远堆着。
  • 有看不见的东西在把它清走。
💬
Nature has no giant rubbish truck. The clean-up happens quietly, under your feet.
大自然没有垃圾车。清理工作就在你脚下悄悄发生。
😄
If dead leaves never disappeared, forests would need a "leaf traffic police".
如果落叶永远不消失，森林可能要请“落叶交警”来疏通了。

The Unsung Heroes无名英雄
  • The clean-up crew is made of living things.
  • We call them decomposers.
  • They break down dead matter.
  • Today we meet them.
  • 这支清理队伍是活的生物。
  • 我们叫它们分解者 (decomposer)。
  • 它们把死物分解掉。
  • 今天我们来认识它们。
🔗
Decomposers are like nature's recycling team: not glamorous, but the whole place collapses without them.
分解者像大自然的回收队：不抢镜，但少了它们整个系统会乱掉。
🧠

Learn · Define

Decomposers: The Unsung Heroes

分解者：无名英雄
What Is a Microorganism?什么是微生物?
  • A microorganism is a living thing too small to see with the eye alone.
  • 微生物是小到肉眼看不见的活生物。
Bacteria — single-celled, everywhere
细菌 — 单细胞, 到处都有
Fungi — moulds and yeasts
真菌 — 霉菌和酵母
Some fungi grow big enough to see (mushrooms)
有些真菌长得够大能看见 (蘑菇)
💬
Micro = tiny. Organism = living thing. So microorganism = tiny living thing.
micro 是很小，organism 是生物。所以 microorganism 就是“小到看不见的生物”。
📌 Bacteria and fungi are the main decomposers.

Why Dead Things Disappear死物为什么会消失
  • Decomposers feed on dead matter and break it down into simple substances.
  • 分解者以死物为食, 把死物分解成简单物质。
Dead leaf, dead animal, droppings = food for decomposers
落叶 / 死动物 / 排泄物 = 分解者的食物
They release simple substances back into the soil
它们把简单物质释放回土壤
Plants can use those substances again
植物又能再利用这些物质
dead matter → decomposer → simple substances → back to soil
💬
Do not go too deep here: the exam point is role, not chemical mechanism.
这里不要讲太深：本课重点是“角色”，不是化学分解机制。
🔗
Think of a dead leaf as a locked lunchbox. Decomposers open it and return the useful stuff to the soil.
把落叶想成一个锁住的饭盒。分解者把它“打开”，让有用物质回到土壤。
📌 Decomposers recycle. Nothing is wasted in nature.

Common Decomposers常见的分解者
  • The most common decomposers are bacteria and fungi (including moulds and mushrooms).
  • 最常见的分解者是细菌和真菌 (包括霉菌和蘑菇)。
Mushrooms on a rotting log
腐木上的蘑菇
Mould on old bread or fruit
旧面包或水果上的霉菌
Bacteria in soil and compost
土壤和堆肥里的细菌
🧠
Main pair to remember: bacteria + fungi. If you remember only one line, remember this pair.
记住主角二人组：细菌 + 真菌。只背一句，就背这对。
📌 You have seen decomposers many times without knowing.

🚫 红牌 · ⛔ Red Card — A Common Mistake
✘ 错 WRONG
"Decomposers are not important — they just rot things."
"分解者不重要 — 它们只是让东西烂掉。"
✔ 对 RIGHT
  • Without decomposers, dead matter would pile up and nutrients would never return to the soil.
  • Plants would run out of what they need.
  • 没有分解者, 死物会越堆越多, 养分永远回不到土壤。
  • 植物会用光它们需要的东西。
🔗
Rotting is not "waste". It is recycling.
腐烂不是“浪费”，它是在回收。
Safety First With Fungi and Microbes接触真菌和微生物的安全须知
  • Some wild mushrooms are poisonous, and decomposer bacteria live on dead matter — handle with care.
  • 有些野生蘑菇有毒, 分解者细菌生活在死物上 — 要小心处理。
Never touch or eat a wild mushroom you do not know
绝不触摸或食用你不认识的野生蘑菇
Wash hands after handling soil or dead matter
接触土壤或死物后要洗手
😄
Rule of thumb: if you do not know the mushroom, do not let it become your snack.
简单规则：不认识的蘑菇，不要让它变成你的点心。
📌 Decomposers are useful, but treat them with respect.

Q05 L3 EXPLAIN 3 marks
Explain why dead leaves do not pile up forever on a forest floor.
解释为什么森林地面上的落叶不会永远堆积。
✅ 答案 Answer
Because decomposers (bacteria and fungi) feed on the dead leaves and break them down into simple substances, so the dead matter is removed and the substances return to the soil.
因为分解者 (细菌和真菌) 以落叶为食, 把它们分解成简单物质, 所以死物被清除, 物质回到土壤。
可接受 Accept:
  • decomposers feed on or break down the dead leaves
  • bacteria and/or fungi are named as decomposers
  • leaves are broken into simple substances or nutrients
  • substances/nutrients return to soil
  • dead matter is removed because decomposers recycle it
Q06 L1 STATE 2 marks
State the two main groups of organisms that act as decomposers.
写出两类主要充当分解者的生物。
✅ 答案 Answer
Bacteria and fungi.
细菌和真菌。
可接受 Accept:
  • bacteria and fungi
  • fungi and bacteria
  • bacteria; fungi
  • bacteria + fungi
Q07 L2 DESCRIBE 2 marks
Describe what happens to the nutrients in a dead animal after decomposers act on it.
描述分解者作用后, 死掉动物体内的养分会发生什么。
✅ 答案 Answer
The decomposers break the body down into simple substances, and the nutrients return to the soil.
分解者把身体分解成简单物质, 养分回到土壤。
可接受 Accept:
  • nutrients return to the soil after decomposers break the body down
  • decomposers break the dead body into simple substances
  • nutrients are released back into the soil
  • simple substances go back into soil for plants to use
Q08 L2 STATE 2 marks
State one reason decomposers are sometimes called nature's recyclers.
写出分解者有时被称为大自然回收者的一个原因。
✅ 答案 Answer
They return nutrients from dead matter back to the soil so plants can reuse them.
它们把死物里的养分送回土壤, 让植物可以再利用。
可接受 Accept:
  • they return nutrients from dead matter to the soil
  • they recycle nutrients so plants can use them again
  • they break dead matter down and return useful substances
  • they make substances from dead matter available again
Q09 L3 EXPLAIN 3 marks
Explain why an ecosystem with no decomposers would soon run out of nutrients for its plants.
解释为什么没有分解者的生态系统很快会让植物缺乏养分。
✅ 答案 Answer
Dead matter and waste would pile up and the nutrients locked inside would not be released back to the soil, so plants could not take them in to grow.
死物和废物会堆积, 锁在里面的养分不会释放回土壤, 所以植物无法吸收来生长。
可接受 Accept:
  • dead matter and waste would pile up
  • nutrients would stay locked inside dead matter or waste
  • nutrients would not return to the soil
  • plants would not be able to take in enough nutrients
  • plants would not grow well because nutrients are not recycled
Q10 L2 IDENTIFY 2 marks
A compost heap contains dead leaves, fungi and bacteria. Identify the decomposers in the heap and state what they do.
一个堆肥堆里有落叶、真菌和细菌。识别堆里的分解者, 并写出它们做什么。
✅ 答案 Answer
Fungi and bacteria are the decomposers; they break down the dead leaves into simple substances.
真菌和细菌是分解者; 它们把落叶分解成简单物质。
可接受 Accept:
  • fungi and bacteria are the decomposers; they break down dead leaves
  • bacteria and fungi break down the dead leaves into simple substances
  • the decomposers are fungi and bacteria; they decompose the leaves
  • fungi and bacteria recycle the dead leaves into simpler substances
