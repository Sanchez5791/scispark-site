Why this matters today.今天为什么要学这个。
⏰ Warm-up recall · 热身回想
  • A geologist reads Earth's history by looking at rocks.
  • The kind of rock tells them how it formed and what happened to the land.
  • 地质学家靠看石头读地球的历史。岩石的种类能告诉他们石头是怎么形成的、 这块土地经历过什么。
Learn · 学
Rocks
岩石
  • There are three types of rock, grouped by how they form.
  • Igneous rock: forms when molten rock (lava or magma) cools and hardens.
  • Sedimentary rock: forms when layers of sediment are pressed together over a long time.
  • Metamorphic rock: forms when existing rock is changed by heat and pressure.
  • 岩石分三类，按形成方式分。
  • 火成岩：熔融的岩石（熔岩或岩浆）冷却变硬形成。
  • 沉积岩：一层层沉积物长时间被压在一起形成。
  • 变质岩：已有的岩石被高温和高压改变形成。
  • Rocks change from one type to another over time.
  • This is the rock cycle.
  • Heat and pressure, weathering, and melting move rock between the three types.
  • 岩石会随时间从一类变成另一类，这叫岩石循环。高温高压、风化、熔化 让岩石在三类之间转换。
  • Wrong idea: "All rocks can contain fossils." Correct: Igneous rock does NOT contain fossils.
  • The high temperature of lava/magma destroys any remains before they can become fossils.
  • Fossils are found in sedimentary rock.
  • 错误想法：「所有岩石里都能有化石。」 正确：火成岩里没有化石。熔岩/岩浆的高温会在遗体变成化石前把它毁掉。
  • 化石是在沉积岩里找到的。
