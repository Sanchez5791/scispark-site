Why this matters today.今天为什么要学这个。
⏰ Warm-up recall · 热身回想
  • A harbour worker reads a tide table every day.
  • If a big ship leaves at low tide it can run aground.
  • Knowing when the sea rises and falls keeps ships, fishers and swimmers safe.
  • 港口工人每天都看潮汐表。大船在低潮出港可能会搁浅。知道海水什么时候涨、 什么时候退，能保护船只、渔民和游泳的人。
  • Last lesson the Moon changed how it looked.
  • But the Moon does more than change shape — it reaches across space and pulls on Earth's oceans.
  • That pull is what makes tides.
  • 上一课月亮会变脸。但月亮不只会变脸——它隔着太空拉地球的海水。这股拉力， 就是潮汐的来源。
  • Twice a day in most places the sea rises high, then falls low.
  • Nothing adds or removes the water.
  • So what makes the same ocean rise and fall?
  • 大多数地方一天两次海水涨高、又退低。没有人加水或抽水。那是什么让同一片 海水一涨一退？
Learn · 学
Tides
潮汐
  • Gravity is the pull of attraction between any two objects.
  • The Moon's gravity pulls on Earth's oceans.
  • Water is free to move, so it bulges out toward the Moon — this raised water is a high tide.
  • 引力是任何两个物体之间的吸引力。月亮的引力拉地球的海水。水可以自由移动， 于是朝月亮那边鼓起来——这块鼓起的海水就是高潮。
  • There is a high-tide bulge on the side of Earth FACING the Moon, and a second bulge on the side FACING AWAY from the Moon.
  • As Earth rotates, a coast passes through two bulges each day — that is why most places get two high tides a day.
  • 面向月亮那一侧有一个高潮 bulge，背对月亮那一侧还有第二个 bulge。地球自转 时，海岸一天会经过两个 bulge——所以大多数地方一天有两次高潮。
  • The Sun also pulls on Earth's water (its pull is smaller than the Moon's because it is much farther away).
  • When the Sun, Earth and Moon line up in a straight line, the two pulls add together: the highest high tides — spring tides.
  • When the Sun and Moon are at right angles, the pulls partly cancel: the lowest high tides — neap tides.
  • 太阳也拉地球的海水（因为离得远很多，拉力比月亮小）。当太阳—地球—月亮排成 一条直线时，两股拉力相加：高潮最高——大潮。当太阳和月亮成直角时，拉力部分 抵消：高潮最低——小潮。
  • A tide gauge records the sea height over time.
  • The curve rises to high tide and falls to low tide, roughly twice a day, but the exact heights change from day to day.
  • 验潮仪记录海平面随时间的变化。曲线涨到高潮、退到低潮，大约一天两次，但每天 的精确高度会变。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
In the exam / 考试里：
  • In the exam, tides are often simplified as caused by the Moon.
  • 考试常简化写：潮汐由月亮造成。
Real science / 真实科学：
  • Both the Moon and the Sun pull Earth’s water.
  • The Moon is closer, so its pull is larger, but the Sun still matters.
  • 真实：月亮和太阳都拉地球海水。月亮更近，所以影响更大；但太阳也有影响。

Wrap · 总结
Tides
潮汐
✨ Summary · 总结
• Tides are caused by gravity: the Moon and the Sun pull on Earth's water.
• There are two bulges, so most coasts get two high tides a day.
• Aligned Sun and Moon give spring tides;
• at right angles, neap tides.
• 潮汐由引力造成：月亮和太阳拉地球的海水。有两个 bulge，所以大多数海岸一天 两次高潮。太阳月亮对齐是大潮；成直角是小潮。
🧠 Two pulls, two bulges, two high tides.
两股拉力，两个鼓包，两次高潮。
