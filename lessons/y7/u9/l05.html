Why this matters today.今天为什么要学这个。
⏰ Warm-up recall · 热身回想
  • A climate scientist measures the exact amounts of gases in the air to study global warming.
  • To do that, they first need to know what the air is really made of — and most people get it wrong.
  • 气候科学家测量空气里各种气体的精确比例来研究全球暖化。
  • 要做到这点，他们得先知道空气真正由什么组成——而大多数人都搞错了。
Learn · 学
Atmosphere Composition
大气成分
  • Dry air is made of these gases: Nitrogen (N2) = 78% Oxygen (O2) = 21% Argon (Ar) = 0.9% Carbon dioxide (CO2) = 0.04% Other gases (neon, helium, water vapour, methane ...) = trace amounts
  • 干燥空气由这些气体组成： 氮气 (N2) = 78% 氧气 (O2) = 21% 氩气 (Ar) = 0.9% 二氧化碳 (CO2) = 0.04% 其他气体（氖、氦、水蒸气、甲烷……）= 微量

✔ Right / 对：
  • Good: you picked the answer that fits this question, not just the general topic — (a) nitrogen (N2) | (b) 78%.
  • 对：你选的是这题真正要的答案，不只是写大主题——(a) 氮气 (氮气 N2) | (b) 78%.
