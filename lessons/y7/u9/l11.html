  • In Sweden the sky is dark for most of the day.
  • On the same date in Australia, children are at the beach in hot summer sun.
  • Near the equator in Singapore, it is warm all year and there are almost no seasons at all.
  • Same planet, same day — why is the season different in each place?
  • 现在是十二月。瑞典一天里大半时间天是黑的。同一天，澳洲的小孩在海 边晒大太阳过夏天。赤道上的新加坡几乎全年一样热，几乎没有四季。同 一个地球、同一天 —— 为什么每个地方季节不一样？
  • This lesson answers: what causes the seasons?
  • 这节课回答：四季是怎么来的？
Learn · 学
Seasons
季节
  • The Earth's axis is tilted at 23.5 degrees.
  • This tilt does not change as the Earth orbits the Sun.
  • The tilt is the reason we have seasons.
  • 地球的地轴倾斜 23.5 度。地球绕太阳公转时，这个倾斜方向不变。这个 倾斜就是四季的原因。
  • When a hemisphere is tilted toward the Sun, sunlight hits it more directly.
  • The same amount of light covers a smaller area, so it is warmer: this is summer.
  • When that hemisphere is tilted away, the light hits at a low angle and spreads over a larger area, so it is cooler: this is winter.
  • 当一个半球朝太阳倾斜，阳光更直射，同样的光照在更小的面积上，所以 更热 —— 这是夏天。当这个半球背离太阳，阳光斜射、铺在更大的面积上， 所以更冷 —— 这是冬天。
  • At any time, one hemisphere is tilted toward the Sun and the other is tilted away.
  • So when it is summer in the northern hemisphere, it is winter in the southern hemisphere.
  • Near the equator, the tilt effect is small, so there are almost no seasons.
  • 任何时候，一个半球朝太阳倾斜、另一个背离。所以北半球是夏天时，南 半球是冬天。赤道附近倾斜的影响很小，所以几乎没有四季。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
In the exam / 考试里：
  • In the exam, write seasons are caused by Earth’s 23.5° tilt, not distance from the Sun.
  • 考试时写：季节由地轴 23.5° 倾斜造成，不是离太阳远近。

  • Earth’s orbit is slightly elliptical, but that small distance change is not the cause of seasons.
  • 真实：地球轨道有一点椭圆，但这点距离变化不是季节的主因。

✨ Summary · 总结
• Seasons are caused by the Earth's 23.5 degree axial tilt — not by distance to the Sun.
• The hemisphere tilted toward the Sun gets more direct sunlight and has summer;
• the other has winter.
• The two hemispheres are always opposite.
• 四季是地球 23.5 度地轴倾斜造成的 —— 不是因为离太阳的距离。朝太阳 倾斜的半球阳光更直射，是夏天；另一个是冬天。两个半球永远相反。
🧠 Tilt, not distance. Direct light + longer days = warmer.
倾斜，不是远近；直射+白天长=更暖。
