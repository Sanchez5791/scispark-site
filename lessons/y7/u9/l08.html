⏰ Warm-up recall · 热身回想
  • For thousands of years, sailors and fishers read the Moon's shape to track the days at sea.
  • A navigator who could name the Moon's phase knew where they were in the month.
  • 几千年来，航海者和渔民靠月亮的形状来记海上的日子。会认月相的领航员， 就知道这个月走到第几天了。
  • Last lesson we toured the 8 planets of the Solar System.
  • Now we zoom in on Earth's closest neighbour — the Moon — and ask: why does its shape keep changing?
  • 上一课我们看完太阳系 8 颗行星。现在镜头拉近到地球最近的邻居——月亮—— 问一个问题：它的形状为什么一直在变？
  • Tonight the Moon might look like a thin curve.
  • In two weeks it could be a full circle.
  • The Moon is not changing shape — so what is?
  • 今晚月亮可能是一条细弯。两周后它可能是一个圆。月亮本身没有变形—— 那变的是什么？
Learn · 学
Moon Phases
月相
  • The Moon does not make its own light.
  • We see the Moon because it reflects light from the Sun.
  • Half of the Moon is always lit by the Sun.
  • A "phase" is how much of that lit half we can see from Earth.
  • 月亮自己不发光。我们看得见月亮，是因为它反射太阳的光。月亮永远有一半 被太阳照亮。所谓「月相」，就是从地球看过去，能看到那被照亮的一半的多少。
  • The Moon cycles through 8 phases as it orbits Earth (about 29.5 days): New Moon -> Waxing Crescent -> First Quarter -> Waxing Gibbous -> Full Moon -> Waning Gibbous -> Third Quarter -> Waning Crescent -> back to New Moon.
  • 月亮绕地球转一圈（约 29.5 天）会经过 8 个相：新月 -> 蛾眉月（渐盈）-> 上弦月 -> 盈凸月 -> 满月 -> 亏凸月 -> 下弦月 -> 残月（渐亏）-> 回到新月。
  • As the Moon orbits Earth, the angle between the Sun, Earth and Moon changes.
  • This changes how much of the lit half faces us.
  • The Moon's lit half never changes size — only our viewing angle changes.
  • 月亮绕地球转时，太阳—地球—月亮之间的角度在变。这改变了被照亮那半边 朝向我们的多少。月亮被照亮的一半大小从没变——变的只是我们看的角度。

一个学生说：「满月是因为地球的影子落在月亮上。」 (a) 说出这个学生是否正确。[1] (b) 解释我们看到满月的真正原因, 以及这个学生把它跟什么搞混了。[2]
✔ Answer · 答案
  • (a) no / incorrect | (b) a Full Moon happens because the Moon is on the opposite side of the Earth from the Sun, so we see the whole lit half;
  • the student has confused it with a lunar eclipse, which is when the Earth's shadow does fall on the Moon (a rare event).
  • (a) no / incorrect | (b) a Full 月亮 happens because the 月亮 is on the opposite side of the 地球 from the 太阳, so we see the whole lit half;

Wrap · 总结
Moon Phases
月相
✨ Summary · 总结
• The Moon reflects the Sun's light.
• It cycles through 8 phases (~29.5 days) as it orbits Earth.
• The phase we see depends on the angle between Sun, Earth and Moon — not on any shadow.
• 月亮反射太阳光。它绕地球转时经过 8 个月相（约 29.5 天）。我们看到的相， 取决于太阳—地球—月亮的角度——跟影子无关。
🧠 Waxing = light grows. Waning = light weakens.
Waxing=亮的部分变多；Waning=亮的部分变少。
