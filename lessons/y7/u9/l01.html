Earth Geography & Structure
地球地理与结构
  • Earth's surface is land and water.
  • There are continents (large land areas) and oceans (large bodies of salt water).
  • The equator is the imaginary line around the middle;
  • it splits Earth into the Northern Hemisphere and the Southern Hemisphere.
  • 地球表面是陆地和水。有大陆 (大片陆地) 和海洋 (大片咸水)。
  • 赤道是绕中间的假想线;
  • 它把地球分成北半球和南半球。
  • This is a real photograph of Earth taken by NASA.
  • From space, Earth looks blue because most of its surface is ocean water.
  • 这是 NASA 拍的真实地球照片。从太空看, 地球是蓝色的, 因为表面 大部分是海洋水。
  • Earth is made of four layers, from outside to centre: 1. Crust — thin, solid rock;
  • the surface we live on.
  • 2. Mantle — thick layer of hot rock;
  • contains some semi-molten rock called magma.
  • 3. Outer core — liquid metal (iron and nickel).
  • 4. Inner core — solid metal (iron and nickel);
  • the hottest part.
  • 地球由四层组成, 从外到中心: 1. 地壳 — 薄的固态岩石;
  • 我们住的表面。
  • 2. 地幔 — 厚的热岩石层;
  • 含一些半熔融岩石, 叫岩浆。
  • 3. 外核 — 液态金属 (铁和镍)。
  • 4. 内核 — 固态金属 (铁和镍);
  • 最热的部分。

⛔ Common mistake · 常见错误
✘ Wrong / 错：
  • "The inside of the Earth is all liquid molten lava."
  • "地球内部全是液态熔岩。"
✔ Right / 对：
  • Most of the inside is SOLID.
  • The mantle is mostly solid hot rock with only some semi-molten magma;
  • the inner core is solid metal.
  • Only the outer core is liquid.
  • Earth is not a ball of lava.
  • 内部大部分是固态。地幔大部分是固态热岩石, 只有部分半熔融 岩浆;
  • 内核是固态金属。只有外核是液态。地球不是一团熔岩。

Wrap · 总结
Earth Geography & Structure
地球地理与结构
✨ Summary · 总结
• Earth's surface is land and oceans, split by the equator into two hemispheres.
• Inside, Earth has four layers: crust (solid rock), mantle (hot rock + some magma), outer core (liquid metal), inner core (solid metal, hottest).
• Most of the inside is solid, not liquid lava.
• 地球表面是陆地和海洋, 被赤道分成两个半球。内部有四层: 地壳 (固态 岩石)、地幔 (热岩石 + 一些岩浆)、外核 (液态金属)、内核 (固态金属, 最热)。内部大部分是固态, 不是液态熔岩。
🧠 Crust-Mantle-Outer core-Inner core = C-M-O-I: “Cold Mango Outside, Iron inside.”
地壳-地幔-外核-内核：外面像冷芒果皮，里面是铁镍核心。
