Learn · 学
Day and Night
昼夜
  • The Earth turns (rotates / spins) on its own axis.
  • One full turn takes 24 hours.
  • 地球绕自己的轴自转（转动）。转一整圈要 24 小时。
  • The axis is an imaginary line through the North and South Poles.
  • 轴是一条穿过南北两极的假想线。
  • At any moment, the half of Earth facing the Sun has day;
  • the half facing away has night.
  • As the Earth spins, a place moves from the dark side into the lit side: this is sunrise;
  • moving from lit to dark is sunset.
  • 任何时刻，朝向太阳的那半边是白天；背向太阳的那半边是黑夜。
  • 地球自转时，一个地方从暗的一边转到亮的一边，就是日出；从亮转到 暗，就是日落。

⛔ Common mistake · 常见错误
✘ Wrong / 错：
  • The Sun moves around the Earth, and that is why we have day and night.
  • 太阳绕着地球转，所以才有昼夜。
✔ Right / 对：
  • The Earth spins on its own axis.
  • The Sun only appears to move across the sky because we are standing on a spinning Earth.
  • 是地球绕自己的轴自转。太阳看起来在天上移动，只是因为我们 站在自转的地球上。

Wrap · 总结
Day and Night
昼夜
✨ Summary · 总结
• Day and night happen because the Earth spins on its axis once every 24 hours — not because the Sun moves around us.
• The Sun is a light source;
• the Moon and planets are seen by reflected sunlight.
• 昼夜的成因是地球每 24 小时绕轴自转一次 — 不是太阳绕着我们转。
• 太阳是光源；月亮和行星是靠反射太阳光才被看见。
🧠 Spin = day/night. Orbit = year. Do not swap them.
自转=昼夜；公转=一年。别换错。
