✨ Summary · 总结
• In this unit we zoomed out from the surface of the Earth to the whole Solar System: the ground we stand on, the air above us, the spin that makes day and night, the Moon and its phases, the tides, the eclipses, and the seasons.
• One planet, one tilt, one orbit — explaining a whole year of changes.
• 这一单元，我们从地球表面一路拉远到整个太阳系：我们站的地面、头顶的 空气、产生昼夜的自转、月亮和它的月相、潮汐、日食月食、还有四季。一个 行星、一个倾斜、一条轨道 —— 解释了一整年的变化。
🧠 Earth line: land → plates → rocks → air → spin. Space line: planets → Moon → tides → eclipses → seasons.
地球线：陆地→板块→岩石→空气→自转。太空线：行星→月相→潮汐→食→季节。
