⏰ Warm-up recall · 热身回想
  • WARNING: NEVER look at a solar eclipse directly.
  • Only view with certified eclipse glasses (ISO 12312-2) or a pinhole projector.
  • Looking at the Sun damages your retina permanently.
  • (A lunar eclipse is safe to watch with your eyes.)
  • 警告：绝不可直视日食。只能用合格的日食眼镜（ISO 12312-2）或针孔投影看。
  • 直视太阳会永久损伤视网膜。（月食用肉眼看是安全的。）
  • Eclipse photographers travel across the world to capture a total solar eclipse — sometimes just a few minutes long.
  • They plan years ahead using the geometry of the Sun, Earth and Moon.
  • 日食摄影师为了拍一次日全食，会跨越大半个地球——有时只有几分钟。他们靠 太阳、地球、月亮的几何，提前好几年就规划好。
  • Last lesson the Moon's gravity reached Earth to pull the tides.
  • Now the Moon moves directly into the line between Earth and the Sun — and blocks the light.
  • That is an eclipse.
  • 上一课月亮的引力够到地球、拉起潮汐。现在月亮正好走进地球和太阳之间的连线， 把光挡住。这就是日食月食。
Learn · 学
Eclipses & Sun-Earth-Moon System
日食月食与日地月系统
  • Light travels in straight lines.
  • When something blocks the light, it casts a shadow.
  • An eclipse happens when the Sun, Earth and Moon line up so that one body's shadow falls on another.
  • 光沿直线传播。当有东西挡住光，就会投下阴影。当太阳、地球、月亮排成一线， 使一个天体的影子落在另一个上时，就发生了日食月食。
  • SOLAR eclipse: the Moon is between the Earth and the Sun.
  • The Moon blocks the Sun's light from reaching part of Earth.
  • It happens during the day.
  • 日食：月亮在地球和太阳之间。月亮挡住太阳的光，使它照不到地球的一部分。
  • 发生在白天。
  • LUNAR eclipse: the Earth is between the Sun and the Moon.
  • Earth blocks the Sun's light, so Earth's shadow falls on the Moon.
  • It happens at night and is safe to watch with your eyes.
  • 月食：地球在太阳和月亮之间。地球挡住太阳的光，于是地球的影子落在月亮上。
  • 发生在晚上，用肉眼看是安全的。
  • WARNING (again): During a SOLAR eclipse the Sun is still dangerously bright.
  • NEVER look directly at it — use certified eclipse glasses (ISO 12312-2) or a pinhole projector.
  • A LUNAR eclipse is safe to watch with your eyes.
  • 警告（再说一次）：日食时太阳仍然亮得危险。绝不可直视——用合格日食眼镜 （ISO 12312-2）或针孔投影。月食用肉眼看是安全的。

Wrap · 总结
Eclipses & Sun-Earth-Moon System
日食月食与日地月系统
✨ Summary · 总结
• An eclipse happens when the Sun, Earth and Moon line up and one shadow falls on another.
• Solar eclipse: Moon between Earth and Sun (daytime).
• Lunar eclipse: Earth between Sun and Moon (night).
• Not every full moon is an eclipse — the alignment must be almost exact.
• 当太阳、地球、月亮排成一线、一个影子落在另一个上时，就发生日食月食。
• 日食：月亮在地球和太阳之间（白天）。月食：地球在太阳和月亮之间（晚上）。
• 不是每个满月都有月食——排列必须几乎完全对齐。
🧠 Solar eclipse: Moon blocks Sun. Lunar eclipse: Earth blocks sunlight to Moon.
日食：月亮挡太阳；月食：地球挡到月亮的阳光。
