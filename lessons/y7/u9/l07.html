Learn · 学
Our Solar System
太阳系
  • Our Solar System has one star (the Sun) and 8 planets that orbit it.
  • In order from the Sun: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune.
  • 我们的太阳系有一颗恒星（太阳）和 8 颗绕它公转的行星。离太阳由近 到远：水星、金星、地球、火星、木星、土星、天王星、海王星。
  • Each planet is different in size and distance.
  • The 4 inner planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars) are small and rocky.
  • The 4 outer planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune) are large.
  • 每颗行星大小和距离都不同。靠内的 4 颗（水星、金星、地球、火星）小 而多岩石。靠外的 4 颗（木星、土星、天王星、海王星）很大。
  • The Sun is a source of light.
  • Planets are NOT light sources — we see them because they reflect sunlight.
  • 太阳是光源。行星不是光源 — 我们能看见它们，是因为它们反射太阳光。

⛔ Common mistake · 常见错误
✘ Wrong / 错：
  • Pluto is the 9th planet.
  • 冥王星是第 9 颗行星。
✔ Right / 对：
  • Since 2006 there are 8 planets.
  • Pluto was reclassified as a dwarf planet.
  • 2006 年起只有 8 颗行星。冥王星被重新分类为矮行星。
✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实
In the exam / 考试里：
Real science / 真实科学：
  • There IS gravity in space (at the ISS it is ~90% of surface gravity).
  • Astronauts float because they are in free fall — orbiting means falling sideways around Earth, not "no gravity".
  • 太空其实有引力（国际空间站处约为地表的 90%）。太空人 漂浮是因为持续自由下落 — 绕地球公转就是不停地往侧边下落，不是 "没有引力"。
