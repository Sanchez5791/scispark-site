Cells → Tissues细胞 → 组织

One muscle cell cannot lift your arm. Thousands of the same cell, working together, can. A group of the same type of cell working together is called a tissue.一个肌肉细胞抬不起你的手臂。成千上万个同一种细胞一起用力就可以。 一群同一种细胞一起工作,叫做组织 (tissue)。

Cells → Tissues细胞 → 组织

•
A tissue is a group of cells of the SAME type that work together to do one job.组织 = 一群同一种类的细胞一起工作,做同一件事。
•
Muscle tissue is made of many muscle cells. Epithelial tissue is a sheet of cells that lines or covers a surface.肌肉组织 = 很多肌肉细胞组成。上皮组织 = 一层细胞,铺在表面或里面当衬里。

Wrap · Reflect · 总结 · 回顾

Cells → Tissues细胞 → 组织

📌 Summary · 总结
•
Level 2 of the hierarchy: many cells of the same type, working together, make a tissue. Examples: muscle tissue, epithelial tissue.层级第二层:很多同一种细胞一起工作 = 组织。例子:肌肉组织、上皮组织。
•
Same-type cells made a tissue. But a real body part, like your stomach, has MORE than one tissue inside it. What do you get when different tissues team up? Next: Tissues -> Organs.同种细胞组成组织。但真实的器官,例如你的胃,里面不止一种组织。 不同组织合在一起会变成什么?下一课:组织 -> 器官。
