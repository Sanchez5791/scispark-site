Plant Cell Extra Parts植物细胞的额外结构

A plant scientist studying climate looks closely at one tiny green part inside a leaf cell: the chloroplast. Every chloroplast pulls carbon dioxide out of the air. Cut down forests and you lose billions of these tiny green factories — and carbon dioxide rises. The work of climate science starts inside a single plant cell.研究气候的植物科学家会盯着叶片细胞里一个很小的绿色部分: 叶绿体。 每个叶绿体都从空气里吸走二氧化碳。砍掉森林, 就失去几十亿个这样的 小绿工厂 — 二氧化碳就上升。气候科学的工作, 从一个植物细胞里开始。

Plant Cell Extra Parts植物细胞的额外结构

•
A plant cell has three parts an animal cell does not have: 1) cell wall — a tough outer layer that gives a rigid fixed shape; 2) chloroplast — green, where photosynthesis happens, holds chlorophyll; 3) large vacuole (sap vacuole) — a big fluid space that keeps the cell firm.植物细胞有三个动物细胞没有的部分: 1) 细胞壁 — 坚硬的外层, 给细胞固定的形状; 2) 叶绿体 — 绿色, 光合作用发生的地方, 含叶绿素; 3) 大液泡 (sap vacuole) — 一个大的液体空间, 让细胞保持坚挺。

🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
Chlorophyll and chloroplast are the same thing.叶绿素和叶绿体是同一个东西。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
The chloroplast is the green STRUCTURE (the part you can label). Chlorophyll is the green PIGMENT (a chemical) inside the chloroplast. A label question wants the structure: chloroplast.叶绿体是绿色的结构 (能标注的那个部分)。叶绿素是叶绿体里面 的绿色色素 (一种化学物质)。标注题要的是结构: 叶绿体。
🟫 Exam vs Reality🟫 考试 vs 真实
In the exam, draw the textbook cell with a nucleus. In real life, cells look very different depending on their job. Red blood cells have no nucleus at all. Muscle cells have many. The textbook picture is a general template, not a real specific cell.考试时画课本那张细胞图 (有核, 有各部分)。真实不同细胞长得完全 不一样: 红血球没核, 肌肉细胞有多个核。课本那张是拼出来的模板, 不是真实某个细胞。

Wrap · Reflect · 总结 · 回顾

Plant Cell Extra Parts植物细胞的额外结构

📌 Summary · 总结
•
A plant cell has three extra parts an animal cell does not: the cell wall (rigid shape), the chloroplast (photosynthesis, holds chlorophyll), and the large vacuole (keeps the cell firm). Chloroplast is the structure; chlorophyll is the green pigment inside it.植物细胞有三个动物细胞没有的部分: 细胞壁 (固定形状)、叶绿体 (光合作用, 含叶绿素)、大液泡 (让细胞坚挺)。叶绿体是结构; 叶绿素是它里面的绿色色素。 BRIDGE FORWARD: L06 → L07: a plant cell has 3 parts an animal cell does not — next lesson lines plant and animal cells up side by side.
