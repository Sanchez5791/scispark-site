Tissues → Organs组织 → 器官

•
An organ is a structure made of several DIFFERENT tissues working together to do a particular job.器官 = 由好几种不同组织一起工作的结构,做一件特定的工作。
•
The stomach, heart, kidney and liver are organs in animals. A leaf is an organ in a plant.胃、心脏、肾、肝是动物的器官。叶子是植物的器官。
