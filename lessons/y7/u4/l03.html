Looking at Animal Cells观察动物细胞

•
An animal cell has 5 main organelles: cell membrane (outer boundary), cytoplasm (jelly-like inside), nucleus (control centre), mitochondria (energy), and ribosomes (very tiny, make proteins).动物细胞有 5 个主要细胞器:细胞膜(外边界)、细胞质(果冻状的 内部)、细胞核(控制中心)、线粒体(能量)、核糖体(非常小,制造 蛋白质)。

An organelle is a small part inside a cell that has its own job (function).细胞器是细胞内部的一个有自己工作(功能)的小部分。
🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
Many students think every animal cell looks exactly like the textbook diagram.很多同学以为每个动物细胞都长得跟课本图一模一样。
Why it's wrong为什么错
The textbook diagram is a general template made to show all the parts. Real cells look very different depending on their job.课本图是为了展示所有部分而拼出来的通用模板。真实细胞会因为工作 不同而长得很不一样。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
Use the textbook diagram to learn the parts, but remember real cells vary in shape and even which parts they have.用课本图来学各个部分,但要记住真实细胞在形状上、甚至在拥有哪些 部分上,都各不相同。 EXAM_VS_REAL_SCIENCE_BOX (required — HIGH risk lesson): GAP_REF: Exam-vs-Real-Science reference #20 (细胞 = 课本万能图). 🟫 Exam vs Reality🟫 考试 vs 真实
In the exam, draw the textbook cell with a nucleus. In real life, cells look very different depending on their job. Red blood cells have no nucleus at all. Muscle cells have many. The textbook picture is a general template, not a real specific cell.考试时画课本那张细胞图(有核，有各部分)。真实里不同细胞 长得完全不一样:红血球没核，肌肉细胞有多个核。课本那张是拼出来的 模板，不是真实某个细胞。

  • 细胞核 — 不给分：细胞核 表示 控制；这 答案 需要 细胞 边界 功能
