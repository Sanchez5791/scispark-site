🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
Many students think mitochondria MAKE energy out of nothing.很多同学以为线粒体凭空"制造"能量。
Why it's wrong为什么错
Energy is not created. Mitochondria RELEASE energy that is already stored in food.能量不是被创造出来的。线粒体是把已经储存在食物里的能量释放出来。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
Say mitochondria "release energy from food", not "make energy".要说线粒体"从食物中释放能量",不要说"制造能量"。

🟫 Exam vs Reality🟫 考试 vs 真实
In the exam, draw the textbook cell with one nucleus and a few mitochondria. In real life, the number of mitochondria varies a lot — busy cells like muscle cells have many. The textbook picture is a general template, not a real specific cell.考试时画课本那张细胞图(一个核、几个线粒体)。真实里线粒体 的数量差别很大 — 像肌肉细胞这种忙碌的细胞有很多个。课本那张是通用 模板，不是真实某个细胞。
