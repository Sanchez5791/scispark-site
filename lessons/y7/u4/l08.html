Specialised Cells — Animal Examples特化细胞 — 动物例子

What is a specialised cell?什么是特化细胞?
•
A specialised cell has a SHAPE and PARTS that fit ONE job. Its structure is adapted to its function.特化细胞有适合"某一种工作"的形状和部件。它的结构对应它的功能。
Five animal specialised cells五种动物特化细胞
•
Red blood cell — carries oxygen; biconcave, no nucleus, has haemoglobin.红血球 — 运送氧气;双凹、无细胞核、含血红蛋白。
•
Sperm cell — fertilises egg; has a tail (flagellum), many mitochondria.精子 — 使卵子受精;有尾巴(鞭毛)、很多粒线体。
•
Nerve cell (neurone) — carries electrical signals; long, branched.神经细胞(神经元) — 传电信号;长、有分支。
•
Muscle cell — contracts to cause movement; many mitochondria.肌肉细胞 — 收缩产生运动;很多粒线体。
•
Ciliated cell — moves dust/mucus; has cilia (tiny hairs).纤毛细胞 — 移动灰尘/黏液;有纤毛(小毛)。
🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
❌ "All cells look the same."❌ "所有细胞长得一样。"
Why it's wrong为什么错
Specialised cells differ a lot: a nerve cell can be very long, a red blood cell is tiny and flat, a sperm cell has a tail.特化细胞差很多:神经细胞可以很长，红血球又小又扁，精子有尾巴。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
✓ Shape matches the job (adaptation). Different jobs → different shapes.✓ 形状对应工作(适应)。工作不同 → 形状不同。
🟫 Exam vs Reality🟫 考试 vs 真实
In the exam, draw the textbook cell with a nucleus. In real life, cells look very different depending on their job. Red blood cells have no nucleus at all. Muscle cells have many. The textbook picture is a general template, not a real specific cell.考试时画课本那张细胞图(有核，有各部分)。真实不同细胞长得完全不一样: 红血球没核，肌肉细胞有多个核。课本那张是拼出来的模板，不是真实某个细胞。
