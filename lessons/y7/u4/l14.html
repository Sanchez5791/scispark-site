Everything you learned this unit is happening inside you right now: the cells in your hand, the muscle tissue moving your fingers, the organs keeping you alive, all working together as one organism.这个单元学的一切此刻正在你体内发生： 你手上的细胞、让手指动的肌肉组织、维持你生命的器官， 全部一起工作，组成一个生物体。

Unit Review and Integration单元复习与综合

•
The whole unit in one picture: a cell is the basic unit of life. Animal cells have a nucleus, cell membrane, cytoplasm, mitochondria and ribosomes. Plant cells add a cell wall, chloroplast and a large vacuole. Cells become specialised for their job. Cells build up into tissue, organ, organ system and finally the whole organism.整个单元浓缩成一张图：细胞是生命的基本单位。 动物细胞有细胞核、细胞膜、细胞质、线粒体和核糖体。 植物细胞另外多了细胞壁、叶绿体和一个大液泡。 细胞会为它的工作而特化。 细胞一层层组成组织、器官、器官系统，最后是整个生物体。

🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
All cells look the same as the textbook cell diagram.所有细胞都长得和课本那张细胞图一样。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
Wrong. The textbook diagram is a general template. Real cells differ by job: a red blood cell has no nucleus, a muscle cell has many, a nerve cell is long, a sperm cell has a tail.错。课本那张图是通用模板。 真实细胞按工作不同而不同：红血球没有核，肌肉细胞有多个核， 神经细胞很长，精子有尾巴。
🟫 Exam vs Reality🟫 考试 vs 真实
In the exam, you draw the textbook cell with a nucleus and all the parts. In real life, cells look very different depending on their job. Red blood cells have no nucleus at all; muscle cells have many. The textbook picture is a general template, not any one real cell.考试时你画课本那张有核、有各部分的细胞图。 真实里，不同细胞按工作长得完全不一样： 红血球完全没有核，肌肉细胞有多个核。 课本那张是通用模板，不是任何一个真实细胞。
