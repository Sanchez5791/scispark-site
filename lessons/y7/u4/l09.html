Why a root drinks better than a straight tube为什么根能比直管子更会"喝水"
•
A plant scientist (botanist) studies why a root can pull up so much water. The answer is in the SHAPE of one tiny cell — the root hair cell, with its long finger-like extension. Shape fits the job.植物科学家(植物学家)研究根为什么能吸这么多水。答案在一个小细胞的 形状里 — 根毛细胞，有一根长长像手指的突起。形状对应工作。

Specialised Cells — Plant Examples特化细胞 — 植物例子

Plant cells can be specialised too植物细胞也可以特化
•
Root hair cell — absorbs water and minerals; long extension = large surface area.根毛细胞 — 吸水和矿物质;长突起 = 大表面积。
•
Palisade cell — photosynthesis; column shape, packed with chloroplasts.栅栏细胞 — 光合作用;柱形，塞满叶绿体。
•
Xylem vessel — carries water up the plant; long hollow tube.木质部导管 — 把水往上运;长的空心管。
🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
❌ "Chloroplast = chlorophyll."❌ "叶绿体 = 叶绿素。"
Why it's wrong为什么错
A chloroplast is the green oval organelle. Chlorophyll is the green pigment INSIDE the chloroplast. The chloroplast holds the chlorophyll.叶绿体是绿色椭圆器官。叶绿素是叶绿体里面的绿色色素。叶绿体装着叶绿素。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
✓ Chloroplast = the container (organelle). Chlorophyll = the green pigment it contains. Not the same thing.✓ 叶绿体 = 容器(器官)。叶绿素 = 里面的绿色色素。不是同一个。
🟫 Exam vs Reality🟫 考试 vs 真实
In the exam, draw the textbook cell. In real life, plant cells look very different by job — a root hair cell has a long extension, a palisade cell is a tall column. The textbook picture is a template, not one real cell.考试时画课本那张细胞图。真实植物细胞按工作长得很不同 — 根毛细胞有长 突起，栅栏细胞是高柱形。课本那张是模板，不是真实某个细胞。

Specialised Cells — Plant Examples特化细胞 — 植物例子

📌 Summary · 总结
•
Plant specialised cells fit their jobs by shape: root hair (absorb water/minerals, long extension = large surface area), palisade (photosynthesis, packed with chloroplasts), xylem (carry water up, long hollow tube). Structure → function.植物特化细胞用形状对应工作:根毛(吸水/矿物，长突起 = 大表面积)、 栅栏(光合，塞满叶绿体)、木质部(向上运水，长空心管)。结构 → 功能。
