Plant vs Animal Cells植物细胞 vs 动物细胞

A cell biologist is handed a microscope slide with no label. Plant or animal? They look for three clues: a tough outer wall, green chloroplasts, one big vacuole. If those are there, it is a plant cell. This sorting skill — telling two things apart by their parts — is how biologists classify all living material.细胞生物学家拿到一张没有标签的显微镜切片。植物还是动物? 他们找 三个线索: 坚硬的外壁、绿色叶绿体、一个大液泡。三个都在, 就是植物 细胞。这种分辨能力 — 靠部分把两个东西分开 — 就是生物学家给所有 生命物质分类的方法。

Plant vs Animal Cells植物细胞 vs 动物细胞

•
BOTH plant and animal cells have: nucleus, cytoplasm, cell membrane, mitochondria. ONLY the plant cell has: cell wall, chloroplast, large (sap) vacuole. So every difference is a part the animal cell is missing.植物和动物细胞都有: 细胞核、细胞质、细胞膜、线粒体。只有植物细胞 才有: 细胞壁、叶绿体、大液泡。所以每一个不同, 都是动物细胞少了的 那个部分。

🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
The cell membrane and the cell wall are the same thing, so animal cells have a wall too.细胞膜和细胞壁是同一个东西, 所以动物细胞也有壁。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
Every cell has a thin cell membrane that controls what enters and leaves. Only plant cells ALSO have a thick cell wall outside the membrane for rigid support. Membrane = both; wall = plant only.每个细胞都有一层薄的细胞膜, 控制进出。只有植物细胞在膜外面 还多一层厚的细胞壁来做坚硬支撑。膜 = 两者都有; 壁 = 只有植物。
🟫 Exam vs Reality🟫 考试 vs 真实
In the exam, draw the textbook cell with a nucleus. In real life, cells look very different depending on their job. Red blood cells have no nucleus at all. Muscle cells have many. The textbook picture is a general template, not a real specific cell.考试时画课本那张细胞图 (有核, 有各部分)。真实不同细胞长得完全 不一样: 红血球没核, 肌肉细胞有多个核。课本那张是拼出来的模板, 不是真实某个细胞。

Wrap · Reflect · 总结 · 回顾

Plant vs Animal Cells植物细胞 vs 动物细胞

📌 Summary · 总结
•
Plant and animal cells share four parts: nucleus, cytoplasm, cell membrane, mitochondria. The plant cell adds three: cell wall, chloroplast, large vacuole. To classify an unlabelled cell, look for those three plant-only parts.植物和动物细胞共有四个部分: 细胞核、细胞质、细胞膜、线粒体。植物 细胞多三个: 细胞壁、叶绿体、大液泡。要给没标签的细胞分类, 就找这 三个只有植物才有的部分。 BRIDGE FORWARD: L07 → L08: plant and animal cells differ — but inside one animal body, cells also differ by job.
