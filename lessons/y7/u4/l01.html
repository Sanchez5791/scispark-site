Your body is made of about 37 trillion cells. Right now, this very second, billions of cells are working inside you.你的身体由大约 37 万亿个细胞组成。就在现在这一秒,你体内有数十 亿个细胞在运作。
A cell is so small you cannot see one with your eyes alone. So what exactly is a cell, and why is it called the basic unit of life?细胞太小了,光用眼睛看不到。那么细胞到底是什么?为什么叫它 "生命的基本单位"?

What is a Cell?什么是细胞？

•
A cell is the smallest unit of a living thing that can carry out the functions of life. All living things are made of one or more cells.细胞是生物体能进行生命功能的最小单位。所有生物都由一个或多个 细胞组成。

Unicellular organisms are made of only one cell (e.g. amoeba, paramecium). Multicellular organisms are made of many cells (e.g. a human, a tree).单细胞生物只由一个细胞组成(例如变形虫、草履虫)。多细胞生物由 许多细胞组成(例如人、树)。

🟥 RED CARD · Common mistake🟥 红牌 · 常见错误
✘ The error✘ 错误想法
Many students think a cell and a particle are the same kind of tiny thing.很多同学以为细胞和粒子是同一种小东西。
Why it's wrong为什么错
A particle (atom/molecule) is far smaller than a cell. A single cell is itself built from huge numbers of particles.粒子(原子/分子)比细胞小得多。一个细胞本身就是由大量粒子 组成的。
✓ Correct idea✓ 正确想法
A cell is the smallest LIVING unit with a function. A particle is not alive. Cell is bigger and is made of many particles.细胞是有功能的最小"活"单位。粒子不是活的。细胞更大,而且由 许多粒子组成。

What is a Cell?什么是细胞？

📌 Summary · 总结
•
A cell is the smallest unit of life. Unicellular organisms have one cell; multicellular organisms have many. The cell is the anchor of all Biology.细胞是生命的最小单位。单细胞生物有一个细胞;多细胞生物有许多。 细胞是整个生物学的锚。
