Growth, Reproduction, Excretion, Nutrition　生长、繁殖、排泄、营养
  • Growth — increase in size or mass.
  • Reproduction — make more of the same type of organism.
  • Excretion — get rid of waste made by the body.
  • Nutrition — 摄取并利用营养 (food).
  • Scope notes (state, do not teach): detailed digestion / nutrition -> Y7 U6 + Y8 U6; fertilisation / chromosomes -> Y8 U4; respiration chemistry -> Y8 U5.
  • 生长 — 体积或质量变大。
  • 繁殖 — 造出更多同一种生物。
  • 排泄 — 去掉身体产生的废物。
  • 营养 — 摄取并使用养分 (食物)。
  • 范围提示 (说一句, 不教): 详细消化 / 营养 -> Y7 U6 + Y8 U6; 受精 / 染色体 -> Y8 U4; 呼吸化学 -> Y8 U5。
🟥 RED CARD — "Excretion is the same as getting rid of faeces"
  • WRONG.
  • Excretion means removing waste MADE BY the body's own reactions (such as carbon dioxide and urine).
  • Passing out undigested food (faeces) is egestion, not excretion.
  • They are different.
  • 错。排泄是去掉身体自己反应产生的废物 (比如二氧化碳和尿)。
  • 排出没消化的食物 (大便) 是排遗, 不是排泄。两者不一样。
⚖️ ✦ 考试 vs 真实 / Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, describe digestion as a tube with enzymes breaking food down.
  • In real life, you have about 100 trillion gut bacteria helping you — more than your own body cells.
  • They help digestion, make some vitamins, and even affect your mood.
  • 考试时写: 食物走一条管子, 酶分解它。真实里, 你肠里有大约 100 万亿个细菌帮你 — 比你自己的细胞还多。它们帮消化、造一些维生素, 还 影响你的心情。
🎯 考官要什么 · Command Words
  • State = give a short word/phrase · 短答, 直接给词
  • Describe = say what / how · 讲发生什么、怎么样
  • Explain = give a reason (fact + because) · 讲为什么 (事实+原因配对)
  • Define = exact syllabus wording · 照大纲原话, 准确术语
🔗 A living thing is like a tiny factory: materials in, waste out, building continues. 生物像小工厂：材料进来，废物出去，身体继续建。

Q01Explain / Describe5 marks
Class 7 investigate growth in plants.
(a) The class use secondary information sources. What is a secondary information source? [1]
(b) Class 7 investigate the growth of five different plant species.
In their first experiment the teacher: fills a plant pot with soil puts 5 seeds of a plant species into the soil waters the soil leaves the seeds for 10 days to grow into seedlings.
The teacher repeats the experiment four more times using different
plant species. Mia and Jamila each choose different ways to measure
the growth of the seedlings. Mia finds the mass of each plant pot and
soil before and after the 10 days. Jamila measures the height of the
seedlings after 10 days and calculates the average height.
(i) Explain why Mia's method of measuring the growth does not give accurate results. [1]
(ii) Explain why Jamila's results are reliable. [1]
(iii) Describe and explain one safety precaution the teacher takes in this investigation.
safety precaution ____________________
explanation ____________________
[2]
💡 Tip Growth means an increase in the plant's own size or mass — not the water in the soil.
生长指植物自己体积或质量变大 — 不是土里的水。 changes the mass even if the plant has not grown.
✅ 标准答案 · Answer
  • (a) information from people who did not do the experiment
  • 按英文答案意思作答：(a) information from people who did not do the experiment
可接受 · Accept
  • (a) information from people who did not do the experiment.
  • (b)(i) mass changes because of water in soil, not only plant growth.
  • (b)(ii) Jamila measures several seedlings and calculates an average.
  • (b)(iii) precaution = wash hands / wear gloves, explanation = protects from bacteria / harmful substances / allergies.
  • (a) 来自没有做实验的人的资料。
  • (b)(i) 质量变化可能来自土壤中的水，不只是植物生长。
  • (b)(ii) Jamila 测量多株幼苗并计算平均值。
  • (b)(iii) 安全措施 = 洗手/戴手套, 解释 = 防止细菌、有害物质或过敏伤害。

Q09Describe / Explain2 marks
A potted plant is kept on a sunny windowsill and watered each week.
Over two months the plant grows taller, opens new flowers, and the
soil at its base becomes drier.
(a) Describe two GREN characteristics the plant shows during the two months. [2]
(b) The student claims the plant shows excretion because the soil dries out. Explain why losing water to the soil is NOT a good example of excretion. [2]
✅ 标准答案 · Answer
  • (a) any two of: growth (gets taller)
  • (a) 以下任意两个: 生长 (gets taller)
可接受 · Accept
  • (a) any two of: growth (gets taller)
  • reproduction (opens flowers / makes seeds)
  • nutrition (takes in water and minerals)
  • (b) excretion is removing waste made by the body's chemical reactions.
  • the drier soil is mostly water taken up by roots or evaporated, not a chemical waste product, so it is not a clear example of excretion.
  • (a) 任意两个: 生长 (gets taller)
  • 繁殖 (opens flowers / makes seeds)
  • 营养 (takes in water and minerals)
  • (b) 排泄 is removing waste made by the body's 化学反应。
  • the drier soil is mostly 被根吸收或蒸发的水, 不是化学废物, so it is 不是清楚的排泄例子。
