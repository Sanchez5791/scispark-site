Dichotomous Keys — Build & Use

二歧检索表 — 看与写
⏰ 热身一句话 · Warm-up
  • A dichotomous key is a yes/no path.
  • Do not jump steps.
  • 二歧检索表是一条是/否路线，不准跳步。
  • A field biologist walks into a forest she has never visited.
  • She sees an insect she does not recognise.
  • She does not guess its name.
  • She opens a small chart of yes/no questions and follows it, one step at a time, until the chart gives her the exact name.
  • That chart is a dichotomous key.
  • Today you learn to read one and build one yourself.
  • 一位野外生物学家走进一片从没去过的森林。她看到一只不认识的昆虫。
  • 她不靠猜。她打开一张「是 / 不是」的小问题图，一步一步跟着走， 直到图给出准确的名字。这张图就叫二歧检索表 (dichotomous key)。
  • 今天你学会看它，也学会自己做一张。
🖼️

HOOK-1-IMG — biologist in forest holding a simple branching chart; source-thin-note for B3 static-image stage
  • Key word — "dichotomous" means "splitting into two".
  • At every step a key asks ONE question with only TWO answers: yes or no.
  • Each answer sends you to the next step, until you reach a name.
  • 关键词 ——「dichotomous」意思是「一分为二」。检索表每一步只问 一个问题，只有两个答案：是 / 不是。每个答案带你到下一步， 直到走到一个名字。
😄 A key is not a magic wand. It only works if you follow the arrows. 检索表不是魔法棒；你要跟着箭头走。

Dichotomous Keys — Build & Use

二歧检索表 — 看与写
🧠 口诀金句 · Memory Hook
Key rule: choose one branch, follow the arrow, repeat.
检索表口诀：选一边，跟箭头，再重复。
⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
📝 考试这样写 · In the exam
  • In the exam, follow one yes/no branch at a time.
  • 考试一次跟一个是/否分支走。
🔬 真实科学 · Real science
  • Real field keys can be longer and may need expert judgement.
  • 真实野外检索表更长，有时要专家判断。
  • Quick recall: the 7 life characteristics — Movement, Respiration, Sensitivity, Growth, Reproduction, Excretion, Nutrition (MRS GREN).
  • A key sorts LIVING things, so we are still inside the living world we have been studying all unit.
  • 快速回顾：生物 7 大特征 —— Movement 运动 / Respiration 呼吸 / Sensitivity 应激 / Growth 生长 / Reproduction 繁殖 / Excretion 排泄 / Nutrition 营养 (MRS GREN)。检索表用来分类 「活的」生物，所以我们还在整个单元的生物世界里。
🖼️

LEARN-1-IMG — MRS GREN 7-row big-text recall card
  • A dichotomous key is a set of paired statements.
  • You start at step 1, pick the statement that fits your organism, and follow the instruction — either go to another step, or get a name.
  • You never go backwards.
  • 二歧检索表是一组成对的句子。从第 1 步开始，挑出符合你这个生物 的那一句，照指示走 —— 要么去到另一步，要么得到一个名字。
  • 不会往回走。
  • Worked example (4 organisms).
  • Branching-tree key shown in standard format.
  • Follow it for one organism, step by step, to its name.
  • 示范例子 (4 种生物)。用标准格式的树形检索表。对一个生物， 一步一步走到它的名字。

配图待出 · Image pending
LEARN-3-IMG — standard-format branching-tree key, 4 organisms, worked path highlighted; B3 static-image stage
  • Worked example: build a simple key for 3-4 organisms.
  • Choose ONE feature that splits the group into two; write the yes/no pair; repeat for each smaller group until each organism is alone.
  • 示范例子：给 3-4 种生物自做一张简单检索表。先选「一个特征」 把这组分成两半；写出「是 / 不是」这一对；对每个更小的组重复， 直到每个生物单独剩下。
  • In the exam, every organism fits cleanly into one branch of the key — each question has a tidy yes/no answer.
  • In real life, living things vary: a feature can be in-between, or hard to see, so a key can be unsure or wrong, and scientists often need more than one key or extra tools.
  • 考试里，每个生物都干净地落进检索表的一条岔路 —— 每个问题都有 整齐的「是 / 不是」。真实里，生物有变异：一个特征可能是中间状态， 或很难看清，所以检索表可能拿不准甚至判错，科学家常常要用不止 一张检索表，或加别的工具。

Dichotomous Keys — Build & Use

二歧检索表 — 看与写
🔁 盖住回想 · Cover & Recall
What is the one thing to remember?
今天最该记住的一句?
  • Good keys use clear visible features, not guesses.
  • 好检索表用清楚可见的特征，不靠猜。
  • A dichotomous key uses paired yes/no questions about observable features to identify an organism.
  • You can now READ a key to find a name, and BUILD a simple key for 3-4 organisms by picking one splitting feature at a time.
  • 二歧检索表用成对的「是 / 不是」问题，针对看得见的特征来识别生物。
  • 你现在能「看」检索表找出名字，也能「做」一张简单检索表 (3-4 种生物)，每次选一个能一分为二的特征。
  • This is the unit's tool in action: the 7 life characteristics tell us a thing is living, species and the 7 classification levels tell us how living things are grouped, and a key is how we put that grouping to work to name a single organism.
  • 这是整个单元工具的实战：7 大生命特征告诉我们一样东西是活的， 物种和 7 层分类法告诉我们生物怎么分组，检索表就是把这套分组 拿来用、给单个生物命名的方法。
  • Next lesson pulls the whole unit together into one story — from the 7 life characteristics all the way to using a key.
  • 下一节课把整个单元串成一个故事 —— 从 7 大生命特征一路走到用检索表。
  • Stay curious: could you build a key for the five people in your family using only features you can see?
  • Where would your key get stuck — and why does that tell you something about real living things?
  • (Do not touch unfamiliar plants, animals or fungi — some are poisonous.
  • Use pictures or specimens the teacher prepares.)
  • 保持好奇：你能不能只用看得见的特征，给家里五个人做一张检索表？
  • 你的检索表会在哪里卡住 —— 这又告诉你真实生物的什么道理？
  • (不要触碰不认识的植物 / 动物 / 蘑菇 —— 有些有毒。用图片或老师 准备好的标本即可。)
