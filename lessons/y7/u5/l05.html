What is a Species?

什么是物种?
⏰ 热身一句话 · Warm-up
  • Species is not “same look”.
  • The exam test is breeding and 可育后代.
  • 物种不是“长得像”。考试看：能交配 + 后代可育。
  • A horse and a donkey can have a baby together.
  • That baby is a mule.
  • But a mule cannot have baby mules of its own.
  • Why?
  • That single fact is what makes a SPECIES a species.
  • Conservation biologists must define a species before they can protect it.
  • About 10,000 species are lost each year (estimate).
  • 马和驴可以一起生一个宝宝。这个宝宝叫骡。但是骡自己不能再生小骡。
  • 为什么?就这一件事,定义了什么叫一个物种。
  • 保育生物学家必须先定义清楚一个物种,才能去保护它。
  • 每年大约有一万个物种消失(估计值)。

What is a Species?

什么是物种?
🧠 口诀金句 · Memory Hook
Species test = Breed + Fertile Babies.
物种测试：能交配 + 后代能再生。
⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 · Exam vs Reality
📝 考试这样写 · In the exam
  • In the exam, same species 产生可育后代.
  • 考试写：同物种能产生可育后代。
🔬 真实科学 · Real science
  • Real species can be more complex, such as asexual organisms and hybrids.
  • 真实物种更复杂，例如无性繁殖生物和杂交。

  • 快速回顾:生命的七个特征——运动、呼吸、应激、生长、繁殖、排泄、营养。

L05_LEARN_IMG01
Species definition (sub-sub-unit 1)
  • A species is a group of organisms that 能交配 and 产生可育后代.
  • Keywords: 能交配 / interbreed; 产生可育后代
  • 一个物种,就是一群可以互相交配、并且生出有生育能力后代的生物。
Fertile-后代 test (sub-sub-unit 2)
  • The test has two parts.
  • (1) Can they 能交配?
  • (2) Are the 后代 themselves able to have young?
  • Both must be YES for one species.
  • 这个测试有两步。第一:它们能交配吗?第二:生出来的后代自己能再生 小宝宝吗?两个都"是",才算同一个物种。
🟥 Mule counter-example (sub-sub-unit 3)
  • RED CARD: "If two animals can have a baby, they are the same species." WRONG.
  • A horse and a donkey have a baby (a mule), but the mule is infertile — so horse and donkey are DIFFERENT species.
  • 红牌:"两个动物能生宝宝,就是同一个物种。"错。
  • 马和驴能生一个宝宝(骡),但骡不能再生育——所以马和驴是不同的物种。

Variation within one species (sub-sub-unit 4)
  • Members of the same species are NOT identical.
  • Differences within one species are called variation.
  • Different dog breeds are all one species (Canis familiaris).
  • 同一个物种里的成员并不是一模一样的。同物种内部的差异叫做变异。
  • 不同品种的狗都是同一个物种(Canis familiaris)。
Around the World scientist (Control v3.9 REQUIRED for L05)
  • Ernst Mayr (Germany / USA, 1942) proposed the biological species concept — the fertile-后代 definition you just learned.
  • 恩斯特·迈尔(德国/美国,1942 年)提出了生物学物种概念——就是你刚学 的"可育后代"定义。

L05_LEARN_IMG04
⚖️ EXAM vs REAL SCIENCE box (HIGH-RISK lesson REQUIRED)
  • EXAM VERSION: A species = organisms that breed and make 可育后代.
  • REAL SCIENCE: The fertile-后代 test only works for organisms that reproduce sexually and are alive today.
  • It cannot be used for organisms that reproduce asexually (e.g.
  • many bacteria), or for fossils.
  • Scientists use several species definitions.
  • 考试版:物种 = 能交配并生出可育后代的生物。
  • 真科学:"可育后代"测试只适用于有性繁殖、而且现在还活着的生物。它不能用在 无性繁殖的生物(例如很多细菌),也不能用在化石上。科学家其实用好几种 不同的物种定义。
🎯 考官要什么 · Command Words
  • State = give a short word/phrase · 短答, 直接给词
  • Describe = say what / how · 讲发生什么、怎么样
  • Explain = give a reason (fact + because) · 讲为什么 (事实+原因配对)
  • Define = exact syllabus wording · 照大纲原话, 准确术语
🔗 Same species is like a recipe that can keep making the next batch. 同物种像能一批接一批做下去的食谱。

What is a Species?

什么是物种?
🔁 盖住回想 · Cover & Recall
What is the one thing to remember?
今天最该记住的一句?
  • Looks can trick you.
  • Fertile 后代 is the safer test.
  • 外表会骗人；可育后代才稳。
Summary
  • A species = organisms that 能交配 and produce FERTILE 后代.
  • Mules and ligers are infertile hybrids, so their parents are different species.
  • Differences inside one species are called variation.
  • 物种=能互相交配并生出"可育"后代的生物。骡和狮虎兽是不育的杂交种, 所以它们的父母是不同物种。同物种内部的差异叫变异。
Cliffhanger (link to L06)
  • Species is the smallest unit of classification.
  • So how do scientists sort all living things above the species level?
  • Next lesson: the 7 levels of classification.
  • 物种是分类里最小的单位。那物种以上,科学家怎么把所有生物 往上分?下一课:分类的七个层级。

  • If a mule is infertile, how did the very first new species ever appear?
  • Scientists call this speciation — you will meet it in Year 9.
