7 Life Characteristics — MRS Part 1 (M/R/S)

七个生命特征 — MRS 第一部分
⏰ 热身一句话 · Warm-up
  • Living things are not guessed by “looks alive”.
  • We check signs.
  • 活不活不是靠感觉，是看生命迹象。
Is it alive?　它是活的吗?
  • A doctor in an emergency room must decide in seconds whether a patient is showing signs of life.
  • Across all of biology, every living thing shares the same seven signs.
  • Today we meet the first three: Movement, Respiration, Sensitivity.
  • career anchor — emergency-room doctor / paramedic checking signs of life.
  • 急诊室医生要在几秒内判断病人有没有生命迹象。整个生物学里, 所有活的东西都有同样的七个迹象。今天先认前三个: 移动、呼吸、感应。
  • 职业锚点 — 急诊医生 / 急救员检查生命迹象。

🧠 口诀金句 · Memory Hook
MRS GREN = Movement, Respiration, Sensitivity, Growth, Reproduction, Excretion, Nutrition.
MRS GREN：移动、呼吸、感应、生长、繁殖、排泄、营养。 SLIDE L-A — The MRS GREN tool

Movement, Respiration, Sensitivity　移动、呼吸、感应
  • Movement — 改变位置 (whole body or part of it).
  • Respiration — release energy from food (happens inside cells).
  • Sensitivity — detect changes in surroundings and respond.
  • Do NOT teach respiration chemistry here (glucose + oxygen equation) — scope note: that is Y8 U5.
  • 移动 — 改变位置 (整个身体或一部分)。
  • 呼吸 — 从食物里放出能量 (在细胞里发生)。
  • 感应 — 察觉周围的变化并作出反应。
  • 这里不教呼吸的化学方程 (葡萄糖+氧气) — 范围提示: 那是 Y8 U5 的内容。
🟥 RED CARD — "Plants don't move, so plants aren't alive"
  • WRONG.
  • Plants DO move — roots grow toward water, shoots bend toward light.
  • Movement can be slow or be part of the organism, not just an animal running.
  • Plants are living.
  • 错。植物会动 — 根朝水长, 茎朝光弯。移动可以很慢, 也可以 只是身体的一部分动, 不一定要像动物那样跑。植物是活的。
⚖️ ✦ 考试 vs 真实 / Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, respiration is written as one line: glucose + oxygen -> carbon dioxide + water (+ energy).
  • In real life, this one line hides many steps happening inside the cell, in tiny parts called mitochondria.
  • The single equation is only the overall summary.
  • 考试时, 呼吸写成一行: 葡萄糖 + 氧气 -> 二氧化碳 + 水 (+ 能量)。
  • 真实里, 这一行藏了细胞里很多步骤, 发生在叫线粒体的小部件里。
  • 一行公式只是总结。
🎯 考官要什么 · Command Words
  • State = give a short word/phrase · 短答, 直接给词
  • Describe = say what / how · 讲发生什么、怎么样
  • Explain = give a reason (fact + because) · 讲为什么 (事实+原因配对)
  • Define = exact syllabus wording · 照大纲原话, 准确术语
🔗 MRS GREN is like a doctor checklist: one tick helps, seven ticks are safer. MRS GREN 像医生检查表：一格有用，七格才稳。
