Every time you open a door, push a shopping trolley, or pull a drawer, you are using a force. A force is a push or a pull. 你每次开门、推购物车、拉抽屉，都在用力。力就是推或拉。

Look at any door you have pushed today. 看看你今天推过的任何一扇门。

Around the world 世界上的科学家

Isaac Newton (UK, 1687, Principia) described how forces make objects speed up, slow down, or change direction. 牛顿（英国，1687，《自然哲学的数学原理》）说明了 力如何让物体加速、减速或改变方向。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Push and Pull Forces
推力和拉力
A force is a push or a pull

A force is a push or a pull. Forces can start movement, stop movement, speed things up, slow things down, or change direction. 力是推或拉。力可以让物体开始动、停下、加速、减速，或改变方向。

Drawing forces as arrows

We draw a force as an arrow. The arrow points in the direction of the force. A longer arrow means a bigger force. 我们用箭头画力。箭头指向力的方向。箭头越长，力越大。

Named forces acting on a moving object

Several forces can act at once: a driving force pushes a vehicle forward, friction and air resistance slow it down, weight pulls it down, and a reaction force pushes up from the ground. 多个力可以同时作用：驱动力把车往前推，摩擦力和空气阻力让它减速， 重力把它往下拉，地面给出的反作用力把它往上推。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✘ Wrong 错: "A moving object only has a forward force on it." 「运动的物体只受到一个向前的力。」

✔ Right 对: Several forces usually act at the same time. A moving car still has friction, air resistance, weight, and a reaction force acting on it, not just the driving force. 通常有好几个力同时作用。行驶中的车除了驱动力， 还受到摩擦力、空气阻力、重力和反作用力。

A force is a push or a pull. We draw forces as arrows; a longer arrow means a bigger force. Several forces can act on one object at the same time. 力是推或拉。我们用箭头画力；箭头越长，力越大。 一个物体可以同时受到好几个力。
