Mass vs Weight
质量与重量
DouDou · Mass vs Weight
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon

Imagine you weigh yourself on Earth, then on the Moon. The number on the scale changes — but the amount of "stuff" in you does not. Why? Because mass and weight are not the same thing. 想象你在地球上量体重，然后到月球上再量。秤上的数字变了—— 但你身体里"东西"的多少没变。为什么？因为质量和重量不是同一件事。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Mass vs Weight
质量与重量
What gravity is

Gravity is a force that pulls objects towards each other. On a planet, gravity pulls objects towards the centre of the planet. A planet with more mass has stronger gravity. 重力是把物体互相吸引的力。在行星上，重力把物体往行星中心拉。 质量越大的行星，重力越强。

DUAL GRAVITY ARROWS

Gravity always works both ways. The Earth pulls an object down, and at the same time the object pulls the Earth up. They are a force pair: equal in size, opposite in direction. 重力总是双向的。地球把物体往下拉，同时物体也把地球往上拉。 它们是一对力：大小相等，方向相反。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✘ Wrong 错: "Only the Earth pulls the object; the object does not pull back." 「只有地球拉物体；物体不会反过来拉地球。」

✔ Right 对: Gravity is a force pair. The object pulls the Earth up with a force equal and opposite to the Earth pulling it down. 重力是一对力。物体也以大小相等、方向相反的力把地球往上拉。

MASS vs WEIGHT, units kg vs N

Mass is how much matter is in an object. It is measured in kilograms (kg) and never changes. Weight is how hard gravity pulls on the object. It is measured in newtons (N) and changes if gravity is different. Weight depends on mass and on the strength of gravity (g). (We learn the formula in a later year.) 质量是物体里有多少物质，单位是千克（kg），永远不变。 重量是重力把物体拉得多用力，单位是牛顿（N），重力不同时它会变。 重量取决于质量和重力强度（g）。（计算公式以后年级再学。）

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✘ Wrong 错: "Mass and weight are the same thing; weight is measured in kg." 「质量和重量是一样的；重量用千克量。」

✔ Right 对: They are different. Mass is in kg and never changes; weight is in N and changes with gravity. Writing "weight = 60 kg" is wrong. 它们不同。质量用千克且永不变；重量用牛顿且随重力改变。 写"重量＝60千克"是错的。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

In the exam: write 'mass = the amount of matter, in kg'. In real life: mass and weight are different. Mass is how much stuff is inside (never changes); weight is how hard gravity pulls (changes — on the Moon you weigh less, but your mass is the same). 考试 时: 写 'mass = matter 的 量, 单位 kg'。 真实 情况: mass 和 weight 不 一样。 mass 是 里面 东西 多少 (不 变); weight 是 地球 拉 它 的 力 (会 变 — 月球 上 比较 轻, 但 mass 一样)。

Summary

Gravity is a force that pulls objects together — and it works both ways (a force pair). Mass (kg) is how much matter you have and never changes. Weight (N) is how hard gravity pulls and changes if gravity is different. 重力是把物体互相吸引的力——而且是双向的（一对力）。 质量（千克）是你有多少物质，永不变。重量（牛顿）是重力拉得多用力， 重力不同它就会变。

💼 Who does this for a living? · 谁靠这个吃饭？

Aerospace engineers work out a spacecraft's weight on different planets, where the strength of gravity (g) is different. They must know that mass stays the same while weight changes. 航天工程师要算出航天器在不同行星上的重量，因为各处重力强度 （g）不同。他们必须知道质量不变、而重量会变。

🌍 Astronaut training happens worldwide — NASA (USA), Roscosmos (Russia), CNSA (China), and ESA (Europe). 航天员训练遍布全球——美国NASA、俄罗斯Roscosmos、 中国CNSA、欧洲ESA。
