Learn · Concept · 概念
Air Resistance
空气阻力
LEARN — Air is matter

Air is made of tiny particles. Air is matter, even though we cannot see it. (Recall Y7 Unit 1 particle model.) 空气由很小的粒子组成。空气也是物质，即使我们看不见它。（回顾 Y7 Unit 1 粒子模型。）

Links to 知识连结

Reference Y7 Unit 1 — air is particles. 参考 Y7 Unit 1——空气是粒子。

LEARN — Falling object: weight down + air resistance up + MISCONCEPTION RED CARD

A falling object has two forces: weight pulling it DOWN, and air resistance pushing it UP. Air resistance comes from air particles hitting the object. 下落的物体受两种力：重力把它往下拉，空气阻力把它往上推。空气阻力来自空气粒子撞击物体。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✘ Wrong 错: "Heavy things always fall faster than light things." “重的东西总是比轻的东西落得快。”

✔ Right 对: In air, shape and surface area change the air resistance, not just weight. (M3 misconception.) 在空气里，形状和表面积会改变空气阻力，不是只看重量。（M3 误解。）

LEARN — Shape / area affects air resistance

A larger surface area meets more air particles, so air resistance is larger. A feather has large area for its weight, so it falls slowly. A streamlined shape meets fewer particles, so less air resistance. 表面积越大，撞到的空气粒子越多，空气阻力越大。羽毛相对重量来说面积大，所以落得慢。流线型形状撞到的粒子少，空气阻力小。

Examples 例子

parachute (large area = high air resistance), kite, streamlined truck. 降落伞（面积大＝空气阻力大）、风筝、流线型卡车。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

📝 Exam 考试: "Ignoring air resistance, all objects fall at the same rate." “忽略空气阻力，所有物体以相同速率下落。”

🌍 Reality 真实: In real life, air resistance always exists. After falling a long time, an object reaches "terminal velocity" and stops speeding up. That's why skydivers don't keep accelerating forever. 真实里空气阻力一直在。掉久了速度会停（terminal velocity）。跳伞的人不会永远越掉越快。
