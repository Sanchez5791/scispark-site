LEARN-S1 — Concept chain recap

Six lessons, one chain: everything attracts everything -> bigger mass means bigger gravity -> air resistance exists -> a vacuum has no air resistance -> in a vacuum all objects fall together. 六节课,一条链:万物互相吸引 -> 质量越大引力越大 -> 空气阻力 存在 -> 真空里没有空气阻力 -> 真空里所有物体一起落下。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌
"Common mistake — don't fall for this!" "常见错误 — 别上当!"

✘ Wrong 错: "Heavier objects always fall first; mass equals weight; a vacuum still has thin air." "重的东西先落地;mass 等于 weight;真空里 还有一点点空气。"

✔ Right 对: In a vacuum all objects fall together (no air resistance). Mass (kg) never changes; weight (N) changes with location. A vacuum has no air particles. 真空里所有物体一起落下(没有空气阻力)。mass(kg)永远不变; weight(N)随地点改变。真空里没有空气粒子。
