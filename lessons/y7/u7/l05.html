Falling Objects
下落的物体
DouDou · Falling Objects
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon

A skydiver jumps from a plane. At first they speed up. Then they stop speeding up — even though they are still falling. Why? Today we find out why falling objects do not keep getting faster forever. 跳伞的人从飞机上跳下。一开始越掉越快。后来不再加速 —— 但人还在往下掉。为什么?今天我们就来看为什么掉下来的东西不会永远越掉越快。

Key words today: gravity (pulls down), air resistance (pushes up against motion), terminal velocity (the steady falling speed). 今天的关键词:gravity 重力(往下拉)、air resistance 空气阻力(往上顶、对抗运动)、terminal velocity 终端速度(稳定的下落速度)。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Falling Objects
下落的物体

When an object falls, gravity pulls it down. Air resistance pushes up against its motion. At the start, gravity is bigger, so the object speeds up. As it falls faster, air resistance grows. When air resistance grows equal to gravity, the forces are balanced — the object stops speeding up. This steady speed is called terminal velocity. 物体下落时,重力把它往下拉。空气阻力往上顶住它的运动。一开始重力比较大,所以物体加速。掉得越快,空气阻力越大。当空气阻力大到等于重力,两个力平衡 —— 物体不再加速。这个稳定的速度叫 terminal velocity 终端速度。

Watch how the skydiver's speed changes: first they speed up, then the increase slows, then they reach a steady speed (terminal velocity). The speed does not keep rising forever. 看跳伞者的速度怎么变:先加速,然后加速变慢,最后达到稳定速度(终端速度)。速度不会一直往上升。

RED CARD — Wrong idea: "Heavy things always fall faster than light things." Correct: in air, shape and air resistance matter, not just weight. In a vacuum (no air), all objects fall at the same rate. 红牌 —— 错误想法:"重的东西总是比轻的掉得快。"正确:在空气中,形状和空气阻力才是关键,不只是重量。在真空里(没有空气),所有东西掉得一样快。

What you learned today 今天学到了什么

Falling objects have two forces: gravity down, air resistance up. They speed up until the forces balance, then fall at a steady terminal velocity. In a vacuum there is no air resistance, so all objects fall at the same rate. 下落的物体有两个力:重力往下,空气阻力往上。它们加速直到两个力平衡,然后以稳定的终端速度下落。在真空里没有空气阻力,所以所有物体掉得一样快。

Can you (1) name the two forces on a falling object, (2) explain why speed stops increasing, (3) say what happens in a vacuum? 你能不能 (1) 说出下落物体上的两个力,(2) 解释为什么速度不再增加,(3) 说出真空里会怎样?
