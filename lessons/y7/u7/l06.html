Vacuum and Apollo 15
真空与阿波罗15号
DouDou · Vacuum and Apollo 15
Hook video · 60-90 sec · Coming soon

In 1971 on the Moon, astronaut David Scott dropped a hammer and a feather at the same time. On Earth the feather would float down slowly. On the Moon they hit the ground together. Why? Because the Moon has no air. Today we learn what a vacuum does to falling objects. 1971 年在月球上,宇航员 David Scott 同时放下一把锤子和一根羽毛。在地球上羽毛会慢慢飘下来。在月球上它们同时落地。为什么?因为月球上没有空气。今天我们学真空对下落物体有什么影响。

Key words today: vacuum (no air / no particles), air resistance (only happens where there is air). 今天的关键词:vacuum 真空(没有空气、没有粒子)、air resistance 空气阻力(只在有空气的地方才有)。

Learn · Concept · 概念
Vacuum and Apollo 15
真空与阿波罗15号

In air, a feather falls slowly because air resistance pushes up on it strongly (its shape catches lots of air particles). A hammer has little air resistance, so it falls fast. In a vacuum there are no air particles, so there is no air resistance at all. Only gravity acts, so both objects speed up the same way and land together. 在空气中,羽毛掉得慢,因为空气阻力强烈地往上顶它(它的形状会接触很多空气粒子)。锤子空气阻力很小,所以掉得快。在真空里没有空气粒子,所以完全没有空气阻力。只有重力在作用,所以两个物体以同样方式加速,一起落地。

RED CARD — Wrong idea: "Without air, things do not fall / there is no gravity in space." Correct: gravity still acts in a vacuum. Things still fall. What is missing is air resistance, not gravity. 红牌 —— 错误想法:"没有空气东西就不会掉 / 太空里没有重力。"正确:真空里重力仍然在作用。东西照样会掉。少掉的是空气阻力,不是重力。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✘ Wrong 错:

✔ Right 对:

Remember: gravity is a two-way pull. The Moon pulls the hammer down, and the hammer pulls the Moon up (equal but opposite). On the Moon, gravity is weaker than on Earth, but it is still there. 记住:重力是双向的拉力。月球把锤子往下拉,锤子也把月球往上拉(大小相等、方向相反)。月球上的重力比地球弱,但它仍然存在。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

📝 Exam 考试: In the exam, write "a vacuum has no particles". 考试时写:真空 = 没有粒子。

🌍 Reality 真实: In real life, even outer space has about 1 particle per cm cubed — not totally empty. A perfect vacuum cannot really be made. 真实连外太空每立方厘米也有约 1 个粒子。完美真空做不出来。

What you learned today 今天学到了什么

A vacuum has no air, so no air resistance. Gravity still acts. With no air resistance, all objects fall at the same rate and land together — just like the hammer and feather on the Moon. 真空里没有空气,所以没有空气阻力。重力仍然在作用。没有空气阻力时,所有物体掉得一样快、一起落地 —— 就像月球上的锤子和羽毛。
