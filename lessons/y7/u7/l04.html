Learn · Concept · 概念
Friction
摩擦力
LEARN — Friction opposes motion

Friction is a force between two surfaces that are touching. Friction always acts in the OPPOSITE direction to motion, so it slows moving objects down. 摩擦力是两个接触表面之间的力。摩擦力的方向总是和运动方向相反，所以它会让移动的物体慢下来。

LEARN — Microscopic rough surfaces + MISCONCEPTION RED CARD

Under a magnifier, even "smooth" surfaces are bumpy. The bumps of two surfaces catch on each other — that is friction. Rougher surfaces have bigger bumps, so more friction. Smoother surfaces, or oil, reduce friction. 在放大镜下，即使“光滑”的表面也是凹凸不平的。两个表面的凸起互相卡住——这就是摩擦力。表面越粗糙，凸起越大，摩擦力越大。更光滑的表面或加油，可以减少摩擦力。

🟥 Red Card · Common Mistake · 误区红牌

✘ Wrong 错: "Smooth surfaces have no friction at all." “光滑表面完全没有摩擦力。”

✔ Right 对: Smooth surfaces still have friction — just less. Only the amount changes, friction does not vanish. 光滑表面仍然有摩擦力——只是更小。改变的只是大小，摩擦力不会消失。

LEARN — Useful and unwanted friction

Friction is useful: tyre grip, brakes stopping a car, shoes not slipping. Friction is unwanted: it slows machines and makes heat. We reduce unwanted friction with oil or smoother surfaces. 摩擦力有用处：轮胎抓地、刹车让车停下、鞋子不打滑。摩擦力也有坏处：它让机器变慢、产生热。我们用油或更光滑的表面来减少不需要的摩擦力。

✦ Exam vs Reality · 考试 vs 真实

📝 Exam 考试: "Use one friction formula (F = uN)." “用一条摩擦力公式（F = uN）。”

🌍 Reality 真实: In real life there are TWO types: it is hardest to START pushing something (static friction), and easier once it is moving (kinetic friction). That is why heavy boxes are hard to start but easier to slide. 真实里摩擦力有 2 种：推不动时大（静摩擦），推动后小（动摩擦）。所以重箱子一开始难推，一旦推动就轻多了。

WRAP — Summary + Y8/Y9 cliffhanger + Stay Curious

Friction is a force between touching surfaces. It acts opposite to motion and slows things down. Rougher surfaces = more friction. Friction can be useful (grip, brakes) or unwanted (slows, heats); reduce it with oil or smoother surfaces. 摩擦力是接触表面之间的力。它的方向和运动相反，会让物体慢下来。表面越粗糙，摩擦力越大。摩擦力可以有用（抓地、刹车），也可以是不需要的（变慢、发热）；用油或更光滑的表面来减少它。
