Acids and Alkalis

酸和碱
Acid, alkali, neutral
  • An acid has a pH below 7.
  • An alkali has a pH above 7.
  • A neutral substance has a pH of 7 (for example, pure water).
  • 酸的 pH 小于 7。
  • 碱的 pH 大于 7。
  • 中性物质的 pH 等于 7(例如纯水)。
The pH scale
  • The pH scale is a number line.
  • Low numbers are acidic, high numbers are alkaline, and 7 is neutral.
  • We use it to compare how acidic or alkaline a substance is.
  • pH 标度是一条数轴。
  • 数字小是酸性,数字大是碱性,7 是中性。
  • 我们用它来比较一个物质有多酸或多碱。
🟥 红牌 · 常见错误 / Red Card
✘ WRONG 错
A strong smell means a substance is an acid.
气味很冲就表示是酸。
✔ RIGHT 对
Smell does not tell you pH. Use an indicator or a pH meter to find out if something is an acid or an alkali.
气味不能告诉你 pH。要用指示剂或 pH 计才能判断是酸还是碱。
⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 / Exam vs Reality
  • "In the exam, write the pH scale from 1 to 14.
  • In real life, very strong acids can have a pH below 0, and very strong alkalis above 14 — 14 is a simplification, not a physical limit."
  • "考试里,pH 标度写 1 到 14。
  • 真科学里,很强的酸 pH 可以低于 0,很强的碱 可以高于 14——14 只是简化,不是物理上限。
  • "
🔗 比喻 / Analogy
  • pH is like a danger/strength number line: lower means more acidic, higher means more alkaline.
  • pH 像一条强弱尺：数字越低越酸，数字越高越碱。
