Why mix metals instead of using a pure one? · 为什么要把金属混起来，而不用纯金属？
  • Pure iron is fairly soft and bends.
  • Mix a little carbon into it and you get steel — much harder.
  • The same trick is used to make stronger bicycle frames and stronger tools.
  • 👔 Metallurgists design alloys so a part is exactly as hard or as light as the job needs.
  • 👀 Look at any bicycle or coin near you — most are alloys, not pure metals.
  • 纯铁相当软，会弯。
  • 往里面混一点碳就得到钢——硬得多。
  • 同样的办法用来做更结实的自行车架和工具。
  • 👔 冶金工程师设计合金，让部件刚好达到工作所需的硬度或轻度。
  • 👀 看看身边任何自行车或硬币——大多是合金，不是纯金属。
😄 Pure metal layers slide like a neat stack of plates. Alloy particles mess up the stack — annoying, but useful. 纯金属层像整齐盘子，容易滑。合金粒子把队形打乱——烦，但有用。

What is an alloy? · 什么是合金？
  • An alloy is a mixture of a metal with one or more other elements.
  • Steel is iron mixed with carbon.
  • Brass is copper mixed with zinc.
  • 合金是金属与一种或多种其他元素的混合物。
  • 钢是铁混碳。
  • 黄铜是铜混锌。
Particle picture — pure metal vs alloy · 粒子图——纯金属 vs 合金
  • In a pure metal the particles are the same size and sit in regular layers that can slide over each other, so it is softer.
  • In an alloy, different-sized particles break up the regular layers, so the layers cannot slide easily — that makes it harder.
  • 纯金属里粒子大小相同，排成规则的层，层与层之间可以滑动，所以较软。
  • 合金里大小不同的粒子打乱了规则的层，层就不容易滑动——所以更硬。

🟥 红牌 · 常见错误 / Red Card
✘ WRONG 错
Mixing in carbon makes steel a brand new single substance, like a compound.
“混入碳后，钢就变成一种全新的单一物质，像化合物。”
✔ RIGHT 对
Steel is still a mixture. The carbon and iron particles are not chemically joined in a fixed ratio — they are mixed together. The mixing changes the properties, not the chemical identity.
钢仍然是混合物。碳和铁的粒子没有以固定比例化学结合——它们是混在一起的。混合改变的是性质，不是化学身份。
⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 / Exam vs Reality
  • In the exam, sort matter into element / compound / mixture.
  • In real life, alloys like steel and brass sit in between — they look like compounds but count as mixtures.
  • In the exam, draw solid particles touching in a regular pattern.
  • In real life, there are tiny gaps between them held by forces — that's why solids can be squeezed a tiny bit.
  • “考试时分：元素 / 化合物 / 混合物三类。
  • 真实：alloy（合金）像钢 / 黄铜算混合物，但性质像化合物。

🔗 比喻 / Analogy
  • An alloy is like adding odd-shaped books into a neat pile: the pile cannot slide smoothly anymore.
  • 合金像把不同大小的书塞进整齐书堆：书堆就不容易整层滑动。
