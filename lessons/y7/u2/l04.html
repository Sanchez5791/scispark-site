Indicators

指示剂
An indicator changes colour to show acid or alkali · 指示剂用变色来显示酸或碱
  • An indicator is a substance that changes colour depending on whether a solution is acidic, neutral, or alkaline.
  • We use the colour to tell what kind of solution we have.
  • 指示剂是一种物质, 它会根据溶液是酸性、中性、还是碱性而变色。
  • 我们用颜色来判断手上是哪一类溶液。
Litmus: red in acid, blue in alkali · 石蕊: 酸里红, 碱里蓝
  • Litmus is a common indicator made from a plant pigment.
  • It is red in acid, blue in alkali, and purple when neutral.
  • 石蕊是一种常见指示剂, 由植物色素制成。
  • 它在酸里是红色, 碱里是蓝色, 中性时是紫色。

Universal Indicator shows the whole pH range · 万能指示剂显示整个 pH 范围
  • Unlike litmus, which only says acid or alkali, Universal Indicator shows a range of colours across pH 0 to 14, so it can estimate how strong the acid or alkali is.
  • 石蕊只说酸或碱, 而万能指示剂在 pH 0 到 14 之间显示一系列颜色, 所以它能估计酸或碱有多强。

🟥 红牌 · 常见错误 / Red Card
✘ WRONG 错
An indicator tells you the exact pH number.
指示剂会告诉你精确的 pH 数字。
✔ RIGHT 对
An indicator shows a COLOUR, which you match to a chart to ESTIMATE the pH. Litmus only tells you acid or alkali, not a number. Only Universal Indicator gives an approximate value across the range.
指示剂显示的是一个颜色, 你把它对到色卡上去估计 pH。石蕊只告诉你酸还是碱, 不给数字。只有万能指示剂能给一个 跨范围的大概值。
🔗 比喻 / Analogy
  • An indicator is like a mood ring for solutions: colour is the message.
  • 指示剂像溶液的心情戒指：颜色就是信息。
