Classifying Substances (Acid / Alkali / Neutral)

Classifying Substances (Acid / Alkali / Neutral)
  • Three groups.
  • Every solution can be put into one of three groups: acidic (pH below 7) neutral (pH equal to 7) alkaline (pH above 7) We use indicators to find out which group a substance is in.
  • 三个组。
  • 每一种溶液都可以放进三个组里的一个: 酸性 (pH 小于 7) 中性 (pH 等于 7) 碱性 (pH 大于 7) 我们用指示剂来看一样东西属于哪一组。
  • Reading the indicators.
  • Litmus: red in acid, blue in alkali.
  • Universal Indicator: gives a colour for each pH value.
  • Methyl orange and thymolphthalein each change at set pH points.
  • Match the colour to a chart to classify the substance.
  • 读指示剂。
  • 石蕊: 遇酸变红, 遇碱变蓝。
  • 万能指示剂: 每个 pH 值对应一个颜色。
  • 甲基橙和百里酚酞各自在固定 pH 点变色。
  • 把颜色对到色卡上, 就能给这样东西分类。
🟥 红牌 · 常见错误 / Red Card
✘ WRONG 错
Corrosive means the substance is an acid.
腐蚀性 = 这东西是酸。
✔ RIGHT 对
Both strong acids AND strong alkalis can be corrosive. The corrosive hazard symbol does not tell you acid or alkali.
强酸和强碱都可能有腐蚀性。 腐蚀危险标志不能告诉你它是酸还是碱。
[SAFETY — Unit 2 required for L05]
  • Safety first.
  • WARNING: Always wear eye protection.
  • Never taste acids or alkalis.
  • 安全第一。
  • 警告: 一定要戴护目镜。
  • 绝不能尝酸或碱。
⚖️ 考试 vs 真实 / Exam vs Reality
  • Exam vs Real Science.
  • In the exam, write pH from 1 to 14.
  • In real life, very strong acids can have pH below 0, and very strong bases above 14.
  • The 1-14 scale is a simplified range for everyday substances.
  • 考试 vs 真实。
  • 考试时写: pH 是 1 到 14。
  • 真实浓强酸可到 0 以下, 浓强碱可到 14 以上。
  • 1-14 是简化范围。
🔗 比喻 / Analogy
  • Sorting acids, alkalis and neutral substances is like sorting laundry: each item must go in the right basket.
  • 分酸、碱、中性像分衣服篮：每样东西要进对的篮。
🎯 考官要什么 / What the examiner wants
  • State = short answer.
  • Describe = say what and how.
  • Explain = say why (fact + reason).
  • Define = exact syllabus wording.
  • State = 简短作答。
  • Describe = 说出是什么、怎样。
  • Explain = 说出为什么(事实+原因)。
  • Define = 用大纲原话精确定义。

  • What we sorted today.
  • Acids have pH below 7.
  • Alkalis have pH above 7.
  • Neutral is exactly 7.
  • Indicators give a colour we match to a chart to classify a substance.
  • Corrosive can mean a strong acid OR a strong alkali.
  • 🔗 Acid + alkali reaction equations come later (Y9 Unit 3).
  • Stay curious.
  • You can now tell an acid from an alkali.
  • But what happens if you mix them together?
  • 今天我们分了什么类。
  • 酸的 pH 小于 7。
  • 碱的 pH 大于 7。
  • 中性正好是 7。
  • 指示剂给出一个颜色, 对到色卡上就能给物质分类。

  • 🔗 酸碱反应方程式以后学 (Y9 Unit 3)。
  • 保持好奇。
  • 现在你能分辨酸和碱了。
  • 但如果把它们混在一起, 会发生什么?
