02 /What SciSpark isSciSpark 是什么

Not a typical AI tutor or course platform. 不是普通 AI 老师 · 也不是 课程平台。

SciSpark is an AI-managed science learning system. Try a real lesson first, sign up free if you like it, and unlock Unit 1 of every grade. Continuous progress tracking with AI feedback and weekly pattern review. SciSpark 是一套 AI 管理的科学学习系统。先试一节真课 · 喜欢就免费注册 · 解锁每个年级的第 1 单元。AI 持续跟踪进度 + 每周看数据改进。

01 / DEMO-FIRST ENTRY

Demo-first entry先试一节 · 再决定

02 / ACCOUNT-BASED CONTINUITY

Account-based continuity账户化连续性

A responsible-adult account and a child record are created first. After activation, the student receives independent sign-in access, and progress continues through linked accounts.先建立负责的成年人账户和孩子的档案。激活后 · 学生获得独立登录权限 · 进度通过关联账户持续。

03 / GUIDED LEARNING PATH

Guided learning path引导式学习路径

Support is structured around your child's level and next-step needs — not random content dumping. Level is set when you upgrade to a paid plan.支持围绕孩子的层级和下一步需要而构建 — 不是堆内容。升级付费时才定层级。

04 / BILINGUAL SYSTEM

Bilingual system双语系统

Every lesson and report exists in English and 中文 — written by humans, not machine-translated.每节课和每份报告都有中英两版 — 真人写 · 不是机器翻译。

03 /The problem问题

Most students don't struggle because science is hard. 大部分学生卡住 · 不是因为 科学难。

They struggle because they're memorising without understanding — and no one notices until the test result comes back. 他们卡住 · 是因为在死记硬背 — 没人发现 · 直到考试结果出来。

"He knows the topic but freezes the moment a question is phrased differently." “他 '懂' 这章内容 · 但题目换个说法他就僵住。”

Parent · Year 8家长 · 8 年级
