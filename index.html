Questions

What parents ask before they start.

Most answers are below. Everything else becomes clear after the assessment — that's the point of it.

Why can't parents just choose the plan themselves? +

Because nine times out of ten the wrong plan is chosen for the right reasons. Parents over- or under-estimate their child by one level — and the child quietly disengages. Our assessment removes the guesswork and our human reviewers check every result before recommending a plan.

Is this just an AI chatbot? +

No. SciSpark is an AI-managed learning system — not a chatbot. AI-guided questioning is one component within it, alongside an adaptive assessment, a human-reviewed level placement, structured Cambridge 0893 lesson units, and weekly parent reports. The system's role is to assess first, route correctly, and monitor progress with teacher oversight. The questioning tool supports that — it doesn't replace it.

My child's English isn't strong. Will this work? +

Yes. Our Bilingual and Bilingual Plus tracks teach the concept in Chinese first, then build the English vocabulary on top — so your child learns the science, not just the words. Most parents whose children sit between English and Chinese end up here.

How is this aligned with Cambridge 0893? +

Every lesson unit maps to a Cambridge 0893 learning objective and is reviewed against the latest Cambridge syllabus. Assessments use Cambridge-style mark schemes, so practice and exam look the same.

Can I cancel? +

Yes — monthly, no notice period. The assessment itself is free and requires no payment details. We'd rather you stay because it's working.
