Hook · Observe

What makes a bulb light up?

灯泡为什么会亮？

A hidden river of charge看不见的电荷之河

Flip a switch and the light comes on at once. Nothing seems to move — yet something invisible is racing around the wire. That something is electric current: a flow of tiny charges.

一按开关，灯马上就亮。看起来什么都没动——但有看不见的东西正在导线里奔跑。那就是电流：微小电荷的流动。

Learn · Define

The parts of a circuit

电路的组成

Four jobs in every simple circuit每个简单电路里的四个角色

A cell pushes the charge. Wires carry it. A component (like a bulb) uses the energy. A switch opens or closes the path. Break any part of the loop and the current stops.

电池推动电荷，导线运送电荷，元件（如灯泡）使用能量，开关让回路通或断。回路中任何一处断开，电流就会停止。

What you learned today

今天学到了什么

Three things to keep记住三件事

1) Current is a flow of charge, pushed by the cell. 2) Current only flows in a complete, unbroken loop. 3) A switch makes or breaks that loop.

1）电流是电荷的流动，由电池推动。2）只有在完整、不断开的回路里电流才会流动。3）开关让回路通或断。
